## The Translation Problem

ISPTEC teaches you petroleum engineering using a rigorous Brazilian-style academic curriculum. But the gap between university knowledge and industry work is real. No one tells you that:

- "Engenharia de Reservatórios II" is the foundation of the material balance calculations you will run in Eclipse
- "Escoamento de Petróleo" is exactly what a Prosper VLP calculation does
- "Fluidos de Perfuração" teaches the mud programme you will design on your first Angola well
- "Termodinâmica Aplicada" underpins every hydrate prediction your flow assurance team makes

This section exists to close that gap. For each major ISPTEC subject, you will find:
1. What it taught you
2. How industry uses exactly that knowledge — with real tools and workflows
3. What the subject does NOT teach that industry additionally requires
4. What to study next to close the gap

## The Subjects That Transfer Most Directly to Industry Work

**Tier 1 — Direct daily use in the job (within first year):**
- Engenharia de Reservatórios I → Every reservoir engineer uses this daily
- Elevação de Petróleo → Every production engineer uses this daily
- Engenharia de Poços → Every drilling engineer uses this daily
- Completação de Poços → Every completions engineer uses this daily
- Avaliação de Formações → Every petrophysicist uses this daily
- Fluidos de Perfuração → Every drilling fluids engineer uses this daily
- Engenharia de Segurança → Every offshore engineer uses PTW, risk matrix daily

**Tier 2 — Use within first 2–3 years:**
- Engenharia de Reservatórios II → Advanced analysis, PTA, complex MBE
- Simulação e Modelagem → Dynamic simulation (Eclipse/CMG)
- Escoamento de Petróleo → Flow assurance, tubing hydraulics, Prosper VLP
- Sistemas de Produção Superfície → FPSO process engineering
- Sistemas Marítimos de Produção → Subsea design and operations
- Engenharia de Gás Natural → Gas compression, treating, Angola LNG

**Tier 3 — Strategic depth (Year 5+):**
- Gerenciamento e Monitoração de Reservatórios → Field management strategy, 4D integration
- Geoestatística → Stochastic reservoir modelling in Petrel
- Engenharia Económica → FDP economics, PSA modelling, project decisions
- Modelagem de Bacias Sedimentares → Exploration-level basin evaluation

## The Subjects ISPTEC Does Not Teach (But Industry Needs)

These are the real gaps. Being aware of them gives you the opportunity to close them before your first interview.

| Missing Topic | Why Industry Needs It | How to Learn |
|--------------|----------------------|--------------|
| Production Sharing Agreement (PSA) economics | Angola operates exclusively under PSAs — you must understand cost oil and profit oil | SPE "Petroleum Fiscal Systems" course; read Angola PSA documents (some public via ANPG) |
| Integrated Production Modelling (IPM) | Connecting reservoir → well → flowline → FPSO in one model | Petroleum Experts free trial (GAP + Prosper) |
| Well Integrity Management | Barrier philosophy, NORSOK D-010, P&A | NORSOK D-010 (free download), see discipline/02_Well_Integrity.md |
| Field Development Planning process | How FDPs are structured and approved | See discipline/01_Field_Development_Planning.md |
| 4D seismic integration | How time-lapse seismic guides reservoir management | SPE papers on 4D seismic; TotalEnergies public presentations on Block 17 |
| Monte Carlo simulation | Probabilistic economic analysis, uncertainty quantification | @Risk tutorial, Python scipy.stats, see discipline/03_Petroleum_Economics.md |
| Python for data analysis | Automation, production surveillance, decline fitting | Python.org tutorial + realpython.com |
| Digital twin / PI Historian | Real-time data access in industry | AVEVA PI documentation (free), SPE Digital Energy workshops |
| ANPG regulations in depth | Angolan local content law, environmental regulations, PSA terms | ANPG website; read Decreto Presidencial 39/16 and 127/19 on local content |
| Offshore emergency procedures | HUET, lifeboat, muster drill, fire response | BOSIET certification (see support/Certifications_Angola.md) |
