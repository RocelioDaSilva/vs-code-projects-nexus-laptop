Decision:

1. Apply direct to Tier 2 from day one.

### IWCF Level 2 timing

Decision:

1. Complete IWCF Level 1 pre-hire.
2. Target IWCF Level 2 with sponsorship once employed.

### Rotation patterns vary (28/28 vs others)

Decision:
