## What Is Upstream?

Upstream is the exploration and production (E&P) sector — finding oil and gas underground and bringing it to surface. In Angola, this is the dominant sector, representing >90% of petroleum industry employment and investment.

**The upstream value chain:**
1. **Exploration** → Find the oil (seismic, geology, geochemistry)
2. **Appraisal** → Confirm the discovery (appraisal wells, testing)
3. **Development** → Build the infrastructure (wells, subsea, [FPSO](../../../docs/glossary.md))
4. **Production** → Operate and optimize (reservoir management, [artificial lift](../../../docs/skills/09-artificial-lift.md), flow assurance)
5. **Decommissioning** → End of field life (remove infrastructure, clean up)

## Career Paths in Upstream

### 1. Drilling Engineer
**What they do:** Plan and execute the drilling of wells — trajectory design, casing design, mud program, [well control](../../../docs/skills/01-well-control.md), rig-site supervision.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Engenharia de Poços (core)
- Resistência dos Materiais (casing design)
- Mecânica das Rochas (wellbore stability)
- Fluidos de Perfuração e Completação (mud engineering)
- Fenómenos de Transporte I (drilling hydraulics)

**Entry path:** Graduate trainee at [SLB](../../../data/company-directory/slb.md) (drilling services), [Halliburton](../../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md), [Baker Hughes](../../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md), or operator ([TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Eni) drilling department.

**Angola employers:** [TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), [Eni](../../../data/company-directory/eni.md), [Chevron](../../../data/company-directory/chevron.md), SLB, Halliburton, Baker Hughes, [Weatherford](../../../data/company-directory/weatherford.md)

**Salary range (Angola):** $50,000–80,000 (entry) → $120,000–200,000+ (senior, 10+ years)

---

### 2. Reservoir Engineer
**What they do:** Estimate reserves, build simulation models, forecast production, design water/gas injection schemes, optimize recovery.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Engenharia de Reservatórios I & II (core)
- Simulação e Modelagem de Reservatórios (core)
- Gerenciamento e Monitoração de Reservatórios (core)
- Geoestatística (geological modeling)
- Estatística Básica (uncertainty, Monte Carlo)
- Cálculo IV (diffusivity equation)

**Entry path:** Graduate trainee at operator ([TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), [Eni](../../../data/company-directory/eni.md), [Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md)) reservoir engineering department. Some service companies ([SLB](../../../data/company-directory/slb.md) — Reservoir Performance) also hire.

**Angola employers:** TotalEnergies, [Eni](../../../data/company-directory/eni.md), [Chevron](../../../data/company-directory/chevron.md), [Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) E&P, SLB

**Salary range (Angola):** $50,000–80,000 (entry) → $150,000–250,000+ (senior/principal, 15+ years)

---

### 3. Production Engineer
**What they do:** Optimize well and facility production — nodal analysis, [artificial lift](../../../docs/skills/09-artificial-lift.md) design, well testing, flow assurance, surface process optimization.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Escoamento de Petróleo (core)
- Elevação de Petróleo (core)
- Sistemas de Produção de Superfície (core)
- Engenharia de Reservatórios II (IPR, well testing)
- Termodinâmica Aplicada (heat transfer, [flow assurance](../../../docs/skills/10-flow-assurance.md))

**Entry path:** Graduate trainee at operator production department or service company ([SLB](../../../data/company-directory/slb.md) — Production Systems, [Baker Hughes](../../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md) — [Artificial Lift](../../../docs/skills/09-artificial-lift.md)).

**Angola employers:** TotalEnergies, Eni, [Chevron](../../../data/company-directory/chevron.md), [Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md), SLB, [Baker Hughes](../../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md)

**Salary range (Angola):** $50,000–75,000 (entry) → $130,000–200,000+ (senior)

---

### 4. Completions Engineer
**What they do:** Design and install well completions — sand control, intelligent completions, packers, tubing, safety valves, subsea trees.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Completação de Poços (core)
- Fluidos de Perfuração e Completação (completion fluids)
- Avaliação de Formações (net pay, reservoir quality)
- Ciências dos Materiais e Corrosão (material selection)

**Entry path:** Service company (SLB — Well Completions, Baker Hughes — Completions & Wellbore Construction, [Halliburton](../../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md) — Completion Tools).

**Angola employers:** SLB, Baker Hughes, [Halliburton](../../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md), [TechnipFMC](../../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md) (subsea trees)

**Salary range (Angola):** $50,000–75,000 (entry) → $120,000–180,000 (senior)

---

### 5. Petrophysicist
**What they do:** Interpret well logs to determine porosity, saturation, permeability, net pay. Build petrophysical models for reservoir characterization.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Avaliação de Formações (core)
- Geologia Geral (lithology)
- Física Geral II & III (electromagnetic and acoustic logging principles)
- Estatística Básica (data QC, distributions)

**Entry path:** Operator (TotalEnergies, Eni — geoscience department) or service company (SLB — Wireline/Data & Consulting, Halliburton — Wireline & Perforating).

**Angola employers:** TotalEnergies, Eni, SLB, Halliburton

**Salary range (Angola):** $50,000–75,000 (entry) → $140,000–220,000 (senior/principal)

---

### 6. Subsea Engineer
**What they do:** Design and manage subsea production systems — trees, manifolds, flowlines, risers, umbilicals, and control systems.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Sistemas Marítimos de Produção (core)
- Mecânica Geral (structural analysis)
- Resistência dos Materiais (pipe stress)
- Ciências dos Materiais e Corrosão (material selection)
- Escoamento de Petróleo (multiphase flow in flowlines)

**Entry path:** [EPC](../../../docs/glossary.md) contractor ([TechnipFMC](../../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md), [Subsea7](../../../data/company-directory/subsea7.md), Saipem) engineering trainee, or operator subsea department.

**Angola employers:** [TechnipFMC](../../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md), [Subsea7](../../../data/company-directory/subsea7.md), [Saipem](../../../data/company-directory/saipem.md), [SBM Offshore](../../../data/company-directory/sbmoffshore.md), Oceaneering, TotalEnergies, Eni

**Salary range (Angola):** $50,000–80,000 (entry) → $130,000–200,000+ (senior)

---

### 7. Geoscientist (Geologist / Geophysicist)
**What they do:** Interpret seismic data, build geological models, identify drilling targets, characterize reservoirs.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Geologia Geral (core)
- Introdução à Análise de Bacias (core)
- Geoestatística (core)
- Geoquímica Orgânica (source rocks)
- Optativa I — Métodos Sísmicos (seismic)
- Física Geral III (wave physics)

**Entry path:** Operator geoscience department (TotalEnergies, Eni, Sonangol) or seismic company (CGG, PGS).

**Angola employers:** TotalEnergies, Eni, Chevron, Sonangol, CGG, PGS

---

### 8. HSE Engineer / Safety Engineer
**What they do:** Manage safety systems, conduct risk assessments, investigate incidents, maintain regulatory compliance.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Engenharia de Segurança (core)
- Engenharia Ambiental (environmental)
- Meio Ambiente e Sustentabilidade (ESG)
- Filosofia e Sociedade (ethics)

**Entry path:** Operator HSE department or dedicated HSE consultancy (RSK, ERM).

**Angola employers:** All operators, all service companies (HSE roles exist everywhere)

## How to Stand Out for Upstream Roles in Angola

1. **SPE Membership:** Join the Society of Petroleum Engineers student chapter at ISPTEC. Attend SPE events, enter paper competitions.
2. **Software Skills:** Learn PETREL, Eclipse, PIPESIM, or OLGA — even basic familiarity sets you apart. Free academic licenses are available.
3. **Python:** Build a portfolio of petroleum engineering Python projects (DCA, material balance, production analysis).
4. **English:** Achieve at minimum B2/C1 level. Take IELTS or Cambridge if possible.
5. **Networking:** Attend ANPG conferences, [SLB](../../../data/company-directory/slb.md)/[TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) campus events, and industry webinars.
6. **Estágio:** Apply to 5+ companies. Start applications early (6 months before).
