## What You Learned

Well planning and design: trajectory design (vertical, directional, S-type, J-type, horizontal), casing design (burst/collapse/tension), cement design and placement, bit selection, rate of penetration (ROP), drilling parameters optimization, [well control](../../../docs/skills/01-well-control.md) fundamentals, and BHA (Bottom Hole Assembly) design.

## Where This Subject Is Used in Industry

- **Well Planning (Office):** Before spudding a well, the drilling engineer designs: trajectory (to reach the target), casing scheme (to maintain [well integrity](../../../docs/skills/15-well-integrity.md)), mud program (for each hole section), cement program, and [BHA](../../../docs/glossary.md) for each run. This planning process uses everything in this course.
- **Drilling Operations (Rig Site):** On the rig, the drilling engineer monitors: ROP, WOB, RPM, torque, pump pressure, mud properties, and survey data. Real-time decisions (change bit, adjust mud weight, set casing early) are informed by this course.
- **[Directional Drilling](../../../docs/skills/05-directional-drilling.md):** Angola's deepwater wells are often drilled from a single FPSO location to reach multiple reservoir targets â€” requiring complex 3D well trajectories. Directional drilling uses survey tools ([MWD](../../../docs/glossary.md)), trajectory calculations, and motor/RSS steering systems.
- **Well Cost Optimization:** A deepwater well costs $60â€“150M. Saving even 5% through better planning (fewer trips, faster drilling, fewer problems) saves millions.

## Angola-Specific Context

Angola's deepwater wells are among the most technically challenging in the world: 1,500m+ water depth, 4,000m+ TD, high-pressure salt formations, sub-salt targets, narrow pore pressure/fracture gradient windows. [TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Eni, and Chevron have dedicated drilling engineering teams in Luanda that plan and execute these wells. SLB, Halliburton, and Baker Hughes provide drilling services.
