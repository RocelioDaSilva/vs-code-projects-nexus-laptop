## Angola-Specific Context

Angola's deepwater reservoirs typically exhibit strong aquifer support (water drive) due to large connected aquifers in thin-bedded turbidite systems. Natural water drive provides pressure support but can also cause early water breakthrough if not managed properly. Understanding drive mechanisms is critical for planning water injection in Block 17 and Block 32 fields.
