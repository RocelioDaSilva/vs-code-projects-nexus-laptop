## Angola-Specific Context

In Angola's multinational offshore environment, an engineer who can present confidently in English will advance faster than one who cannot. Many Angolan graduates have strong technical knowledge but hesitate in English-language meetings. English III is the level where you transition from "understanding English" to "performing in English."
