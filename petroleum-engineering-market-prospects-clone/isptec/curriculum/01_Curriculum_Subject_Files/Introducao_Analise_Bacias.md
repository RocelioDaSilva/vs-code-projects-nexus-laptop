## What You Learned

Basin classification (rift, passive margin, foreland), tectono-sedimentary evolution, stratigraphic principles (sequence stratigraphy), depositional environments, and how sedimentary basins form, fill, and evolve over geological time.

## Where This Subject Is Used in Industry

- **Exploration Strategy:** Before deciding where to drill, geoscientists analyze the entire basin: rift history, thermal subsidence, sediment supply, and depositional architecture. This basin analysis determines which areas are most likely to contain petroleum accumulations.
- **Play Fairway Analysis:** Mapping the distribution of source rocks, reservoir rocks, seals, and traps across a basin is a direct application of basin analysis. Companies use this to bid on exploration acreage.
- **Sequence Stratigraphy:** Interpreting seismic sections using sequence stratigraphic principles (lowstand, transgressive, highstand systems tracts) is how explorationists predict reservoir connectivity and quality.
