## Angola In-Person Training

### PETROTEC Training Center — Luanda

The primary OPITO-accredited offshore training provider in Angola.

**Courses offered:**
- BOSIET/HUET (4 days) — ~$800–1,400
- FOET Refresher (1 day) — ~$400–600
- H2S Awareness (1 day) — ~$50–150
- IWCF Level 1 (2 days + exam) — ~$300–600
- IWCF Level 2 (5 days + exam) — ~$600–1,500
- Fire fighting & first aid — various
- Rigging & lifting — various

**Location:** Luanda (verify current address — schedules shift quarterly)
**How to contact:** Website or Angola OPITO representative

---

### ICAT (Instituto de Capacitação Técnica) — Luanda

**Courses:** Offshore safety, technical vocational training
**Note:** Verify current OPITO accreditation status before enrolling

### SOS International (SOS Angola) — Luanda

**Service:** Offshore Medical Certificate
**Standard:** International SOS partner, widely operator-approved
**Cost:** $150–350

---

### Hospital Sagrada Esperança — Luanda

**Service:** Offshore Medical Certificate
**Standard:** Approved international standards clinic

---

### ISPTEC (Instituto Superior Politécnico de Tecnologias e Ciências)

**What:** Angola's leading petroleum engineering university
**Degrees:** Petroleum Engineering (5 years), Chemical Engineering, Geosciences
**Where:** Luanda
**Website:** [isptec.co.ao](https://isptec.co.ao)
**SPE Student Chapter:** Active at ISPTEC — join for networking + OnePetro access

### Sonangol Training Programs

**What:** Periodic training support programs for Angolan petroleum engineering students
**Where:** Via Sonangol EP / Fundação Sonangol
**Website:** [sonangol.ao](https://www.sonangol.ao)
**Scholarship fund:** Bolsas for professional certification — check current availability

---

### ANPG Training & Local Content Programs

**What:** The regulator periodically funds training for Angolan nationals
**Website:** [anpg.ao](https://www.anpg.ao)

## Skill-Specific Learning Links

Quick-reference: for each skill file, here are the best places to learn it.

### Skill 01 — Well Control
- **Certification:** [IWCF Level 1 & 2](https://iwcf.org) (Luanda: PETROTEC)
- **Free:** [Drillingformulas.com — Well Control](https://drillingformulas.com) | [PetroWiki — Well Control](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Well_control)
- **Book:** *Applied Drilling Engineering* (Bourgoyne) — Chapters 4–5
- **Video:** YouTube "IWCF Well Control" prep videos
- **Practice:** IWCF study guide (free PDF at iwcf.org)

### Skill 02 — Drilling Fundamentals
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Drilling](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Drilling) | [Drillingformulas.com](https://drillingformulas.com)
- **Course:** [PetroSkills — Drilling Practices](https://www.petroskills.com) | Udemy drilling courses ($10–30)
- **Book:** *Applied Drilling Engineering* (Bourgoyne) | *Fundamentals of Drilling Engineering* (Mitchell & Miska)
- **Software:** WellPlan (employer-provided) | Excel drill-string design spreadsheets

### Skill 03 — Drilling Fluids
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Drilling Fluids](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Drilling_fluids) | [Drillingformulas.com — Mud Weight](https://drillingformulas.com)
- **Company training:** M-I SWACO (SLB), Baroid (Halliburton), Baker Hughes fluids division
- **Book:** *Drilling Fluids Processing Handbook* (ASME)
- **Lab:** ISPTEC Fluidos de Perfuração lab sessions

### Skill 04 — Formation Evaluation
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Formation Evaluation](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Formation_evaluation) | [learn.slb.com — Log Interpretation](https://learn.slb.com)
- **Course:** SLB log interpretation chartbooks (free download) | PetroSkills log analysis courses
- **Book:** *Well Logging for Earth Scientists* (Ellis) | *The Geological Interpretation of Well Logs* (Rider)
- **Software:** [Petrel RE](https://learn.slb.com) (free student) for log display and interpretation

### Skill 05 — Directional Drilling
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Directional Drilling](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Directional_drilling) | [Drillingformulas.com — Directional](https://drillingformulas.com)
- **Company training:** SLB PowerDrive, Halliburton GeoPilot, Baker Hughes AutoTrak (employer programs)
- **Book:** *Directional Drilling* (Inglis) | *Applied Drilling Engineering* (Bourgoyne)

### Skill 06 — MWD/LWD Tools
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — MWD](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Measurement_while_drilling_(MWD)) | [PetroWiki — LWD](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Logging_while_drilling_(LWD))
- **Company training:** SLB, Halliburton, Baker Hughes field engineer trainee programs (most comprehensive)
- **Course:** [learn.slb.com](https://learn.slb.com) — downhole tools modules

### Skill 07 — Mudlogging
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Mudlogging](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Mudlogging) | YouTube mudlogging tutorials
- **Company training:** Geoservices (SLB), Baker Hughes Datalog — entry-level mudlogger training
- **Book:** *Well Logging for Earth Scientists* (Ellis) — cuttings analysis chapters

### Skill 08 — FPSO Process Operations
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Surface Facilities](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Surface_facilities) | YouTube "FPSO process overview" videos
- **Course:** PetroSkills surface facilities courses | Udemy "Oil and Gas Processing" ($10–30)
- **Software:** [Aspen HYSYS](https://www.aspentech.com/en/university-program) (student license) for process simulation
- **Book:** *Petroleum Production Engineering* (Guo et al.)

### Skill 09 — Artificial Lift
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Artificial Lift](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Artificial_lift) | [learn.slb.com](https://learn.slb.com) — ESP modules
- **Software:** [PROSPER](https://www.petex.com/academic/) (student license) — nodal analysis, IPR/VLP curves
- **Course:** PetroSkills artificial lift | Udemy ESP design courses
- **Book:** *Production Optimization Using Nodal Analysis* (Beggs)

### Skill 10 — Flow Assurance
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Flow Assurance](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Flow_assurance) | SPE papers on hydrate management
- **Software:** OLGA (SLB) — professional; LedaFlow — professional (not student accessible)
- **Book:** *Flow Assurance for Production Operations* (Forrest)
- **Course:** PetroSkills flow assurance | NExT flow assurance short courses

### Skill 11 — Subsea Systems
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Subsea Systems](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Subsea_engineering) | YouTube "Subsea production system" animations
- **Company training:** TechnipFMC, Subsea 7, Saipem graduate programs (best in-depth training)
- **Book:** *Subsea Engineering Handbook* (Bai & Bai) | *Subsea Pipeline Engineering* (Palmer & King)
- **Organization:** [IMCA](https://www.imca-int.com) — subsea contractor standards

### Skill 12 — Offshore HSE & Safety
- **Certification:** [NEBOSH IGC](https://www.nebosh.org.uk) (online from Angola: Astutis, Red Oak, Zoe Talent) | [IOSH Managing Safely](https://iosh.com)
- **Free:** [OSHA Training](https://www.osha.gov/training) | [IOGP Life-Saving Rules](https://www.iogp.org/safety/) | [PetroWiki — HSE](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Health,_safety,_and_environment_(HSE))
- **Book:** *Lees' Loss Prevention* (Mannan) | *Introduction to Health and Safety at Work* (Hughes & Ferrett)
- **Angola:** BOSIET at PETROTEC Luanda | ANPG/MINAMB regulatory framework

### Skill 13 — Cementing & Completions
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Well Completions](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Well_completions) | [PetroWiki — Cementing](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Cementing)
- **Company training:** Halliburton, Baker Hughes, SLB completions/cementing trainee programs
- **Book:** *Well Completion Design* (Bellarby) | *Oilwell Cement and Cementing* (Nelson & Guillot)

### Skill 14 — Reservoir Engineering
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Reservoir Engineering](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Reservoir_engineering) | [learn.slb.com](https://learn.slb.com) — Petrel and Eclipse tutorials
- **Software:** [Petrel RE + Eclipse](https://learn.slb.com) (free student) | [MBAL](https://www.petex.com/academic/) (student license)
- **Book:** *Reservoir Engineering Handbook* (Ahmed) | *Fundamental of Reservoir Engineering* (Dake)
- **Course:** PetroSkills reservoir engineering | NExT reservoir simulation

### Skill 15 — Well Integrity
- **Free:** [PetroWiki — Well Integrity](https://petrowiki.spe.org/Well_integrity) | NORSOK D-010 standard (free download from standard.no)
- **Certification:** IWCF Level 2 covers well barrier philosophy
- **Book:** *Well Integrity for Workovers and Recompletions* (Skinner)
- **Standard:** [NORSOK D-010](https://www.standard.no) — the global well integrity standard (free)
