## Quick-Pick: Which Path Matches You?

### "I want the fastest entry into offshore work"
→ **[HSE/Safety Engineer](05-hse-safety-engineer.md)** — NEBOSH + BOSIET gets you offshore quickly. Always hiring.

### "I love math, simulation, and modeling"
→ **[Reservoir Engineer](03-reservoir-engineer.md)** — Highest intellectual demand, highest pay ceiling.

### "I want to be on the rig floor, hands-on with heavy equipment"
→ **[Drilling Engineer](01-drilling-engineer.md)** — High adrenaline, direct impact, excellent training programs at SLB/Halliburton.

### "I want stable, rotation-based work on an FPSO"
→ **[Production Engineer](02-production-engineer.md)** — FPSOs always need production engineers. Multiple Angola FPSOs = many openings.

### "I'm fascinated by deepwater technology and robotics"
→ **[Subsea Engineer](04-subsea-engineer.md)** — ROV, subsea trees, flowlines — cutting-edge deepwater hardware.

### "I like precision engineering and downhole tools"
→ **[Completions & Well Intervention](06-completions-engineer.md)** — Every completion is a $20M+ design challenge.
