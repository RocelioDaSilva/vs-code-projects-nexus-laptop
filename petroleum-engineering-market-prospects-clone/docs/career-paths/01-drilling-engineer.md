# Career Path: Drilling Engineer

> A step-by-step guide from graduation to senior drilling engineer in Angola's deepwater sector.
> Estimated timeline: 5–7 years from entry-level to senior/wells manager roles.

---

## Why Drilling Engineering?

Angola is a **deepwater drilling hub**. Blocks 0, 15, 17, 31, and 32 involve water depths from 400 m to 2,500+ m, requiring advanced drillship operations, managed pressure drilling (MPD), and complex well architectures. Drilling engineers are always needed — wells must be drilled before anything else can happen.

**Salary progression (Angola):**

| Stage | Role | Monthly (USD) | Annual (USD) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Year 0–1 | Junior Wellsite Engineer / Trainee | $1,200–$2,500 | $14,400–$30,000 |
| Year 1–3 | Field Engineer (OFS) or [MWD](../glossary.md)/LWD Operator | $2,500–$5,000 | $30,000–$60,000 |
| Year 3–5 | Drilling Engineer (Tier 2) | $5,000–$8,000 | $60,000–$96,000 |
| Year 5–7 | Senior Drilling Engineer (Tier 1/2) | $8,000–$12,000 | $96,000–$144,000 |
| Year 7–10+ | Wells Manager / Drilling Superintendent | $12,000–$18,000+ | $144,000–$216,000+ |

## Phase 1: Foundation (Year 0–1)

### Goal
Get your first offshore exposure, learn rig-floor operations, and earn mandatory certifications.

### Certifications to Complete
1. **[BOSIET](../glossary.md)/HUET** — Mandatory before any offshore trip (~$500–$1,500)
2. **Offshore Medical Certificate** — Valid 2 years (~$300–$800)
3. **[IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 1 (Surface)** — [Well control](../skills/01-well-control.md) awareness (~$1,000–$2,000)
4. **[H2S](../glossary.md) Awareness** — Required on many blocks (~$500)

### Target Employers (Tier 3 Entry)
- **Algoa Cabinda Fabrication** — Wellhead maintenance, yard operations
- **[SONILS](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) (Sociedade Nacional de Industria e Logística)** — Logistics base, equipment handling
- **Operatec** — Well services support
- **[Petromar](../../data/company-directory/petromar.md)** — Local drilling support services
- **[Sonamet](../../data/company-directory/sonamet.md)** — Fabrication yard, workshop experience

### Target Roles
- Rig floor assistant / Roughneck
- Wellsite trainee
- Equipment coordinator (yard-based)
- Workshop technician (drill pipe, BOP)

### Skills to Study
- [02 — Drilling Fundamentals](../skills/02-drilling-fundamentals.md): Rig types, drill string, BHA, casing
- [01 — Well Control](../skills/01-well-control.md): BOP, kick detection, shut-in procedures
- [12 — Offshore HSE](../skills/12-offshore-hse-safety.md): PTW, risk matrix, BOSIET prep

### Action Items
- [ ] Book [BOSIET](../glossary.md) course (Luanda or Soyo providers)
- [ ] Register [IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 1 course
- [ ] Apply to Tier 3 companies above (walk-in with CV + all certs)
- [ ] Learn basic rig layout — draw a rig from memory (derrick, rotary table, mud pits, BOP stack)
- [ ] Study well construction sequence: spud → conductor → surface casing → intermediate → production

## Phase 2: Technical Growth (Year 1–3)

### Goal
Move to Tier 2 (OFS companies) for structured technical training. Specialize in one drilling discipline.

### Target Employers (Tier 2)
- **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) ([Schlumberger](../../data/company-directory/slb.md))** — Field Engineer Trainee (drilling services, M-I SWACO fluids)
- **[Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md)** — LEAD Program (Sperry Drilling, Baroid fluids)
- **[Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md)** — Emerging Talent Program ([directional drilling](../skills/05-directional-drilling.md), drilling fluids)
- **[Weatherford](../../data/company-directory/weatherford.md)** — Drilling services, tubular running services

### Target Roles
- [MWD](../glossary.md)/LWD Field Engineer
- Directional Driller (DD) Trainee
- [Drilling Fluids](../skills/03-drilling-fluids.md) Engineer (mud engineer)
- Cementing / Completions Field Engineer

### Certifications to Add
- **[IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 2 (Supervisor/Driller)** — [Well control](../skills/01-well-control.md) at supervisory level (~$1,500–$2,500)
- **IADC WellSharp** — Industry standard [well control](../skills/01-well-control.md) (complementary to [IWCF](../glossary.md))

### Skills to Study
- [05 — Directional Drilling](../skills/05-directional-drilling.md): Trajectory design, MWD surveys, geosteering
- [06 — MWD/LWD Tools](../skills/06-mwd-lwd-tools.md): Downhole sensors, real-time telemetry
- [03 — Drilling Fluids](../skills/03-drilling-fluids.md): Mud types, rheology, solids control
- [13 — Cementing & Completions](../skills/13-cementing-and-completions.md): Primary cementing, wiper plugs
- [04 — Formation Evaluation](../skills/04-formation-evaluation.md): Log interpretation for formation tops
- [15 — Well Integrity](../skills/15-well-integrity.md): Barrier philosophy, MAASP

### Software to Learn
| Software | Purpose | Provider |
|---|---|---|
| **WellPlan** | Torque & drag, hydraulics, casing design | Landmark ([Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md)) |
| **COMPASS** | Wellbore survey and trajectory planning | Landmark |
| **WellCat** | Casing design, thermal modeling | Landmark |
| **Drillbench** | Managed pressure drilling, [well control](../skills/01-well-control.md) simulation | OLGA/[Schlumberger](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) |
| **AutoPipe / CemCADE** | Cementing design | [Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md) |

### Action Items
- [ ] Apply to [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md), Halliburton, or [Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md) trainee programs
- [ ] Build relationship with Tier 2 field engineers you met during Tier 3 (get referrals)
- [ ] Complete [IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 2
- [ ] Log 180+ offshore days for rig credentials
- [ ] Master the daily drilling report (DDR) — learn to write one professionally
- [ ] Study SPE papers on Angola deepwater wells (Block 17, Block 32)

## Phase 3: Specialization (Year 3–5)

### Goal
Transition from field to office-based drilling engineering, or become a senior field specialist. Begin well planning.

### Target Employers
- **Tier 2 (Senior Field):** [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md), [Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md), [Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md) — Senior Field Engineer / District Technical Lead
- **Tier 1 (Operator well planning):** [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Eni, Chevron, Azule Energy, ExxonMobil — Junior Drilling Engineer in Wells team

### Target Roles
- Drilling Engineer (well planning, AFE preparation)
- Senior Directional Driller
- Drilling Supervisor / Night Toolpusher
- Wells Operations Engineer

### Certifications to Add
- **[IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 3/4 (Well Intervention / Engineer level)** — Advanced [well control](../skills/01-well-control.md)
- **NEBOSH IGC** — If considering HSE-integrated roles

### Skills to Deepen
- Casing design calculations (burst, collapse, tension, biaxial)
- Hydraulics optimization (ECD management in deepwater)
- Stuck-pipe prevention and fishing operations
- Managed Pressure Drilling (MPD) — growing in Angola deepwater
- Pore pressure & fracture gradient prediction (pre-drill and real-time)

### Software to Master
| Software | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Techlog** | Log analysis for formation tops / pore pressure |
| **Petrel Well Section** | 3D well planning within geological model |
| **OLGA** | Multiphase flow, [well control](../skills/01-well-control.md) scenarios |
| **Python / MATLAB** | Custom drilling calculations, data analysis |

### Action Items
- [ ] Network into operator wells teams through service company contacts
- [ ] Build a portfolio: well designs, post-well reports, drilling optimization case studies
- [ ] Present at SPE Angola section or company technical forums
- [ ] Study economics: well cost breakdown, AFE preparation, NPT analysis
- [ ] Learn contract management basics (day rates, performance contracts)

## Phase 4: Senior & Leadership (Year 5–10+)

### Goal
Become a recognized drilling/wells authority. Lead teams, manage budgets, drive technical standards.

### Target Roles
- Senior Drilling Engineer
- Wells Team Lead / Wells Manager
- Drilling Superintendent
- VP Wells / Chief Wells Engineer (long-term)

### Target Employers
- **Operators:** [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) Angola, Chevron ([CABGOC](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md)), [ExxonMobil](../../data/company-directory/exxonmobil.md), Eni, [Azule Energy](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md), Sonangol E.P.
- **Consultancies:** AGR Well Management, Exceed Well Management, Wild [Well Control](../skills/01-well-control.md)
- **Drilling contractors (management):** Transocean, Valaris, Noble Corp — Rig Manager roles

### Skills at This Level
- Multi-well campaign planning (batch drilling, continuous improvement)
- Drilling technology evaluation (RSS, MPD, automated drilling)
- Regulatory compliance with ANPG drilling standards
- Budgeting: $50M–$200M+ well campaign cost management
- Team leadership: managing 20–50+ field engineers and contractors

### Action Items
- [ ] Pursue SPE Petroleum Engineering Certification
- [ ] Publish technical papers (SPE, IADC, OTC conferences)
- [ ] Build consulting network for post-operator career options
- [ ] Consider advanced degree (MSc Drilling Engineering — Heriot-Watt, Curtin, LSU)

## Angola-Specific Context

### Active Drilling Blocks
| Block | Operator | Water Depth | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Block 0 | [Chevron](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md) ([CABGOC](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md)) | <500 m | Cabinda shallow water, Angola's oldest |
| Block 14 | [Chevron](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md) | 150–400 m | Deepwater, Benguela-Belize-Lobito |
| Block 15 | [Eni](../../data/company-directory/eni.md) | 200–1,500 m | Kizomba complex, mature fields |
| Block 15/06 | [Eni](../../data/company-directory/eni.md)/[Azule](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md) | 300–1,800 m | Agogo discovery, major development |
| Block 17 | [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) | 600–1,500 m | Girassol, Dalia, Pazflor, CLOV |
| Block 17/06 | [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) | 1,200–1,900 m | Cameia discovery |
| Block 31 | [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) | 1,800–2,500+ m | Ultra-deepwater (Kaombo) |
| Block 32 | TotalEnergies | 1,500–2,200 m | New development phase |
| Block 18 | BP/[Azule](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md) | 1,200–1,800 m | Greater Plutonio |

### Rigs Operating in Angola (Typical Fleet)
- **Drillships:** Valaris DS-series, Transocean Deepwater Proteus-class, Noble Globetrotter
- **Semi-submersibles:** Some on older blocks (Block 0)
- **Tender-assist rigs:** Occasionally for shallow Block 0 workovers

### Key Drilling Challenges in Angola
1. **Deepwater riser management** — 1,500–2,500 m water depth, heavy mud columns
2. **Narrow pore pressure/fracture gradient window** — Requires MPD on some Block 31/32 wells
3. **Salt sections** — Pre-salt formations in some Angola blocks (like Brazil's pre-salt but less extensive)
4. **Lost circulation** — Common in fractured carbonate zones
5. **Wellbore instability** — Reactive shales in overburden sections
6. **Logistics** — Remote offshore locations, limited helicopter slots, weather windows

## Key Professional Organizations

- **SPE (Society of Petroleum Engineers)** — Angola Section (Luanda). Attend monthly technical talks.
- **IADC (International Association of Drilling Contractors)** — Industry standards, WellSharp program.
- **AIPN (Association of International Petroleum Negotiators)** — Useful for contract understanding.
- **ANPG (Agência Nacional de Petróleo, Gás e Biocombustíveis)** — Regulator. Know their standards.
