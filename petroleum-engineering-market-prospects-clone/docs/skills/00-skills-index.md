## Priority Study Order — Offshore Graduate (Entry Level)

If you are starting from zero and want your first offshore job within 12 months:

### Months 1–2: Safety-First Foundation
1. **Skill 12 — [Offshore HSE](12-offshore-hse-safety.md)** — Book your BOSIET; learn risk matrix and PTW
2. **Skill 01 — [Well Control](01-well-control.md)** — Most employers ask about this; consider IWCF

### Months 3–4: Core Technical (Pick One Track)

**Track A: Drilling/OFS ([SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md), [Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md), [Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md))**
3. **Skill 02 — [Drilling Fundamentals](02-drilling-fundamentals.md)**
4. **Skill 03 — [Drilling Fluids](03-drilling-fluids.md)**
5. **Skill 07 — [Mudlogging](07-mudlogging.md)**

**Track B: [FPSO](../glossary.md)/Production ([TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Eni, Azule)**
3. **Skill 08 — [FPSO Process Operations](08-fpso-process-operations.md)**
4. **Skill 09 — [Artificial Lift](09-artificial-lift.md)**
5. **Skill 10 — [Flow Assurance](10-flow-assurance.md)**

**Track C: Subsea ([Subsea 7](../../data/company-directory/subsea7.md), [TechnipFMC](../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md), [Oceaneering](../../data/company-directory/oceaneering.md))**
3. **Skill 11 — [Subsea Systems](11-subsea-systems.md)**
4. **Skill 10 — [Flow Assurance](10-flow-assurance.md)**
5. **Skill 15 — [Well Integrity](15-well-integrity.md)**

### Months 5–8: Depth Building
- Whichever remaining skills apply to your track
- Focus on formulas: Archie equation, Darcy's Law, Arps' decline, MAASP calculation

### Months 9–12: Advanced and Angola Context
- Complete all 15 skills
- Read specific SPE papers for Angola blocks (Block 17, 32, 15)
- Start learning software: PIPESIM, CMG, WellPlan, or Python for PE calculations
