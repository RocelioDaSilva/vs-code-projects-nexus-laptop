# petroleum-engineering-market-prospects

A concise, practical guide for Petroleum Engineering students seeking offshore career paths in Angola. The repository collects ANPG category mappings, company research, role descriptions, and actionable job-search advice focused on drilling, production, subsea, and reservoir careers.

How to use this repository

1. **Start here:** Read [`SUMMARY.md`](docs/SUMMARY.md) for recommended reading order and orientation.
2. **Understand the market:** Review [`glossary.md`](docs/glossary.md) (§ [ANPG Service Categories](docs/angola/anpg-company-service-categories.md)) and [`service-specializations.md`](docs/career/service-specializations.md) to understand Angola's offshore sectors, entry routes, and role-by-role details.
3. **Find employers:** Use [`angolan-offshore-companies.md`](docs/angola/angolan-offshore-companies.md) and [`company-directory/`](data/company-directory) for company research, career links, and quick-apply URLs.
4. **Plan your career:** Consult [`career-climbing-strategy.md`](docs/career/career-climbing-strategy.md) for a realistic 5–7 year Tier 3 → Tier 2 → Tier 1 path with role progressions and salary expectations.
5. **Prepare to apply:** Follow [`application-guide.md`](docs/career/application-guide.md) for certifications ([BOSIET](docs/glossary.md), IWCF, H2S), CV strategy, interview tips, and salary negotiation.
6. **Know the reality:** Read [`offshore-reality-check.md`](docs/career/offshore-reality-check.md) for an honest account of offshore life, first rotations, and mental health realities.
7. **Learn the language:** Use [`glossary.md`](docs/glossary.md) as a quick reference for technical and industry terms.
8. **Map your role:** Consult [`service-specializations.md`](docs/career/service-specializations.md) to match ANPG service categories to specific job families, entry roles, and career progression.
9. **ISPTEC students:** Start with the [ISPTEC Career Playbook](isptec/guide/00_START_HERE.md) (9-file guided path from market overview to applications), then go deeper with the [Tseluca Career Accelerator](isptec/tseluca/00_INDEX.md) (30+ files: 6 career paths, 13 skill tutorials, discipline deep-dives, certifications, company targeting). The [ISPTEC Curriculum Map](isptec/curriculum/00_START_HERE_Curriculum_Map.md) connects every Licenciatura subject to industry roles.
10. **Find courses, books, and free software:** Use [`learning-resources.md`](docs/learning-resources.md) for free/paid platforms, Angola certification providers, student software, and recommended books by skill.

- Help petroleum engineering graduates and students find **the fastest, most realistic path to offshore rotational roles** in Angola (drilling, production, subsea, reservoir).
- Provide **actionable resources** for certification, job search, interview preparation, and long-term career strategy.
- Build a **living guide** reflecting the actual hiring practices, compensation, and career structures in Angola's offshore sector (verified via industry sources, company websites, and field practitioner feedback).
