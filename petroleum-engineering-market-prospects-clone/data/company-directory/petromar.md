Petromar — profile

Website: https://www.petromar.co.ao (verify availability)
Careers: Contact via LinkedIn or direct application
Angola presence: Local Angolan fabrication and logistics company based in Luanda with yard facilities in Ambriz (Bengo province). Provides offshore support services including fabrication, pipe coating, logistics, and supply base operations. Key local content partner for major operators.
Typical entry roles: Graduate Engineer (Mechanical, Operations), Logistics Coordinator, Yard Technician, QA/QC Inspector.
Keywords: Petromar, Ambriz, Fabrication, Logistics, Supply Base, Local Content, Angola
Apply notes: Petromar is a local Angolan company — hiring strongly favors Angolan nationals. Apply via LinkedIn or unsolicited CV submission. Petromar is a Tier 3 entry point providing exposure to fabrication and logistics operations that feed directly into offshore projects.

LinkedIn: Search "Petromar Angola" on LinkedIn
Tip: Petromar's Ambriz yard handles fabrication for multiple operators. It's an excellent Tier 3 entry for mechanical/structural engineering graduates. 2–3 years at Petromar → transition to [TechnipFMC](technipfmc.md), [Saipem](saipem.md) (T&I engineer track), or directly to operator logistics roles. The supply base experience is also valued by [SONILS](sonangol.md)-based companies.
