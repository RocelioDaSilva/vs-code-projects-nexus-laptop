PAENAL (Porto Amboim Estaleiros Navais) — profile

Website: https://www.paenal.com (verify availability)
Careers: Contact via general email or LinkedIn
Angola presence: Fabrication and integration yard at Porto Amboim, Kwanza Sul province. Joint venture between [Sonangol](sonangol.md) and [SBM Offshore](sbmoffshore.md). Specializes in FPSO topsides integration, module fabrication, and offshore construction support. One of Angola's key local content fabrication assets.
Typical entry roles: Graduate Engineer (Mechanical, Structural), QA/QC Inspector, Fabrication Technician, Commissioning Support Engineer.
Keywords: PAENAL, Porto Amboim, Fabrication, [FPSO](../../docs/glossary.md), Topsides, Commissioning, Sonangol
Apply notes: PAENAL is a local content yard — hiring favors Angolan nationals. Apply via LinkedIn, direct email, or through [Sonangol](sonangol.md)'s supplier network. Fabrication yard work is onshore but provides direct exposure to FPSO systems and T&I workflows. Good bridge to offshore commissioning roles.

LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/company/paenal/
Tip: PAENAL is project-driven (peaks during [FPSO](../../docs/glossary.md) integration campaigns). Monitor announcements from [SBM Offshore](sbmoffshore.md) and [Sonangol](sonangol.md) for new FPSO projects — PAENAL typically handles the local fabrication scope. 2–3 years here can lead to offshore commissioning roles with [TechnipFMC](technipfmc.md), [Saipem](saipem.md), or [SBM Offshore](sbmoffshore.md).
