## Tier 3 (Local Angolan Providers) — Typical Entry Points for Graduates

**[SONILS](sonangol.md) ([Sonadrill](sonangol.md) Logistics)**
- Service: Offshore logistics, supply base, marine support
- Entry roles: Logistics Technician, Operations Coordinator, Field Assistant
- Entry salary: USD 800–1,200/month
- Verify: ANPG registry; check Oferta Permanente for active registration
- Path: 1–2 years at [SONILS](sonangol.md) → Tier-2 service company ([SLB](slb.md), HAL) or operator

**Algoa Cabinda Fabrication**
- Service: Fabrication, maintenance, assembly, construction
- Entry roles: Welder, QA Inspector, Field Technician, Junior Engineer
- Entry salary: USD 800–1,500/month
- Verify: ANPG registry; check [Sonangol](sonangol.md) supplier portal
- Path: 1–2 years at Algoa → [SLB](slb.md), [Halliburton](halliburton.md), or [Chevron](chevron.md) ([CABGOC](chevron.md)) entry
- Note: Direct connection to [Chevron](chevron.md) ([CABGOC](chevron.md)) operations

**Operatec**
- Service: Engineering services, equipment maintenance, technical support
- Entry roles: Junior Engineer, Field Assistant, Maintenance Technician
- Entry salary: USD 1,000–1,500/month
- Verify: ANPG registry; known quality provider
- Path: 1–2 years → [TechnipFMC](technipfmc.md), Subsea7, or Saipem (subsea track) or [SLB](slb.md) (drilling track)

**[Petromar](petromar.md) (Yard in Ambriz)**
- Service: Subsea fabrication, yard operations, construction
- Entry roles: Welding Inspector, QA Technician, Construction Supervisor
- Entry salary: USD 1,000–1,500/month (on-yard basis)
- Verify: ANPG registry; major [EPC](../../docs/glossary.md) partner ([Saipem](saipem.md), TechnipFMC)
- Path: 2–3 years at [Petromar](petromar.md) → [TechnipFMC](technipfmc.md), Saipem (T&I engineer track)

**[PAENAL](paenal.md) (Porto Amboim Estaleiros Navais)**
- Service: [FPSO](../../docs/glossary.md) topsides integration, module fabrication, quayside load-out
- Entry roles: Graduate Engineer (Mechanical/Structural), QA/QC Inspector, Yard Planner
- Entry salary: USD 1,000–1,800/month
- Verify: ANPG registry; [SBM Offshore](sbmoffshore.md)/[Sonangol](sonangol.md) JV
- Path: 2–3 years → [SBM Offshore](sbmoffshore.md) (offshore [FPSO](../../docs/glossary.md) ops) or [Saipem](saipem.md) (construction engineer)

**[SONAMET](sonamet.md) ([Sonangol](sonangol.md) Metalúrgica)**
- Service: Subsea structure fabrication, pipe spooling, jacket/platform construction
- Entry roles: Fabrication Technician, Welding Inspector, Project Planner, Junior Engineer
- Entry salary: USD 1,000–1,800/month
- Verify: ANPG registry; [Subsea7](subsea7.md)/[Sonangol](sonangol.md) JV in Lobito
- Path: 2–3 years → [Subsea7](subsea7.md) (offshore installation) or [TechnipFMC](technipfmc.md) (subsea track)
- Note: Due diligence required — check recent governance news before applying

**Brimont, Cabship, Poliedro, Prodoil**
- Service: Specialized services (lifting, rigging, vessel services, equipment, drilling support)
- Entry roles: Field Technician, Inspector, Equipment Operator
- Entry salary: USD 800–1,200/month
- Verify: ANPG registry; smaller but solid local providers
- Path: 1–2 years → Tier-2 company or operator

**Newrest Angola**
- Service: Catering, camp management, logistics
- Entry roles: Camp Manager Trainee, Catering Coordinator, Logistics Support
- Entry salary: USD 600–1,000/month
- Verify: ANPG registry; contracts with [TotalEnergies](totalenergies.md), Chevron, and others
- Path: Not technical track; use for network building and experience only
- Note: Lowest entry point but limited technical growth

**Certex Angola**
- Service: Lifting and rigging certification, [NDT](../../docs/glossary.md) inspection
- Entry roles: Inspection Technician, Quality Inspector
- Entry salary: USD 1,000–1,500/month
- Verify: ANPG registry; specialized niche provider
- Path: 2–3 years → Operator integrity teams or specialist contractor
