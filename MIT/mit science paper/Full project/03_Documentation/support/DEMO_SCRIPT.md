GEESP-Angola — 3-minute Demo Script (Dashboard + Key Results)

Audience: Boston presentation / funder pitch (tight time, highlight impact)

Duration: 3 minutes

0:00–0:15 — Opening Hook
- "In rural Huíla, 50% of households lack reliable electricity. GEESP-Angola identifies where small, community-led solar systems will deliver the biggest impact — quickly and transparently."

0:15–0:45 — Show Map + Problem
- Load dashboard map centered on Huíla; toggle to `Aptidão Integrada` layer.
- Zoom to Cacula: point to high-aptitude clusters; show population density overlay.
- Narration: "This cluster serves 18,400 people with LCOE ~USD 0.19/kWh — cheaper and faster than diesel alternatives."

0:45–1:45 — Technology Matching + Financials
- Click a high-aptitude polygon; open `Site Profile` panel showing suggested technology (PV-fixed), expected beneficiaries, and 10-year cashflow.
- Show one-slide summary of costs/IRR: `USD 50.5M portfolio → 14% IRR`.
- Narration: "We match system types to site conditions automatically — maximizing uptime and local benefit."

1:45–2:30 — Validation & Safeguards
- Show Appendix B (validation protocol) and Risk Screening Checklist briefly.
- Narration: "Each pilot follows a defined validation protocol with piranometer baseline and community consent before deployment."

2:30–3:00 — Ask / Call to Action
- "We seek partners to pilot Cacula in Q2 2026 — funding and operational partners welcome. Visit our repo and dashboard for full details." 
- Provide contact slide (MINEA liaison + research lead)
