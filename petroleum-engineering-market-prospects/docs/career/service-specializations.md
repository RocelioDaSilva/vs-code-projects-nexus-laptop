## Recommended Entry Sequence for Petroleum Engineers

**Fastest path to 28/28 rotation (Years 1�2):**
1. **Tier 3:** Field Technical Assistant ([SONILS](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) or Algoa Cabinda)
2. **Tier 2:** Field Engineer Trainee ([SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) or [Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md)) � drilling or production track
3. **Tier 1:** Drilling or Production Engineer ([TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Chevron, ExxonMobil)

**Most lucrative path (Years 1�5):**
1. **Tier 3:** Operations Support ([SONILS](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md), Operatec)
2. **Tier 2:** [MWD](../glossary.md)/LWD Specialist ([SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) Sperry) or Completions Engineer (HAL, BH)
3. **Tier 1:** Senior Drilling or Completions Engineer (Operator)

**Long-term stable path (Years 1�7):**
1. **Tier 3:** Field Assistant ([Petromar](../angola/angolan-offshore-companies.md) or welding QA)
2. **Tier 2:** Subsea Installation or T&I Engineer ([TechnipFMC](../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md), Saipem, Subsea 7)
3. **Tier 1:** Project/Commissioning Engineer (Operator) or [EPC](../glossary.md) Management
