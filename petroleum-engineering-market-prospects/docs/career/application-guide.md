6) Interview & vetting tips (what employers ask)
- Safety-first questions: be prepared to describe safety incidents or what you would do in an HSE scenario.
- Technical basics: mud weight, ROP, casing design basics, what BOP stands for, separation stages on an [FPSO](../glossary.md).
- Behavioral: teamwork, handling fatigue on rotation, conflict resolution on a diverse team.
- Ask: rotation pattern (e.g., 28/28), training program duration, mobilization timeframe, lodging quality, and medicals required.
