## Part 1: Expanded Multi-Tier Contractor Tree — Granular Detail by Operator

### Understanding the Tier Structure

Before we descend into the specific trees, it is essential to understand the contractual and legal architecture that governs employment in Angola's offshore sector. This is not arbitrary; it is mandated by Presidential Decree No. 271/20 (the Local Content Regime).

- **Tier 1: The Concessionaire (Block Owner/Operator).** This entity holds the Risk Service Contract (RSC) or Production Sharing Agreement (PSA) with ANPG. They are financially responsible for the block. They do *not* perform the work themselves. They are the "architect" and "bank."
- **Tier 2: Tier-1 Service Providers (Direct Contracts).** These are the large international companies that bid for and win major scopes of work directly from the Operator. Examples: [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) for integrated drilling services; [Saipem](../../data/company-directory/saipem.md) for EPC; [SBM Offshore](../../data/company-directory/sbmoffshore.md) for FPSO lease and operate.
- **Tier 3: Subcontractors & Local JV Partners.** The Tier-1 providers cannot do everything themselves, nor would ANPG allow them to. They subcontract specialized work (e.g., riser manufacturing) and form Joint Ventures with Angolan entities to meet local content targets.
- **Tier 4: Specialized Niche Providers.** These are smaller, often Angolan-owned, companies that provide essential but narrow services: catering, security, rope access inspection, waste management, and crucially, **training** ([BOSIET](../glossary.md)/HUET).
- **Tier 5: Entry Points for a Graduate.** This is where *you* fit in.

### 1. TotalEnergies

- **Tier 1 – Block Owner (Operator)**
  - **Subsidiary:** **[TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) E&P Angola** (also operates under the legacy name Total E&P Angola).
  - **Key Offshore Blocks:** Block 17 (Operator, 38% in CLOV, 30% in various fields), Block 17/06 (Operator, 30%), Block 32 (Operator, 30%), Block 20/11 (Operator, 50%).
  - **Argument for Importance:** [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) operates assets representing over 45% of Angola's total national oil production. The company's Angolan portfolio is the single largest and most diverse in the country. Securing a position here, even indirectly, is the gold standard.

- **Tier 2 – Tier-1 Service Providers (Direct Contracts)**
  - **Category 1 (Drilling, Completion & Well Intervention):**
    - **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) OneSubsea:** Awarded a major contract for a 13-well subsea production system for the Kaminho project in Block 20/11. This involves subsea trees, manifolds, and controls.?previous response†part-1?
    - **[Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md):** Secured a contract to supply centrifugal and electrical-driven integrated compressor line (ICL) compressors for the all-electric Kaminho [FPSO](../glossary.md) project. This is a critical technology for minimizing emissions and eliminating routine flaring.
    - **[Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md):** Provides ongoing cementing and completions services for the CLOV and Begonia tie-back projects. These are "brownfield" projects connecting new wells to existing FPSOs.?previous response†part-1?
  - **Category 3 (Engineering & Project Management):**
    - **[TechnipFMC](../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md):** A long-term partner for subsea engineering, specifically providing integrated Engineering, Procurement, Construction, and Installation (iEPCI) services. They are heavily involved in the CLOV Phase 3 development in Block 17.?previous response†part-1?
  - **Category 6 (Transport, Installation, Hook-up & Commissioning):**
    - **[Saipem](../../data/company-directory/saipem.md):** Awarded a massive **$3.7 billion** [EPC](../glossary.md) contract package for the Kaminho project, including a 30km [SURF](../glossary.md) (Subsea, Umbilicals, Risers, and Flowlines) package.?previous response†part-1?
    - **McDermott:** Delivered the EPSCI (Engineering, Procurement, Supply, Construction, and Installation) contract for the Begonia field tie-back to the Pazflor [FPSO](../glossary.md).?previous response†part-1?
    - **[Subsea 7](../../data/company-directory/subsea7.md):** Has a contract for subsea infrastructure installation for the Agogo project, which is an Azule Energy project but in a block where TotalEnergies is a major stakeholder.?previous response†part-1?
  - **Category 8 (Geosciences Services):**
    - **CGG (Viridien):** Provides 4D seismic processing and reservoir characterization services, primarily for Block 17. This is "time-lapse" seismic, critical for monitoring how the reservoir is depleting over time and planning infill wells.?previous response†part-1?

- **Tier 3 – Subcontractors & Local JV Partners**
  - **Fabrication:** [Saipem](../../data/company-directory/saipem.md) partners extensively with the **Petromar yard in Ambriz** for the fabrication of large subsea structures and platform components. This is a deliberate local content strategy.
  - **Local Affiliates:** **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) Angola** is the local registered entity that executes SLB's contracts and is subject to Angolan labor and tax laws. **[TechnipFMC](../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md)** has maintained established offices and industrial facilities in Angola since the 1970s.
  - **Marine Logistics:** Companies like **BOURBON** and **Tidewater** provide the anchor handling tugs, supply vessels (AHTS/PSV), and crew boats that service [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md)' drilling rigs and FPSOs. These are often contracted by the drilling contractor or directly by TotalEnergies.

- **Tier 4 – Specialized Niche Providers**
  - **Catering & Camp Services:** **Newrest** holds a major contract with [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) (since 2023) for catering services on multiple offshore platforms and onshore camps. This is a critical but often overlooked part of offshore morale.
  - **Training:** **OSHAS Academy** and the **Industrial Fire and Safety Academy (IFSA)** in Soyo are the primary providers of OPITO-approved [BOSIET](../glossary.md) and [HUET](../glossary.md) training.
  - **Inspection & Certification:** Local firms like **Certex Angola** provide lifting gear inspection and certification, a non-negotiable safety requirement on any rig.

- **Tier 5 – Entry Points for a Petroleum Engineering Graduate**
  - **Direct Entry:** **[TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) Young Graduate Program.** This is an 18-month structured program for Angolan nationals with a bachelor's or master's degree. It includes rotations through different departments (Drilling, Production, Projects) and often includes an international assignment.
  - **Service Company Entry:** **Field Engineer Trainee** at [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md), [Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md), or [Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md). These are the most common and accessible entry points. You are hired by the service company but spend 80%+ of your time working on TotalEnergies' rigs or projects.
  - **Typical Job Titles:** "Young Graduate – Drilling & Wells," "Field Engineer Trainee – [MWD](../glossary.md)/LWD," "Junior Subsea Engineer."

- **Tier 2 – Tier-1 Service Providers (Direct Contracts)**
  - **Category 1 (Drilling, Completion & Well Intervention):**
    - Afentra is currently in the planning phase for its 2026 drilling and workover program on Block 3/24. It is expected to contract with major service providers like **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md), Halliburton, or Baker Hughes** for these campaigns. As a smaller operator, it may also consider competitive bids from local service companies.
  - **Category 6 (Transport, Installation, Hook-up & Commissioning):**
    - For its shallow-water operations, Afentra will require marine support (supply vessels, crew boats), likely contracting with established players like **BOURBON** or **Tidewater**.

- **Tier 3 – Subcontractors & Local JV Partners**
  - **Partners:** The other partners in Block 3/24 are Maurel & Prom Angola (40%) and Sonangol E&P (20%). This partnership provides Afentra with significant in-country expertise.

- **Tier 4 – Specialized Niche Providers**

- **Tier 5 – Entry Points for a Petroleum Engineering Graduate**
  - **Direct Entry:** **Junior Drilling Engineer** or **Production Technologist** at Afentra. As a smaller company, job postings may be less frequent and more targeted. Direct networking and approaching the company are key.
  - **Service Company Entry:** You would be working *for* a service company like [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) but deployed *on* Afentra's projects. This is the most likely first step.

### Narrative Analysis: Choosing Your Path

**Best Overall Employer for a New Graduate (Drilling Engineering): TotalEnergies**

TotalEnergies offers the optimal combination of a world-class, structured training program (Young Graduate), excellent compensation and benefits, a strong safety culture, and unmatched long-term job security in Angola. The breadth of its portfolio—from the mature Block 17 to the new frontier Block 20/11 (Kaminho)—means you will be exposed to the full lifecycle of deepwater operations. The only downside is that entry is highly competitive.

**Operator vs. Service Company — The Fundamental Trade-Offs**

**The "Hidden Gem": Afentra**

Afentra is the high-risk, high-reward play. For an ambitious, entrepreneurial graduate, joining a new operator of a shallow-water block offers a career accelerator that a supermajor cannot match. You will be part of a small team where your contributions are highly visible. You will gain experience in well planning, AFE management, and contractor oversight that might take 7+ years to get at a large company. The risk is that the 5-year contract may not be extended, but the skills you gain are your portable asset. **Target Afentra if you want to be a decision-maker faster than your peers.**

**Companies to Approach with Caution**

- **Chevron ([CABGOC](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md)):** **Approach with eyes wide open regarding the 2025 BBLT platform fire.** In any interview, you should ask direct and respectful questions about what specific safety improvements have been implemented since that incident. A company's response to tragedy is as revealing as the tragedy itself.
- **[Sonangol](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md):** Approach with the understanding that career progression will be slower and more bureaucratic. It is a stable, long-term home but not a fast-track.
- **Small, Unvetted Local Contractors:** Always verify their ANPG registration, their safety record, and their commitment to training. The lower tiers of the service industry can be a "wild west," and your first job should be with an established player that prioritizes your development.

## Part 4: The "First 5-Year" Strategic Recommendation — A Precise, Actionable Pathway

### Years 1-2: The Foundation (Maximum Field Exposure)

**Recommendation: Target [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) or [Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md) as a Field Engineer Trainee.**

**Why This is the Optimal First Step:**
1.  **Accessibility:** Getting a direct-hire graduate position at an Operator like [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) is statistically very difficult. Service companies hire larger cohorts of field engineers every year. Baker Hughes' "LEAD - Field Engineer" program in Angola, for example, is a 36-month program that is currently recruiting. This is a more achievable first step.
2.  **The "Field-Proven" Credential:** The first question a Senior Drilling Supervisor will ask you is, "Have you been on a rig?" A 2-3 year stint as a service company Field Engineer answers that question definitively. You will gain a level of practical, hands-on knowledge that an "office engineer" simply cannot acquire. You will learn to read a mud report at 3:00 AM, troubleshoot a downhole tool failure, and communicate effectively with a Toolpusher who has 30 years of experience and no time for academic theory.
3.  **Financial Advantage:** The offshore day-rate uplifts will give you a strong financial foundation early in your career.

**Specific, Actionable Steps:**
1.  **Immediately apply to:** **[Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md)' "Emerging Talent: LEAD - Field Engineer (ANGOLA) 2026 Opportunities"** on LinkedIn and their careers portal.
2.  **Search for and apply to:** "Entry Level Field Engineer" or "Field Engineer Trainee" positions at **[Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md)** and **SLB** for their Luanda locations.
3.  **Before Applying, Obtain a Tangible Credential:** Complete the free **[IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 1 Well Control Awareness Course** online. It takes 4-6 hours and provides a certificate. Mentioning this in your cover letter demonstrates initiative and a genuine interest in drilling.

### Years 3-5: The Pivot to Planning & Long-Term Strategy

**Recommendation: Transition to an Operator (Target [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) or Azule Energy).**

After 2-3 years of intense field experience, you are now a highly desirable candidate for an Operator. You have the "field-proven" credential and you speak the language of the rig.

**Path A (Recommended) — Move to [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) as a Drilling Engineer:**
- **Strategy:** Apply for experienced-hire positions at [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) E&P Angola. Your service company experience makes you a stronger candidate than a fresh graduate. You can also check the eligibility requirements for TotalEnergies' Young Graduate Program; they sometimes accept candidates with up to 2 years of relevant experience.
- **By Year 5, you should aim for:** **Senior Drilling Engineer** role. In this position, you will be the primary author of drilling programs, responsible for casing design and AFE cost tracking, and will lead the peer review of well plans.

**Path B — Move to [Azule Energy](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md) for Faster Promotion:**
- **Strategy:** [Azule](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md) Energy is in a growth phase and needs experienced engineers. Your field experience will be highly valued. Use the network you built while working on their projects (e.g., if you were a [Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md) engineer on an Agogo well) to make connections within Azule's drilling department.
- **By Year 5, you should aim for:** **Senior Drilling Engineer** role, potentially faster than at [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) due to Azule's flatter organizational structure.

### The "Switch to Production" Pathway (Years 5+)

This is the long-term goal you articulated. Here is the precise, realistic pathway.

- **Option 1 (Within Same Operator) — The Internal Pivot:**
  - **Year 3-4:** As a Drilling Engineer, **volunteer** for any project that bridges the gap between drilling and production. This includes:
    - **Well Completion Design:** Drilling makes the hole; completion makes it produce. This is the most natural bridge.
    - **Subsea Tree Installation Planning:** Working on the interface between the wellhead and the subsea production system.
  - **Year 5:** Formally request a transfer or apply for an open position in the **Production Technology** or **[Flow Assurance](../skills/10-flow-assurance.md)** team. Your argument is simple: "I know how this well was drilled. I understand the reservoir from the drilling data. I am uniquely positioned to help optimize its production." This is a compelling and logical internal career move.

- **Option 2 (Master's Degree) — The Formal Qualification:**
  - **Year 5:** Apply for a **company-sponsored Master's degree** in **Production Engineering** or **Subsea Engineering**. [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), [Chevron](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md), and [ExxonMobil](../../data/company-directory/exxonmobil.md) all have scholarship programs for high-potential employees.
  - This signals a clear, intentional career pivot and provides the formal academic credential to back up your practical experience.

- **The Research Path (If You Choose It):**
  - **Target:** **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md)'s Africa Performance Center in Luanda.** This 3,200 sqft state-of-the-art facility opened in January 2025 and is a collaborative hub for digital, AI, and new energy solutions. It is the premier location for oil and gas R&D in Angola. A role here would involve working with [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md)'s Delfi digital platform, which uses AI to accelerate well planning and reservoir modeling.
  - **How to Get In:** Target [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md)'s "University Relations" and "Internship" programs. A PhD is not required; SLB hires engineers for these centers to bridge the gap between pure research and operational deployment.
