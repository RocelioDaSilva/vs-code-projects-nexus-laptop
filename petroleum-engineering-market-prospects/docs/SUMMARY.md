Repository summary and recommended reading order

Purpose
- Comprehensive guide for petroleum engineering students and graduates seeking offshore rotational careers in Angola.
- Provides market analysis, company research, career strategies, certification guides, and realistic lifestyle preparation.

---

## Recommended Reading Order

**Phase 1: Understand the Market (30 min, 3 files)**

1. [README.md](../README.md) — Overview of repository structure and how to use it.
2. [SUMMARY.md](SUMMARY.md) — This reading guide.
3. [anpg-company-service-categories.md](angola/anpg-company-service-categories.md) — Ranked ANPG service categories most relevant to petroleum engineers. Shows what actually leads to offshore rotations.

**Phase 2: Find Your Role (45 min, 1 file)**

4. [service-specializations.md](career/service-specializations.md) — **Master niche guide.** ANPG-to-role mapping with salary ranges, career progression, specialized skill requirements, and a quick-reference cheat sheet. Helps you choose drilling vs. production vs. subsea.

**Phase 3: Understand Employers (60 min, 3 files)**

5. [company-directory/](../data/company-directory) — Profiles for major operators ([TotalEnergies](../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Chevron, ExxonMobil, Azule, Sonangol) and Tier-1 service companies ([SLB](../data/company-directory/slb.md), [Halliburton](../data/company-directory/halliburton.md), [Baker Hughes](../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md), TechnipFMC, Subsea7, SBM Offshore, Yinson, Oceaneering). Includes websites, Angola presence, typical entry roles, and careers links.
6. [company-directory/producer-service-tree.md](../data/company-directory/producer-service-tree.md) — Node tree showing which service providers work for which operators. Maps dependencies and supply chains.
7. [angolan-offshore-companies.md](angola/angolan-offshore-companies.md) — **Master company guide.** Exhaustive Tier 1–5 contractor analysis, plus quick-apply career links for every major employer.

**Phase 4: Plan Your Career (60 min, 1 file)**

8. [career-climbing-strategy.md](career/career-climbing-strategy.md) — **Central strategic document.** A realistic 5–7 year career-climbing plan: Tier 3 → Tier 2 → Tier 1. Includes target employers, timeline, salary progression, mentorship tactics, certifications checklist, networking playbook, pitfalls to avoid, and a year-by-year drilling engineer progression guide.

**Phase 5: Prepare to Apply (90 min, 2 files)**

9. [application-guide.md](career/application-guide.md) — Comprehensive guide to: mandatory offshore certifications ([BOSIET](glossary.md)/HUET, IWCF, H2S), costs, providers in Angola, validity periods. CV strategy for Angola (bilingualism emphasis, certifications-first format). Salary expectations by tier and role. How to apply (direct, LinkedIn, referral). Interview tips. Mentorship ("Padrinho") strategy. Tier-3 entry-level companies and how to contact them.
10. [glossary.md](glossary.md) — Technical terminology reference (ROP, WOB, [BOSIET](glossary.md), BOP, ECD, FPSO, [SURF](glossary.md), MWD/LWD, umbilicals, nodal analysis, etc.). Use as a quick lookup while reading.

**Phase 6: Know the Reality (45 min, 1 file)**

11. [offshore-reality-check.md](career/offshore-reality-check.md) — **Read this before you commit.** Raw, unfiltered account of first offshore rotation: helicopter operations, 05:15 mobilization, 12-hour shifts on the rig floor, isolation, fatigue, and coping strategies. Now includes reference tables for Angola's active FPSOs, drilling rigs, and company desirability rankings.

**Phase 7: Choose Your Career Path (60–90 min, pick 1 file)**

12. [career-paths/](career-paths/00-career-paths-index.md) — **NEW. Step-by-step career guides.** Choose your discipline and follow a phased plan from graduation to senior roles. Each guide includes year-by-year milestones, certifications, software to learn, essential books, target employers, and Angola-specific context.
    - [01 — Drilling Engineer](career-paths/01-drilling-engineer.md)
    - [02 — Production Engineer](career-paths/02-production-engineer.md)
    - [03 — Reservoir Engineer](career-paths/03-reservoir-engineer.md)
    - [04 — Subsea Engineer](career-paths/04-subsea-engineer.md)
    - [05 — HSE/Safety Engineer](career-paths/05-hse-safety-engineer.md)
    - [06 — Completions & Well Intervention](career-paths/06-completions-engineer.md)

**Phase 7b: Career Deep Dives (reference, pick your discipline)**

13. [career-deep-dives/](career-deep-dives/) — **Comprehensive career encyclopedias.** Each file answers 26 detailed questions: daily work, global salary tables, 15 interview questions, books by career stage, software tools, portfolio projects, certifications ROI, career roadmaps, day-1 action plans, and more.
    - [Drilling Engineer](career-deep-dives/career_drilling_engineer.md)
    - [Production Engineer](career-deep-dives/career_production_engineer.md)
    - [Reservoir Engineer](career-deep-dives/career_reservoir_engineer.md)
    - [Subsea Engineer](career-deep-dives/career_subsea_engineer.md)
    - [HSE/Safety Engineer](career-deep-dives/career_hse_engineer.md)
    - [Completions Engineer](career-deep-dives/career_completions_engineer.md)
    - [Career → Skill Learning Maps](career-deep-dives/career-learning-maps.md) — maps each career to its skill training modules

**Phase 8: Build Technical Skills (ongoing, pick your track)**

14. [skills/](skills/00-skills-index.md) — 15 technical skill learning guides, each with Beginner → Advanced roadmaps, Angola context, and role mappings. See the [Skills Index](skills/00-skills-index.md) for which modules match your chosen career path.
    - [Skill Compendium](skills/skill-compendium.md) — 12-month learning paths for all 15 skills with month-by-month plans, projects, and edge courses

15. [career-niches.md](career/career-niches.md) — Complete overview of all 15 career niches with salary benchmarks, curriculum mapping, and job growth outlook.

**Phase 9: ISPTEC Student Track (start here if you're at ISPTEC)**

16. [isptec/guide/](../isptec/guide/00_START_HERE.md) — **Offshore Career Playbook.** 9-file guided pathway from Angola market overview through career tracks, companies, certifications, offshore reality, to application strategy. Read in order.

17. [isptec/tseluca/](../isptec/tseluca/00_INDEX.md) — **Tseluca Career Accelerator.** The complete system for ISPTEC Year 3–4 students targeting offshore rotation. Includes:
    - [6 career-path roadmaps](../isptec/tseluca/paths/01_Drilling_Path.md) with semester-by-semester plans
    - [13 skill tutorials](../isptec/tseluca/skills/01_Well_Control_Tutorial.md) with worked examples and Angola context
    - [6 discipline deep-dives](../isptec/tseluca/discipline/00_PE_Discipline_Map.md) (field development, well integrity, petroleum economics, etc.)
    - [Company Targeting Guide](../isptec/tseluca/support/Company_Targeting_Guide.md) — every major Angola operator and service company
    - [Certifications Angola](../isptec/tseluca/support/Certifications_Angola.md) — where to get every cert in Luanda with costs

18. [isptec/curriculum/](../isptec/curriculum/00_START_HERE_Curriculum_Map.md) — ISPTEC curriculum-to-career mapping: 64 subject files connecting Licenciatura subjects to industry roles + 3 sector guides (Upstream, Midstream, Downstream).

## Getting Started (Priority 1: Immediate)

**If you have 2 hours:**
1. Read [README.md](../README.md) (5 min)
2. Skim [service-specializations.md](career/service-specializations.md) — pick 1 job family that interests you (15 min)
3. Find matching companies in [company-directory/](../data/company-directory) (20 min)
4. Read [career-climbing-strategy.md](career/career-climbing-strategy.md) to understand the Tier 3→2→1 path (60 min)
5. Skim [application-guide.md](career/application-guide.md) section on certifications (20 min)

**If you have 5 hours:**
Complete the "2 hours" path above, then:
6. Read [offshore-reality-check.md](career/offshore-reality-check.md) (45 min)
7. Deep dive into [company-directory/](../data/company-directory) profiles for your 5 target companies (30 min)
8. Read [application-guide.md](career/application-guide.md) in full (45 min)

**If you have 10+ hours:**
Complete all of the above, plus:
9. Read [angolan-offshore-companies.md](angola/angolan-offshore-companies.md) for context (90 min)
10. Consult [glossary.md](glossary.md) while reading technical files
11. **Choose your career path** from [career-paths/](career-paths/00-career-paths-index.md) — follow the step-by-step guide for your discipline (60 min)
12. Study the relevant [skills modules](skills/00-skills-index.md) for your chosen path (ongoing)
13. Build your application strategy using [application-guide.md](career/application-guide.md)
14. Create your 5–7 year plan using [career-climbing-strategy.md](career/career-climbing-strategy.md)

## Final Note

This repository is built for **students and graduates in Angola seeking realistic, actionable career guidance.** Every document is grounded in current industry practices, verified company information, and practitioner feedback. It is not a generic career guide; it is Angola-specific.

**Read the files in order.** They build on each other. Start with Phase 1 (market understanding) before jumping to Phase 5 (application prep).

**Good luck. Now go climb.**
