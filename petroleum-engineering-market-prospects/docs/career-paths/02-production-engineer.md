# Career Path: Production Engineer

> A step-by-step guide from graduation to senior production engineer in Angola's offshore sector.
> Estimated timeline: 5–7 years from entry-level to senior/production manager roles.

---

## Why Production Engineering?

Once wells are drilled, they must **produce** — and someone must keep them producing efficiently. Production engineers are responsible for every barrel that flows from reservoir to export tanker. Angola operates **10+ FPSOs** (Girassol, Dalia, Pazflor, CLOV, Kaombo, Kizomba A/B, Greater Plutonio, [FPSO](../glossary.md) N'Goma), each a floating factory producing 100,000–200,000+ bbl/d. Every FPSO needs production engineers — this path is one of the most stable in the industry.

**Salary progression (Angola):**

| Stage | Role | Monthly (USD) | Annual (USD) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Year 0–1 | Production Technician / Process Trainee | $1,200–$2,500 | $14,400–$30,000 |
| Year 1–3 | [FPSO](../glossary.md) Production Operator / Junior Production Engineer | $2,500–$5,000 | $30,000–$60,000 |
| Year 3–5 | Production Engineer (Tier 2/1) | $5,000–$8,000 | $60,000–$96,000 |
| Year 5–7 | Senior Production Engineer / Production Optimization Lead | $8,000–$12,000 | $96,000–$144,000 |
| Year 7–10+ | Production Manager / Asset Manager | $12,000–$18,000+ | $144,000–$216,000+ |

## Phase 1: Foundation (Year 0–1)

### Goal
Understand surface production systems, get [FPSO](../glossary.md) exposure, earn mandatory certifications.

### Certifications to Complete
1. **[BOSIET](../glossary.md)/HUET** — Mandatory before any offshore trip (~$500–$1,500)
2. **Offshore Medical Certificate** — Valid 2 years (~$300–$800)
3. **STCW Basic Safety Training** — Required on FPSOs (maritime vessels) (~$300–$800)
4. **[H2S](../glossary.md) Awareness** — Required for production facilities (~$500)
5. **[IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 1** — [Well control](../skills/01-well-control.md) awareness (useful even for production roles)

### Target Employers (Tier 3/4 Entry)
- **[SONILS](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md)** — Logistics base, equipment familiarization
- **[Petromar](../../data/company-directory/petromar.md)** — Production support services
- **[Yinson](../../data/company-directory/yinson.md)** — FPSO operator (leases [FPSO](../glossary.md) Agogo to [Eni](../../data/company-directory/eni.md)/[Azule](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md))
- **[SBM Offshore](../../data/company-directory/sbmoffshore.md)** — FPSO operator (N'Kossa, other West Africa units)
- **Local operators** — Tank farm operations, onshore processing

### Target Roles
- Process plant trainee / control room assistant
- Production technician (onshore terminal)
- [FPSO](../glossary.md) production operator trainee
- Instrument/electrical technician (P&ID familiarization)

### Skills to Study
- [08 — FPSO Process Operations](../skills/08-fpso-process-operations.md): Separation, gas treatment, water injection, export
- [09 — Artificial Lift](../skills/09-artificial-lift.md): ESP, gas lift, IPR/VLP nodal analysis
- [12 — Offshore HSE](../skills/12-offshore-hse-safety.md): PTW, risk matrix, ESD systems
- [10 — Flow Assurance](../skills/10-flow-assurance.md): Hydrates, wax, chemical injection basics

### Action Items
- [ ] Book [BOSIET](../glossary.md) and STCW courses
- [ ] Learn the FPSO process flow: wellhead → manifold → separator → gas/water treatment → oil export
- [ ] Study P&IDs (Piping & Instrumentation Diagrams) — learn to read them
- [ ] Understand the three-phase separator: how oil, gas, and water are separated
- [ ] Apply to FPSO operators ([Yinson](../../data/company-directory/yinson.md), [SBM](../../data/company-directory/sbmoffshore.md) Offshore) and local production companies

## Phase 2: Technical Growth (Year 1–3)

### Goal
Move to an operator or major service company. Learn well performance analysis and production optimization.

### Target Employers (Tier 2/1)
- **[TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) Angola** — Production operations on Block 17/31/32 FPSOs
- **[Eni](../../data/company-directory/eni.md) / [Azule Energy](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md)** — Block 15/06 production operations
- **[Chevron](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md) ([CABGOC](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md))** — Block 0, Block 14 production
- **[Weatherford](../../data/company-directory/weatherford.md)** — [Artificial lift](../skills/09-artificial-lift.md) specialist (ESPs, rod pumps, gas lift valves)
- **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) Production Management** — Well performance monitoring, PROSPER/GAP

### Target Roles
- [FPSO](../glossary.md) Production Operator (senior grade)
- Junior Production Engineer (operator wells team)
- [Artificial Lift](../skills/09-artificial-lift.md) Engineer ([Weatherford](../../data/company-directory/weatherford.md), SLB)
- Well Performance Analyst

### Certifications to Add
- **[IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 2** — [Well control](../skills/01-well-control.md) at supervisory level
- **Process Safety (IChemE)** — Hazard identification, HAZOP participation

### Skills to Study
- [14 — Reservoir Engineering](../skills/14-reservoir-engineering.md): Darcy's law, material balance, decline curves (understand what drives production)
- [15 — Well Integrity](../skills/15-well-integrity.md): Barrier philosophy, MAASP, production casing monitoring
- [11 — Subsea Systems](../skills/11-subsea-systems.md): Wellheads, flowlines connecting wells to FPSO
- [01 — Well Control](../skills/01-well-control.md): Kill procedures for producing wells

### Software to Learn
| Software | Purpose | Provider |
|---|---|---|
| **PROSPER** | Well performance modeling (IPR/VLP, nodal analysis) | PETEX |
| **GAP** | Network optimization (multi-well production system) | PETEX |
| **PIPESIM** | Multiphase flow, production system design | [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) |
| **WellFlo** | [Artificial lift](../skills/09-artificial-lift.md) design and optimization | Weatherford |
| **OFM (Oil Field Manager)** | Production data surveillance, decline analysis | [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) |
| **Spotfire / Power BI** | Production data visualization and dashboards | TIBCO / Microsoft |

### Action Items
- [ ] Apply to operator production teams ([TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Eni, [Azule](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md))
- [ ] Master nodal analysis (intersection of IPR and VLP curves)
- [ ] Learn to diagnose underperforming wells (skin damage, scale, wax, sand production)
- [ ] Understand gas lift design and ESP selection for Angola deepwater
- [ ] Build proficiency in production reporting (daily production allocation, well test reporting)
- [ ] Study the Angola [FPSO](../glossary.md) fleet — know each unit, its operator, and capacity

## Phase 3: Specialization (Year 3–5)

### Goal
Become the go-to production engineer on your asset. Lead production optimization initiatives. Build cross-functional knowledge.

### Target Roles
- Production Engineer (leading well optimization on an [FPSO](../glossary.md))
- Production Optimization Engineer (multi-well, field-level)
- [Artificial Lift](../skills/09-artificial-lift.md) Specialist (ESP fleet management for an asset)
- [Flow Assurance](../skills/10-flow-assurance.md) Engineer (production interface)

### Skills to Deepen
- **Nodal analysis mastery** — Sensitivity analysis, impact of tubing size, gas lift injection rate optimization
- **Well intervention planning** — When to workover vs. stimulate vs. change [artificial lift](../skills/09-artificial-lift.md)
- **Water management** — Water cut trending, produced water reinjection (PWRI), scale management
- **Sand management** — Gravel packs, sand screens, critical drawdown calculations
- **Production allocation** — Well test validation, metering uncertainty, fiscal allocation
- **Decline curve analysis** — Arps' equations, type curve matching, reserves estimation

### Software to Master
| Software | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **PIPESIM (advanced)** | Network modeling, field optimization |
| **Eclipse / CMG** | Reservoir model coupling for integrated production models |
| **Python / MATLAB** | Custom production analytics, automation |
| **PI System (OSIsoft)** | Real-time production data historian |
| **OLGA** | Transient multiphase flow (slugging, shutdown/startup analysis) |

### Action Items
- [ ] Lead a production optimization project (e.g., gas lift optimization, ESP fleet review)
- [ ] Build integrated models (reservoir + well + facility)
- [ ] Present results at SPE Angola or internal technical forums
- [ ] Study reservoir simulation basics — understand what drives your wells from below
- [ ] Learn basic facility constraints: separator capacity, gas compressor limits, water treatment limits

## Phase 4: Senior & Leadership (Year 5–10+)

### Goal
Lead production teams, manage asset-level production targets, interface with reservoir and facilities teams.

### Target Roles
- Senior Production Engineer
- Production Manager ([FPSO](../glossary.md)-based or onshore office)
- Asset Manager (responsible for entire block production)
- Production Technology Advisor

### Target Employers
- **Operators:** [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Eni, Azule Energy, Chevron, ExxonMobil, Sonangol
- **Consultancies:** Xodus, CGG, Rystad Energy — production advisory
- **[FPSO](../glossary.md) owners:** [SBM Offshore](../../data/company-directory/sbmoffshore.md), BW Offshore, Yinson — fleet performance management

### Skills at This Level
- Multi-field production forecasting
- Economics: production revenue optimization vs. operating costs (OPEX)
- Regulatory reporting to ANPG (production records, compliance)
- Field development plan (FDP) contributions — production facilities sizing
- Team leadership: managing 10–30+ production engineers and technicians

### Action Items
- [ ] Pursue SPE Petroleum Engineering Certification
- [ ] Consider MSc in Production Engineering or Petroleum Engineering (Heriot-Watt, Aberdeen, Imperial)
- [ ] Publish SPE papers on Angola production optimization case studies
- [ ] Build consulting network for independent career options
- [ ] Mentor junior engineers — build your reputation as a "padrinho"

### Key Production Challenges in Angola
1. **Water cut management** — Many mature fields have rising water cuts (Block 15, Block 17 older satellites)
2. **ESP failures** — Deepwater ESPs operate at high temperatures, corrosive environments; run-life optimization is critical
3. **Gas lift optimization** — Some fields use gas lift rather than ESPs; injection gas supply is limited
4. **[Flow assurance](../skills/10-flow-assurance.md)** — Cold seabed temperatures (3–4°C) cause hydrate and wax risks in flowlines
5. **Subsea integrity** — Ageing subsea infrastructure on 20+ year old fields
6. **Production decline** — Angola's overall production has declined from ~1.8M bbl/d peak to ~1.1M bbl/d; production engineers must squeeze more from existing wells
