# Career Path: Reservoir Engineer

> A step-by-step guide from graduation to senior reservoir engineer / subsurface manager in Angola.
> Estimated timeline: 5–8 years from entry-level to senior/asset manager roles.

---

## Why Reservoir Engineering?

Reservoir engineers are the **decision-makers of the subsurface**. Every field development plan (FDP), every well location, every waterflood strategy, and every reserves booking depends on reservoir engineering analysis. In Angola, operators manage multi-billion-dollar deepwater assets, and reservoir engineers directly influence billions in investment decisions.

This is one of the highest-paid and most intellectually demanding career tracks in petroleum engineering. It is also one of the hardest to enter — most roles require 2–5 years of oilfield experience before operators will hire you into reservoir engineering.

**Salary progression (Angola):**

| Stage | Role | Monthly (USD) | Annual (USD) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Year 0–2 | Production/Petroleum Engineer Trainee | $1,500–$3,000 | $18,000–$36,000 |
| Year 2–4 | Junior Reservoir Engineer | $3,500–$6,000 | $42,000–$72,000 |
| Year 4–6 | Reservoir Engineer | $6,000–$10,000 | $72,000–$120,000 |
| Year 6–8 | Senior Reservoir Engineer | $10,000–$15,000 | $120,000–$180,000 |
| Year 8–12+ | Subsurface Manager / Asset Manager | $15,000–$22,000+ | $180,000–$264,000+ |

## Phase 1: Foundation (Year 0–2)

### Goal
Build field experience and fundamental petroleum engineering skills. You typically do NOT start as a reservoir engineer — you need field context first.

### Certifications to Complete
1. **[BOSIET](../glossary.md)/HUET** — Mandatory before offshore work (~$500–$1,500)
2. **Offshore Medical Certificate** — Valid 2 years
3. **[IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 1** — [Well control](../skills/01-well-control.md) awareness
4. **SPE Membership** — Start building your network immediately

### Common Entry Paths
Reservoir engineering is typically reached through one of these routes:
1. **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md)/[Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md)/[Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md) Field Engineer** → 2–3 years field, then apply to operator RE team
2. **Operator Graduate Program** ([TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) YGP, Azule Energy internship) → Direct RE assignment
3. **Production Engineer** → 2–3 years, then internal transfer to reservoir team
4. **Petrophysicist / [Formation Evaluation](../skills/04-formation-evaluation.md)** → Transfer into reservoir via subsurface team

### Target Employers (Entry)
- **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md)** — Reservoir testing (DST), production logging, reservoir characterization services
- **[Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md)** — Formation tester tools, reservoir evaluation
- **[TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) Angola** — Young Graduate Program (reservoir stream)
- **[Azule Energy](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md)** — Professional internship program (150 positions/year)
- **[Sonangol](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) E.P.** — Annual graduate intake
- **[Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md)** — Reservoir assessment services

### Target Roles
- Petroleum / Production Engineer Trainee
- [Formation Evaluation](../skills/04-formation-evaluation.md) Trainee (field-based)
- Reservoir Testing Field Engineer (DST operations)
- Production Data Analyst

### Skills to Study
- [14 — Reservoir Engineering](../skills/14-reservoir-engineering.md): Darcy's law, material balance, decline curves, reserves classification
- [04 — Formation Evaluation](../skills/04-formation-evaluation.md): Log interpretation, porosity, saturation calculation
- [09 — Artificial Lift](../skills/09-artificial-lift.md): IPR curves, production rate relationships
- [08 — FPSO Process Operations](../skills/08-fpso-process-operations.md): Understand the surface system your wells feed into
- [01 — Well Control](../skills/01-well-control.md): Pressure fundamentals (pore pressure, hydrostatic pressure)

### Action Items
- [ ] Join SPE as a student/young professional member
- [ ] Apply to operator graduate programs ([TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), [Azule](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md), [Chevron](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md), Sonangol)
- [ ] If no direct RE entry: take any field engineering role at [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md)/[Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md) and build toward RE
- [ ] Master pressure-volume-temperature (PVT) basics — understand Bo, Rs, μ, z-factor
- [ ] Study Darcy's law until you can derive it from first principles
- [ ] Practice material balance calculations by hand

## Phase 2: Technical Growth (Year 2–4)

### Goal
Transition into a dedicated reservoir engineering role. Build simulation and analysis skills.

### Target Employers (Tier 1/2)
- **[TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) Angola** — Reservoir studies team (Block 17, 32)
- **[Eni](../../data/company-directory/eni.md) / [Azule Energy](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md)** — Subsurface team (Block 15/06 — Agogo development)
- **[Chevron](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md) ([CABGOC](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md))** — Reservoir management (Block 0, 14)
- **[ExxonMobil](../../data/company-directory/exxonmobil.md)** — Subsurface engineering (Block 15)
- **[Sonangol](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) E.P.** — Reservoir management department
- **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) Reservoir Performance** — Consulting and studies

### Target Roles
- Junior Reservoir Engineer
- Reservoir Studies Analyst
- Reservoir Simulation Support Engineer
- Production/Reserves Analyst

### Certifications to Add
- **SPE Petroleum Engineering Certification** — Industry-recognized professional qualification
- **Petrel/Eclipse Certified User** — [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) certification for reservoir modeling tools

### Skills to Study
- [10 — Flow Assurance](../skills/10-flow-assurance.md): Understand well-to-facility fluid behavior
- [15 — Well Integrity](../skills/15-well-integrity.md): Pressure monitoring, barrier management from subsurface perspective
- [02 — Drilling Fundamentals](../skills/02-drilling-fundamentals.md): Understand well construction for well placement optimization
- [05 — Directional Drilling](../skills/05-directional-drilling.md): Extended-reach well trajectories, well targeting

### Software to Learn
| Software | Purpose | Provider |
|---|---|---|
| **Petrel RE** | Geological/reservoir model building | [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) |
| **Eclipse 100/300** | Black-oil and compositional reservoir simulation | SLB |
| **CMG (IMEX/GEM/STARS)** | Alternative reservoir simulator (thermal, compositional) | CMG |
| **OFM** | Production surveillance, decline analysis | SLB |
| **MBAL** | Material balance analysis | PETEX |
| **Topaze** | Analytical reservoir model ([TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) internal) | TotalEnergies |
| **Python / MATLAB** | Custom analytics, history matching automation | Open source |

### Core Technical Skills
- **Decline curve analysis** — Arps' equations (exponential, hyperbolic, harmonic)
- **Material balance** — Havlena-Odeh, Schilthuis, drive mechanism identification
- **Well test analysis** — Pressure buildup (Horner plot), drawdown, PTA interpretation
- **Reserves classification** — SEC, PRMS (SPE), proved/probable/possible (1P/2P/3P)
- **PVT analysis** — Fluid sampling, EOS tuning, Bo/Rs/μ correlations
- **Volumetric estimation** — STOIIP/GIIP calculation, Monte Carlo uncertainty

### Action Items
- [ ] Take Eclipse/CMG introductory training (SPE or SLB offer short courses)
- [ ] Build your first reservoir model (even a simple 1D model in CMG/Eclipse)
- [ ] Practice well test interpretation using real or practice PTA data
- [ ] Read the SPE Reserves Estimation course notes
- [ ] Study Angola block geology: turbidite channel sands, salt tectonics, structural traps
- [ ] Master Excel-based reservoir calculations before relying on software

## Phase 3: Specialization (Year 4–6)

### Goal
Become a full reservoir engineer. Lead reservoir studies, history-match models, book reserves, plan well targets.

### Target Roles
- Reservoir Engineer (leading an asset/field study)
- Reservoir Simulation Engineer
- Reserves Evaluator
- Field Development Planning Engineer

### Skills to Deepen
- **History matching** — Calibrate simulation models to production history (most time-consuming RE task)
- **Uncertainty & risk analysis** — Monte Carlo simulation, decision trees, P10/P50/P90
- **EOR screening** — Water alternating gas (WAG), polymer flooding, CO₂ injection applicability
- **Integrated asset modeling** — Coupled reservoir-well-network-facility models
- **Geostatistics** — Variograms, kriging, stochastic simulation for property distribution
- **Field development planning** — Optimum well count, well spacing, phased development

### Software to Master
| Software | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Petrel (advanced)** | Integrated reservoir modeling workflow |
| **Eclipse (advanced)** | Full-field simulation, sector models, history matching |
| **RMS (Roxar/Emerson)** | Alternative geological modeling |
| **Intersect** | High-resolution simulation ([SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) next-gen) |
| **tNavigator** | Alternative fast simulator (Rock Flow Dynamics) |
| **Python** | OOIP scripts, automated history matching (SciPy, pyESMDA) |
| **Spotfire / Power BI** | Reserves reporting, production dashboards |

### Action Items
- [ ] Lead or co-lead a reservoir study (history match + forecast + well targets)
- [ ] Participate in reserves audits (DeGolyer & MacNaughton, NSAI, Ryder Scott methodologies)
- [ ] Present at SPE Angola or internal company technical forums
- [ ] Publish a technical paper (SPE ATCE, EAGE, or OTC)
- [ ] Learn economics: NPV, IRR, EMV for development decisions
- [ ] Understand Angola regulatory requirements for reserves reporting to ANPG

## Phase 4: Senior & Leadership (Year 6–12+)

### Goal
Become a subsurface authority. Lead teams, influence FDP decisions, manage reserves portfolios.

### Target Roles
- Senior Reservoir Engineer
- Lead Reservoir Engineer / Technical Authority
- Subsurface Manager
- Asset Manager
- Reserves Manager

### Target Employers
- **Operators:** [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Eni, Azule, Chevron, ExxonMobil — senior subsurface roles
- **Consultancies:** DeGolyer & MacNaughton, NSAI, Gaffney Cline — reserves auditing
- **Advisory firms:** Rystad Energy, Wood Mackenzie — market analysis
- **National companies:** [Sonangol](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) (strategic planning, reservoir management)

### Skills at This Level
- Multi-field portfolio management (reserves reporting across all assets)
- FDP approval process with ANPG (Angolan regulatory submissions)
- Capital allocation decisions ($100M–$1B+ investments)
- Cross-functional leadership: interfacing drilling, facilities, finance, legal
- Mentoring: building the next generation of reservoir engineers

### Action Items
- [ ] Pursue MSc/PhD in Reservoir Engineering if desired (Stanford, Imperial, Heriot-Watt, Tulsa)
- [ ] Obtain SPE Petroleum Engineering Certification (if not already)
- [ ] Build international network through SPE committees (ATCE, Reservoir Study Groups)
- [ ] Consider roles at international consultancies for portfolio breadth
- [ ] Develop business acumen: understand JOA, PSA, concession agreement economics

## Angola-Specific Context

### Major Reservoir Types in Angola
| Reservoir Type | Blocks | Characteristics |
|---|---|---|
| **Oligocene–Miocene turbidite channels** | 17, 31, 32, 15 | High-quality sands, channel-levee architecture, strong aquifer support |
| **Pre-salt carbonates** | Emerging (Block 20/21) | Fractured carbonates, analogs to Brazil's pre-salt (Lula, Búzios) |
| **Cretaceous clastics** | 0, 14 | Shallow water, mature fields, high water cuts |
| **Sub-salt plays** | Frontier (Block 48, 49) | Exploration targets, potential major discoveries |

### Key Reservoir Engineering Challenges in Angola
1. **Turbidite reservoir heterogeneity** — Complex channel architecture makes prediction difficult
2. **Aquifer uncertainty** — Strong aquifer influx can dominate production but is hard to model
3. **Water breakthrough** — Mature fields (Block 15 Kizomba, Block 17 Girassol) experiencing high water cuts
4. **EOR opportunities** — WAG (Water Alternating Gas) being evaluated for Block 17 fields
5. **Pressure maintenance** — Water injection and gas injection schemes across all major blocks
6. **Reserves maturation** — Converting 2P/3P reserves to 1P through appraisal drilling
7. **Data quality** — Older fields have limited modern logging/testing data

### Key Operators for RE Roles in Angola
| Operator | Blocks | RE Team Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **[TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md)** | 17, 17/06, 20/21, 31, 32, 33, 48 | Largest RE team in Angola. Turbidite modeling, EOR studies, new developments |
| **[Eni](../../data/company-directory/eni.md) / [Azule Energy](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md)** | 15/06 | Agogo field development — fast-track FDP, reservoir characterization |
| **[Chevron](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md) ([CABGOC](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md))** | 0, 14 | Mature field management, waterflood optimization |
| **[ExxonMobil](../../data/company-directory/exxonmobil.md)** | 15 | Kizomba A/B reservoir management, decline mitigation |
| **[Sonangol](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) E.P.** | Multiple (as concessionaire) | Reserves oversight, ANPG reporting, JV partner reviews |
