# Career Path: Subsea Engineer

> A step-by-step guide from graduation to senior subsea engineer in Angola's deepwater sector.
> Estimated timeline: 5–8 years from entry-level to senior/subsea manager roles.

---

## Why Subsea Engineering?

Angola's entire deepwater production infrastructure is **subsea**. Every well on Blocks 15, 17, 31, and 32 connects to an [FPSO](../glossary.md) through subsea trees, manifolds, flowlines, umbilicals, and risers. With 400+ subsea wells operating in Angola, and new developments like Block 15/06 Agogo adding more, subsea engineers are in constant demand for installation, operations, maintenance, and decommissioning.

Subsea engineering is highly specialized, well-compensated, and one of the fastest-growing niches as fields mature and require intervention/replacement of aging infrastructure.

**Salary progression (Angola):**

| Stage | Role | Monthly (USD) | Annual (USD) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Year 0–1 | Subsea Technician / [ROV](../glossary.md) Trainee | $1,200–$2,500 | $14,400–$30,000 |
| Year 1–3 | Junior Subsea Engineer / [ROV](../glossary.md) Pilot Technician | $2,500–$5,500 | $30,000–$66,000 |

## Phase 1: Foundation (Year 0–1)

### Goal
Understand subsea hardware, gain offshore exposure, and build foundational knowledge of what sits on the seabed.

### Certifications to Complete
1. **[BOSIET](../glossary.md)/HUET** — Mandatory for offshore work (~$500–$1,500)
2. **Offshore Medical Certificate** — Valid 2 years
3. **STCW Basic Safety** — Required for vessel-based work (~$300–$800)
4. **[ROV](../glossary.md) Awareness / Subsea Awareness Course** — IMCA or equivalent (~$500–$1,500)

### Target Employers (Tier 3/4 Entry)
- **[Oceaneering](../../data/company-directory/oceaneering.md)** — ROV services, subsea tooling (very large Angola presence)
- **[Subsea 7](../../data/company-directory/subsea7.md)** — SURF installation, IRM (Inspection, Repair, Maintenance)
- **[TechnipFMC](../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md)** — Subsea production systems, flexible pipes, umbilicals
- **[Saipem](../../data/company-directory/saipem.md)** — Pipeline installation, subsea construction
- **DOF Subsea / Solstad** — Subsea support vessel operators

### Target Roles
- [ROV](../glossary.md) Pilot Technician Trainee (most common subsea entry point)
- Subsea Technician / Equipment Handler
- Offshore Support Vessel (OSV) deck crew
- Workshop technician — subsea tree assembly, valve testing

### Skills to Study
- [11 — Subsea Systems](../skills/11-subsea-systems.md): Wellheads, Xmas trees, manifolds, umbilicals, risers, ROV
- [12 — Offshore HSE](../skills/12-offshore-hse-safety.md): PTW, lifting operations, confined spaces
- [15 — Well Integrity](../skills/15-well-integrity.md): Barrier philosophy, subsea wellhead pressure monitoring
- [10 — Flow Assurance](../skills/10-flow-assurance.md): Hydrate formation in subsea flowlines

### Action Items
- [ ] Book [BOSIET](../glossary.md) and STCW courses
- [ ] Apply to [Oceaneering](../../data/company-directory/oceaneering.md) and Subsea 7 for trainee positions (both have Angola offices)
- [ ] Learn to identify subsea hardware: draw a subsea production system from memory
- [ ] Study the subsea production lifecycle: installation → commissioning → operation → intervention → decommissioning
- [ ] Understand [ROV](../glossary.md) classes: observation (Class I), work-class (Class III), trenching
- [ ] Learn basic hydraulics: how subsea control systems work (electrohydraulic, direct hydraulic, all-electric)

## Phase 2: Technical Growth (Year 1–3)

### Goal
Specialize in one subsea discipline: [SURF](../glossary.md) (Subsea, Umbilicals, Risers, Flowlines), SPS (Subsea Production Systems), or [ROV](../glossary.md) operations.

### Target Employers (Tier 2)
- **[TechnipFMC](../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md)** — World leader in subsea trees and flexible pipe; large Angola presence
- **[Subsea 7](../../data/company-directory/subsea7.md)** — Major SURF contractor; offshore installation and IRM
- **[Oceaneering](../../data/company-directory/oceaneering.md)** — ROV fleet operator; subsea engineering services
- **OneSubsea ([SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md))** — Subsea processing, boosting, compression
- **Aker Solutions** — Subsea production systems, manifolds
- **[Saipem](../../data/company-directory/saipem.md)** — Heavy-lift installation, pipeline lay

### Target Roles
- [ROV](../glossary.md) Pilot Technician (qualified)
- Subsea Engineer (installation support)
- Subsea Controls Engineer
- [SURF](../glossary.md) Engineer (flowline/riser design support)
- IRM (Inspection, Repair, Maintenance) Engineer

### Certifications to Add
- **IMCA [ROV](../glossary.md) Competence** — If pursuing ROV track (International Marine Contractors Association)
- **[IWCF](../glossary.md) Level 2** — If working on wellhead interventions
- **CSWIP 3.4U** — Underwater inspection (if pursuing IRM/inspection)

### Skills to Study
- [01 — Well Control](../skills/01-well-control.md): Subsea BOP systems, deepwater well control
- [08 — FPSO Process Operations](../skills/08-fpso-process-operations.md): How subsea systems connect to topsides
- [09 — Artificial Lift](../skills/09-artificial-lift.md): Subsea boosting, multiphase pumps
- [14 — Reservoir Engineering](../skills/14-reservoir-engineering.md): Understand what drives flow from reservoir through subsea system

### Software to Learn
| Software | Purpose | Provider |
|---|---|---|
| **AutoCAD / AutoCAD Plant 3D** | Subsea layout drawings, P&IDs | Autodesk |
| **OLGA** | Multiphase flow simulation in pipelines | [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md)/Kongsberg |
| **PIPESIM** | Steady-state flow modeling | [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) |
| **Orcaflex** | Riser and mooring dynamics | Orcina |
| **ANSYS / Abaqus** | Structural analysis (subsea structures, connectors) | Ansys / Dassault |
| **LedaFlow** | Advanced multiphase flow (Kongsberg) | Kongsberg |

### Action Items
- [ ] Choose a subsea subdiscipline: SPS (trees/manifolds), [SURF](../glossary.md) (pipes/risers), ROV, or controls
- [ ] Qualify as ROV Pilot Tech (if pursuing ROV path) — requires 500+ hours logged
- [ ] Study riser types: steel catenary (SCR), top-tensioned (TTR), flexible risers
- [ ] Learn flexible pipe structure: carcass, pressure sheath, armor wires, outer sheath
- [ ] Understand umbilical design: hydraulic lines, electrical, fiber optic, chemical injection
- [ ] Study subsea tree types: horizontal (most common in Angola), vertical, dual-bore

## Phase 3: Specialization (Year 3–5)

### Goal
Become a full subsea engineer. Lead installation campaigns, manage IRM scopes, design [subsea systems](../skills/11-subsea-systems.md).

### Target Roles
- Subsea Engineer (operator wells/facilities team)
- [SURF](../glossary.md) Design Engineer ([FEED](../glossary.md) to detailed design)
- Subsea Installation Engineer (offshore campaign lead)
- Subsea Integrity Engineer (lifecycle management)
- [ROV](../glossary.md) Superintendent (if [ROV](../glossary.md) track)

### Skills to Deepen
- **Subsea system design** — [FEED](../glossary.md) studies, equipment selection, vendor evaluation
- **Installation engineering** — Lift plans, rigging calculations, offshore installation procedures
- **[Flow assurance](../skills/10-flow-assurance.md) integration** — Insulation requirements, MEG/MeOH injection rates, pigging design
- **Subsea integrity management** — Corrosion monitoring, cathodic protection, fatigue life assessment
- **Deepwater riser engineering** — Dynamic analysis, vortex-induced vibration (VIV), riser fatigue
- **Materials selection** — CRA (Corrosion Resistant Alloy) for sour service, duplex stainless steel

### Software to Master
| Software | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Orcaflex (advanced)** | Full riser/mooring dynamic analysis |
| **ANSYS Mechanical** | Subsea structure FEA (stress, fatigue) |
| **PIPESIM (advanced)** | Full-field production system modeling |
| **OLGA (advanced)** | Transient [flow assurance](../skills/10-flow-assurance.md), shutdown/restart, slugging |
| **Python** | Automation of engineering calculations, data analysis |
| **Subsea Production Control System simulators** | Hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) for testing SPS controls |

### Action Items
- [ ] Work on at least one subsea installation campaign from engineering through offshore execution
- [ ] Understand SPS procurement: tree selection, manifold configuration, template design
- [ ] Study deepwater Angola subsea architectures (Block 17 CLOV layout, Block 32 Kaombo tieback)
- [ ] Learn project economics: CAPEX for subsea developments ($200M–$2B for [SURF](../glossary.md)+SPS)
- [ ] Build expertise in subsea boosting/processing (growing technology — [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) OneSubsea, Aker)

## Phase 4: Senior & Leadership (Year 5–12+)

### Goal
Lead subsea engineering teams, drive technology selection, manage multi-hundred-million-dollar subsea projects.

### Target Roles
- Senior Subsea Engineer
- Subsea Engineering Manager
- [SURF](../glossary.md) Manager / Project Director
- Subsea Technology Advisor (operator or vendor)

### Target Employers
- **Operators:** [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Eni/Azule, Chevron, ExxonMobil — subsea focal points in wells/facilities
- **EPIC Contractors:** [TechnipFMC](../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md), Subsea 7, Saipem — project execution leadership
- **Consultancies:** Xodus, 2H Offshore, Intecsea, Wood — subsea engineering advisory
- **Technology companies:** OneSubsea ([SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md)), Aker Solutions, [Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md) Subsea — R&D, product management

### Skills at This Level
- Subsea field development planning (concept selection, Architecture studies)
- Subsea decommissioning (growing need as Angola fields age)
- All-electric subsea technology and subsea processing adoption
- Contract strategy: EPCI vs. [EPC](../glossary.md) vs. alliance models
- Team leadership: managing 20–50+ engineers across disciplines

### Action Items
- [ ] Consider MSc in Subsea Engineering (Aberdeen, Cranfield, NTNU) or Offshore Engineering
- [ ] Pursue Chartered Engineer (CEng) or Professional Engineer (PE) status
- [ ] Publish papers on Angola subsea challenges (SPE, OTC, Subsea conferences)
- [ ] Build a consulting network for independent career options
- [ ] Mentor junior subsea engineers coming through the pipeline

## Angola-Specific Context

### Subsea Architecture in Angola
| Development | Operator | Architecture | Key Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Girassol** | [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) | Riser tower + FPSO | Pioneer deepwater development (2001) |
| **Dalia** | [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) | Trunklines + flexible risers | High flowrate capacity |
| **CLOV** | [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) | 4 field centers, 34 wells | Complex multi-field subsea layout |
| **Kaombo** | TotalEnergies | 2 FPSOs, 59 wells planned | Largest subsea development in Angola |
| **Kizomba A/B** | [ExxonMobil](../../data/company-directory/exxonmobil.md) | Subsea tieback to FPSOs | Mature fields, ongoing IRM |
| **Block 15/06 Agogo** | [Eni](../../data/company-directory/eni.md)/[Azule](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md) | Subsea tieback to FPSO N'Goma/Agogo | Fast-track development, new subsea infrastructure |

### Key Subsea Challenges in Angola
1. **Ultra-deepwater** — 1,500–2,500 m water depth, requiring specialized installation vessels
2. **Aging infrastructure** — Girassol subsea system (2001) approaching design life limits
3. **[Flow assurance](../skills/10-flow-assurance.md)** — Seabed temp ~3–4°C; aggressive hydrate/wax management required
4. **Long tieback distances** — Some satellite fields 15–30 km from [FPSO](../glossary.md)
5. **Intervention cost** — Subsea well intervention requires specialized vessels ($300k–$500k+/day)
6. **Decommissioning planning** — No major decommissioning done yet in Angola, but upcoming
