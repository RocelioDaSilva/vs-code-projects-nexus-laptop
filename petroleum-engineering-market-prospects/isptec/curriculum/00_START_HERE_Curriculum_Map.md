# ISPTEC Petroleum Engineering — Career Navigation & Curriculum Mapping

# 🛑 STOP: DO NOT PROCEED TO JOB ROLES UNTIL YOU HAVE COMPLETED THIS FILE-BY-FILE AUDIT.

---

## What Is This Repository?

This folder is an exhaustive career guide built **exclusively** for ISPTEC Licenciatura em Engenharia de Petróleos students. It maps every single subject in your curriculum to tangible career opportunities in Angola's oil and gas industry. The goal is simple: by the time you finish this audit, you will know exactly **why** you studied each subject and **which career path** it unlocks.

---

## Important Note on Research Methodology

While this repository provides a curated and structured foundation based on the ISPTEC syllabus, you are **strongly encouraged to verify and supplement** the information here with independent internet research. This includes:

- Checking current job postings on **LinkedIn** ("petroleum engineer Angola")
- Visiting official company career portals (e.g., [TotalEnergies Careers](https://careers.totalenergies.com), [SLB Careers](https://careers.slb.com), [Halliburton Careers](https://jobs.halliburton.com))
- Consulting Angola-specific industry news for the most up-to-date hiring trends, salary benchmarks, and internship application deadlines
- Reading SPE papers and technical journals (free with student SPE membership ~$15/year)

The industry evolves faster than any document. **Use this as your starting map, not your only source.**

## Objective of This Audit

The ISPTEC Petroleum Engineering curriculum is a toolbox. You cannot build a career until you know which tool solves which problem. This audit forces you to open each "Subject File" (linked below) and manually connect it to the Oil & Gas value chain.

## Instructions for File-by-File Analysis

1. **Open Your Transcript:** Have your list of completed/ongoing ISPTEC subjects ready.
2. **Navigate to `01_Curriculum_Subject_Files/`:** Inside this folder, you will find a separate `.md` file for **EVERY SINGLE SUBJECT** listed in your Licenciatura.
3. **Follow the 3-Step Process for EACH Subject:**
   - **Step A — Read the Subject Summary:** Open the file (e.g., `Mecanica_das_Rochas.md`). Read the 2-sentence summary of what you learned.
   - **Step B — Industry Mapping:** Read the section titled *"Where This Subject Is Used in Industry."*
   - **Step C — Personal Reflection (Mandatory):** In a notebook or digital document, write one sentence answering: *"If I were a [Reservoir/Drilling/Production] Engineer, how would I use the formula/concept from this subject tomorrow?"*
4. **Checklist:** Mark the checkbox next to each subject below once you have completed the reflection for that file.
