## What Is Midstream?

Midstream covers everything between the wellhead and the end consumer: transportation (pipelines, tankers), processing (gas treatment, LNG), and storage (tank farms, terminals). In Angola, midstream is smaller than upstream but growing — especially with gas monetization.

**The midstream value chain:**
1. **Oil Export** → Custody transfer from [FPSO](../../../docs/glossary.md) to tankers → shipping to refineries worldwide
2. **Gas Processing** → Offshore gas separation → pipeline to onshore processing → Angola LNG
3. **Pipeline Transport** → Subsea flowlines and export pipelines
4. **Storage & Terminals** → Onshore tank farms, LNG storage, loading terminals

## Career Paths in Midstream

### 1. Pipeline Engineer
**What they do:** Design, operate, and maintain pipeline systems — subsea flowlines, export pipelines, onshore gas pipelines. Pipeline integrity management, pigging, leak detection, and flow modeling.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Fenómenos de Transporte I (pipe flow, pressure drop)
- Escoamento de Petróleo (multiphase flow)
- Ciências dos Materiais e Corrosão (pipeline corrosion)
- Resistência dos Materiais (pipeline stress)
- Termodinâmica Aplicada (heat loss, insulation)

**Entry path:** [EPC](../../../docs/glossary.md) contractor ([TechnipFMC](../../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md), Saipem, Subsea7) or pipeline operator.

**Angola employers:** [TechnipFMC](../../../data/company-directory/technipfmc.md), [Saipem](../../../data/company-directory/saipem.md), [Subsea7](../../../data/company-directory/subsea7.md), [TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), Chevron

---

### 2. LNG / Gas Processing Engineer
**What they do:** Operate and optimize gas processing facilities — dehydration, acid gas removal, NGL extraction, liquefaction, storage, and loading.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Engenharia de Gás Natural (core)
- Termodinâmica Aplicada (core — refrigeration cycles, heat exchangers)
- Físico-Química (phase equilibria)
- Fenómenos de Transporte II (heat and mass transfer)
- Tecnologia de Refino (process engineering concepts)

**Entry path:** Angola LNG (Soyo) operations or engineering trainee. [Chevron](../../../data/company-directory/chevron.md) operates Angola LNG.

**Angola employers:** Angola LNG ([Chevron](../../../data/company-directory/chevron.md)-led), [Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) Gas Natural, EPC contractors

**Salary range:** $60,000–90,000 (entry) → $120,000–180,000 (senior)

---

### 3. Marine Logistics / Shipping Coordinator
**What they do:** Manage the logistics chain: supply vessels, tanker scheduling, helicopter operations, materials supply, customs/import processes.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Gerenciamento de Projectos (planning, scheduling)
- Empreendedorismo e Inovação (business operations)
- Inglês III/IV (constant English communication with vessels, agents)

**Entry path:** Operator logistics department ([TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md), [Eni](../../../data/company-directory/eni.md)) or logistics companies ([Petromar](../../../data/company-directory/petromar.md), Maersk Supply Service).

**Angola employers:** [Petromar](../../../data/company-directory/petromar.md), Maersk Supply Service, Total Logistics, [Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) Logística

---

### 4. Terminal Operations Engineer
**What they do:** Manage oil/gas storage terminals, loading/offloading operations, tank gauging, and custody transfer metering.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Optativa II — Instrumentação e Medição (metering, custody transfer)
- Sistemas de Produção de Superfície (process concepts)
- Engenharia de Segurança (terminal safety — fire protection, vapor control)

**Entry path:** [Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) Distribuidora, terminal operations at Luanda or Lobito.

**Angola employers:** Sonangol, international trading houses (Vitol, Trafigura — Angola offices)

## Angola's Midstream — Key Facts

| Asset | Description |
|---|---|
| **Angola LNG** | 5.2 mtpa LNG plant in Soyo, processing associated gas from offshore blocks |
| **Crude Export** | ~1.1 million bbl/d exported via [FPSO](../../../docs/glossary.md) offloading to shuttle/conventional tankers |
| **Luanda Terminal** | Oil storage and import terminal (refined products) |
| **Future Gas Master Plan** | Expanded domestic gas utilization — power generation, petrochemicals, fertilizer |

## Why Consider Midstream?

- **Less competitive entry** than upstream operator roles
- **Growing sector** — Angola's Gas Master Plan drives investment
- **Transferable skills** — pipeline, process, and project management skills work across sectors
- **Energy transition role** — gas is a transition fuel; LNG demand is growing globally
- **Entrepreneurship opportunity** — logistics, marine, and terminal services have local content potential

---

## How to Stand Out for Midstream Roles

1. **Gas knowledge:** Deep understanding of gas processing, LNG, and pipeline hydraulics
2. **Process simulation:** Learn HYSYS or Aspen Plus — standard tools for gas/process engineering
3. **Logistics experience:** Any experience coordinating operations, supply chain, or multi-party activities
4. **Safety training:** [BOSIET](../../../docs/glossary.md)/HUET certification for offshore work, NEBOSH for safety roles
