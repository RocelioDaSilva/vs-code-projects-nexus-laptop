## What Is Downstream?

Downstream transforms crude oil into products (gasoline, diesel, jet fuel, LPG, lubricants, petrochemicals) and delivers them to consumers. In Angola, downstream is underdeveloped — the country exports crude but imports refined products. This represents both a national challenge and a career opportunity.

**The downstream value chain:**
1. **Refining** → Crude oil → distillation, cracking, reforming → finished products
2. **Petrochemicals** → Naphtha, ethane → plastics, chemicals, fertilizers
3. **Distribution** → Tank farms, pipelines, road/rail tankers → retail stations
4. **Marketing** → Fuel retail, lubricant sales, commercial fuel supply

## Career Paths in Downstream

### 1. Refinery Process Engineer
**What they do:** Operate and optimize refinery units — atmospheric distillation, vacuum distillation, FCC, hydrocracker, reformer, HDS, blending.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Tecnologia de Refino (core)
- Físico-Química (phase equilibria, VLE)
- Termodinâmica Aplicada (heat exchangers, energy balance)
- Fenómenos de Transporte I & II (fluid mechanics, heat/mass transfer)
- Quimica Orgânica (hydrocarbon chemistry)

**Entry path:** Luanda Refinery ([Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md)), future Cabinda/Lobito refineries, or international refinery experience.

**Angola employers:** [Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) (Refinaria de Luanda), future refinery projects

**Note:** Refinery opportunities in Angola are currently limited to one facility. For broader experience, consider refinery internships in other countries (Portugal, Brazil, South Africa) before returning to Angola.

---

### 2. Chemical / Process Engineer
**What they do:** Design and optimize chemical processes — reaction kinetics, separation, heat integration, process simulation (HYSYS/Aspen Plus).

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Quimica Orgânica (hydrocarbon reactions)
- Físico-Química (kinetics, thermodynamics)
- Fenómenos de Transporte II (heat and mass transfer)
- Termodinâmica Aplicada (process energy)
- Tecnologia de Refino (unit operations)

**Entry path:** This role is broader than petroleum — chemical engineering skills apply to cement, food processing, water treatment, and other Angolan industries.

**Angola employers:** [Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md), Nova Cimangola (cement), SAB Miller (beverages), future petrochemical facilities

---

### 3. Quality Control / Laboratory Engineer
**What they do:** Test crude oil properties (API, sulfur, TAN, pour point, water content), monitor product quality (octane, cetane, sulfur, flash point), and ensure compliance with specifications.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Quimica Geral (laboratory techniques, analytical methods)
- Quimica Orgânica (hydrocarbon analysis)
- Físico-Química (measurement, phase behavior)
- Estatística Básica (QC statistics, measurement uncertainty)

**Entry path:** Refinery laboratory, crude oil assay laboratory, or inspection company (SGS, Intertek, Bureau Veritas).

**Angola employers:** Sonangol (refinery lab), SGS Angola, Intertek, Bureau Veritas

---

### 4. Distribution / Marketing Engineer
**What they do:** Manage fuel distribution networks — storage terminal operations, transport logistics, retail station operations, and commercial fuel sales.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Engenharia Económica (business analysis, NPV)
- Empreendedorismo e Inovação (business development)
- Engenharia de Segurança (fuel storage safety)
- Gerenciamento de Projectos (operations management)

**Entry path:** Sonangol Distribuidora, Pumangol, Total Marketing Angola.

**Angola employers:** Sonangol Distribuidora, Pumangol, Total Marketing Angola, Puma Energy

---

### 5. Lubricants / Specialty Products Engineer
**What they do:** Formulate, test, and market lubricants and specialty petroleum products. Technical advisor for industrial customers.

**Key ISPTEC subjects:**
- Quimica Orgânica (base oil chemistry)
- Ciências dos Materiais e Corrosão (wear, friction, tribology)
- Engenharia Económica (product economics)

**Entry path:** [TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) Lubrifiants, Shell Lubricants, or local distributor.

**Angola employers:** [TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) (lubricants), Shell, Mobil (via distributor)

## Angola's Downstream — Current State and Opportunities

### Current Situation
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Refineries** | 1 (Luanda Refinery — ~65,000 bbl/d capacity) |
| **Product imports** | Angola imports ~70% of refined product needs |
| **Fuel retail stations** | [Sonangol](../../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) Distribuidora and Pumangol dominate |
| **Planned refineries** | Cabinda Refinery, Lobito Refinery (under development) |

### Why This Matters for Your Career
Angola's downstream sector is set for massive investment:
- **New refineries** will create thousands of engineering jobs
- **Petrochemical projects** (fertilizer, methanol from natural gas) are in planning
- **Fuel distribution** modernization requires engineering and management talent
- **Import substitution** is a national priority — reducing fuel imports means building domestic capacity

### The Entrepreneurship Angle
Downstream has the most entrepreneurship potential of any sector:
- **Fuel distribution** — retail station ownership/operation
- **Lubricant distribution** — becoming a local distributor for major brands
- **Industrial chemicals** — supply chain for mining, construction, manufacturing
- **Laboratory services** — crude assay, product testing, environmental monitoring

---

## How to Stand Out for Downstream Roles

1. **Process simulation:** HYSYS (now Aspen HYSYS) or Aspen Plus fluency is essential
2. **Laboratory skills:** Hands-on analytical chemistry experience
3. **Business acumen:** Downstream is closer to the consumer — understanding economics, marketing, and supply chain matters more than in upstream
4. **International experience:** Consider a refinery internship in Portugal, Brazil, or South Africa to gain experience unavailable in Angola currently
5. **Portuguese + English:** Downstream in Angola uses more Portuguese (domestic market) than upstream, but English remains critical for international companies

---

## The Long View: Angola's Downstream Future

Angola's leadership has identified downstream development as a national priority. The country should not export crude cheaply and import expensive products. As new refineries come online, downstream career opportunities will expand significantly. Engineers who position themselves now — with refining knowledge, process simulation skills, and business understanding — will be ready when these opportunities emerge.
