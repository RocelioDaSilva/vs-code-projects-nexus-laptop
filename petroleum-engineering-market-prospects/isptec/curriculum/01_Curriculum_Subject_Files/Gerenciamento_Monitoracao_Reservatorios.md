## What You Learned

## Where This Subject Is Used in Industry

- **Production Optimization:** Monitoring each well's performance (rate, watercut, GOR, pressure) and taking action to maximize field recovery. Closing watering-out zones, adjusting injection rates, optimizing ESP speeds, and planning infill wells.

- **Infill Well Planning:** Using surveillance data to identify remaining oil targets for infill wells. Where is bypassed oil? Where is remaining pressure support?
