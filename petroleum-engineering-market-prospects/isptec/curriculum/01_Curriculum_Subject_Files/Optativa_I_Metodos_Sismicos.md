## What You Learned

Seismic data acquisition (marine streamer, OBC, OBN), seismic processing (stacking, migration, velocity analysis), seismic interpretation (horizons, faults, attributes), and the use of seismic data in exploration and development.

## Where This Subject Is Used in Industry

- **Exploration - Prospect Identification:** Seismic data is the primary tool for finding oil and gas. Interpreters map structural and stratigraphic traps, identify direct hydrocarbon indicators (bright spots, flat spots, AVO anomalies), and define drilling targets.
- **Reservoir Delineation:** Development seismic (high-resolution 3D) defines reservoir geometry, fault patterns, and connectivity - inputs to the geological model and simulation.
- **4D Seismic (Time-Lapse):** Repeated 3D seismic surveys over time show changes in fluid saturation and pressure - revealing drained vs. undrained zones, water injection front movement, and bypassed oil. This is increasingly used in Angola.
- **Seismic-to-Well Tie:** Connecting seismic reflections to geological formations identified in wells - using check-shot surveys and synthetic seismograms - is a fundamental geoscience workflow.

## Angola-Specific Context

Angola has one of the most extensively surveyed continental margins in Africa. PGS, CGG, and TGS hold massive multiclient 3D seismic libraries covering Blocks 15-32 and beyond. [TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) has shot 4D seismic over Girassol and Dalia to monitor field performance. Geophysicist is a viable career path in Angola - working at operators or seismic companies.
