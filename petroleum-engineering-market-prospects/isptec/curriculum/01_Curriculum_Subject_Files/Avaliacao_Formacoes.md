## What You Learned

Wireline and [LWD](../../../docs/glossary.md) logging: gamma ray, resistivity (laterolog, induction), neutron, density, sonic, NMR, and formation testing (RFT/MDT). Log interpretation: lithology identification, porosity calculation, water saturation (Archie equation), permeability estimation, and net pay determination.

## Where This Subject Is Used in Industry

- **Petrophysics:** The petrophysicist interprets well logs to determine: what rock is present (lithology), how much pore space exists (porosity), what fraction is oil vs. water (saturation), and whether the rock can flow (permeability). These are the fundamental reservoir properties.
- **Archie Equation:** The most important equation in petrophysics: $S_w = \left(\frac{a \cdot R_w}{\phi^m \cdot R_t}\right)^{1/n}$. It converts resistivity measurements into water saturation Гўв‚¬вЂќ telling you how much oil is in the rock.
- **Net Pay Determination:** Not all reservoir rock is productive. The petrophysicist applies cutoffs (porosity > 10%, saturation < 60%, permeability > 1 mD) to distinguish net pay from gross interval. Net pay goes into the STOIIP calculation.
- **Formation Pressure Testing:** MDT/RFT tools measure pressure at multiple depths Гўв‚¬вЂќ identifying fluid contacts (OWC, GOC), pressure gradients (fluid density), and reservoir connectivity.

## Angola-Specific Context

Every well drilled in Angola runs a comprehensive logging program: typically GR, resistivity, neutron-density, sonic, and MDT at minimum. Petrophysics is the bridge between raw measurements and reservoir properties. [SLB](../../../data/company-directory/slb.md) ([Schlumberger](../../../data/company-directory/slb.md)) and Halliburton are the dominant logging service providers in Angola. Petrophysicist is a specialized career path with excellent long-term prospects.
