## What You Learned

Intermediate English: technical reading comprehension, paragraph writing, presentations, and expanded vocabulary including semi-technical terms. You progressed from basic communication to understanding structured texts.

## Where This Subject Is Used in Industry

- **Reading SPE Papers:** The Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE) publishes thousands of technical papers annually â€” all in English. These papers describe real field cases, new technologies, and best practices. A Reservoir Engineer studying waterflooding in Angola turbidites will read SPE papers from Block 17. If you cannot read these fluently, you cannot stay current.
- **Equipment Manuals:** [SLB](../../../data/company-directory/slb.md), [Halliburton](../../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md), and [Baker Hughes](../../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md) tools come with English-language manuals. An MWD engineer who cannot read the tool operating manual cannot troubleshoot in the field.
- **Email Communication:** Daily emails with service company engineers, operator representatives, and shore-based support teams are in English. Miscommunication in an email about mud weight or casing depth could cause a costly mistake.
- **Safety Communications:** Safety alerts, lessons learned bulletins, and industry incident reports (from IOGP, IADC, UK HSE) are published in English.

## Angola-Specific Context

In Angola, Portuguese is the national language, but international operators conduct technical business in English. [TotalEnergies](../../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) uses English for all technical documentation on its Angola operations (French is not used offshore). At [SLB](../../../data/company-directory/slb.md), [Halliburton](../../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md), and [Baker Hughes](../../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md), your interview will be in English. Your technical discussions with international colleagues will be in English. English II builds the bridge from conversational to professional English.
