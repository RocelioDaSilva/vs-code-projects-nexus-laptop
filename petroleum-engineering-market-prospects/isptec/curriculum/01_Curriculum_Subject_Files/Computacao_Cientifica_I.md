## What You Learned

Spreadsheet tools (Excel/Google Sheets), basic programming concepts (VBA or similar), data manipulation, formula-based calculations, chart creation, and introduction to computational problem-solving. You learned to use a computer as an engineering tool rather than just a word processor.
