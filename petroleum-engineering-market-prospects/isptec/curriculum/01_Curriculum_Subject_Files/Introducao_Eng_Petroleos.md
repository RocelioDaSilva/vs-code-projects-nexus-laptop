## What You Learned

A broad survey of the entire oil and gas industry: exploration, drilling, production, transportation, refining, and marketing. You learned the lifecycle of a petroleum project from discovery to abandonment, basic reservoir concepts, drilling methods, production systems, and the economic framework of the industry.

## Angola-Specific Context
