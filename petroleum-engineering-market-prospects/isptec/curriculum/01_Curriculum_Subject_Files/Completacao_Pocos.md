## What You Learned

Completion types (open hole, cased hole, slotted liner, gravel pack, frac-pack), completion equipment (tubing, packers, safety valves, gauges, inflow control devices, sliding sleeves), sand control methods, intelligent completions, and subsea christmas trees.

## Where This Subject Is Used in Industry

- **Completion Design:** After drilling the well, the completion connects the reservoir to surface. Decisions: open hole vs. cased hole screen vs. gravel pack vs. frac-pack; single zone vs. multi-zone; natural flow vs. ESP; tubing size. Each decision affects well productivity, cost, and long-term operability.
- **Sand Control:** Angola's poorly consolidated turbidite sandstones produce sand if not controlled. Sand control completions (gravel packs, screens, frac-packs) prevent sand production while maintaining productivity. This is a critical issue for Angola wells.
- **Intelligent Completions:** Multi-zone intelligent completions with downhole flow control valves and permanent gauges allow optimizing production from multiple zones without intervention. Used increasingly in Angola for managing water/gas breakthrough.
- **Subsea Trees:** Angola's subsea wells are completed with subsea christmas trees (wet trees) Гўв‚¬вЂќ the interface between the well and the subsea flowline system. Tree selection and design is part of completion engineering.
