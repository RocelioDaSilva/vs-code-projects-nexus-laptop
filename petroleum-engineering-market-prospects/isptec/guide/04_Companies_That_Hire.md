# Companies That Hire ISPTEC Graduates

> This is not a generic list. Every company listed here has active operations in Angola and a history of hiring Angolan petroleum engineering graduates. For each company, you get: what they actually do, what entry-level looks like, what programs exist, and exactly how to apply.

---

## The Hiring Logic: Why Your Degree Is Sought After

Angola's Presidential Decree 271/20 (Local Content Regime) requires all operators and service companies to meet **Angolan employment ratios**. The minimum target for technical/engineering staff is approximately **70% Angolan** for operational roles, rising year-on-year.

This is enforced. ANPG audits it. Operators who fail face fines and contract penalties.

**This means:** You are not competing against the world. You are competing against a small pool of Angolan petroleum engineering graduates — primarily ISPTEC, UNIA, and Universidade Agostinho Neto graduates — for roles that companies are legally required to fill with Angolans.

Use this advantage.

## TIER 2 — SERVICE COMPANIES (Primary Target for Fresh Graduates)

### 1. SLB (Schlumberger)

**What they do in Angola:** Drilling services, [MWD](../../docs/glossary.md)/LWD, [drilling fluids](../../docs/skills/03-drilling-fluids.md) (M-I SWACO), completions, cementing, well testing, reservoir characterization, wireline logging, production technologies.

**Why start here:** [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) has the most structured graduate training program in the industry. Their Field Engineer Trainee (FET) curriculum runs 2–3 years and systematically builds your technical competence. Engineers trained at [SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) are hired everywhere. Former SLB engineers have a premium on the market.

**Angola presence:** SLB Angola operates from Luanda. Active on Block 17, 32, 15, and others. Large Angola office with technical and training infrastructure.

**Entry-level roles:**
- **Field Engineer Trainee (FET)** — 0–1 years experience, all disciplines
- **Mud Logger** — entry level, [formation evaluation](../../docs/skills/04-formation-evaluation.md)
- **Field Operations Coordinator** — logistics support

**How to apply:**
1. Visit **careers.slb.com** → filter by "Angola" or "Luanda"
2. LinkedIn: follow SLB Angola page, connect with local HR/recruiters
3. SPE Angola events — SLB always has a recruiting presence
4. Cold email to: angola.careers@slb.com (verify current address on their careers page)

**What they look for:** English proficiency, [BOSIET](../../docs/glossary.md)/HUET (or willingness to get it within 30 days), strong academics in drilling or reservoir subjects, demonstrated safety mindset.

**Company profile:** [data/company-directory/slb.md](../../data/company-directory/slb.md)

### 2. Halliburton

**What they do in Angola:** Cementing (Landmark), completion tools, production enhancement, [directional drilling](../../docs/skills/05-directional-drilling.md) (Sperry), logging, well testing, consulting/software (Landmark WellPlan, COMPASS).

**Angola presence:** [Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md) Angola office in Luanda. Active on most major blocks. The cementing division is particularly strong — Angola's deepwater wells require large-volume, high-pressure cement jobs.

**Entry-level roles:**
- **Cementing Operator / Trainee Engineer** — mixes and pumps cement downhole
- **[Directional Drilling](../../docs/skills/05-directional-drilling.md) Field Specialist** — Sperry Drilling division
- **Production Solutions Trainee** — stimulation and completions

**Key program:** The **LEAD (Leadership and Education for Accelerated Development)** program for graduates. Structured 18-month rotational program.

**How to apply:**
1. **jobs.[halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md).com** → search "Angola" or "Luanda"
2. LinkedIn: message Halliburton Angola HR directly. Subject line: "LEAD Program Application — ISPTEC Petroleum Engineering Graduate"
3. Ask your ISPTEC professors — some have Halliburton contacts from previous students

**Company profile:** [data/company-directory/halliburton.md](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md)

## TIER 1 — OPERATORS (Graduate Programs & Direct Entry)

### 1. TotalEnergies Angola

**What they do:** Operate Blocks 17, 32, 20/11 — Angola's most productive blocks. 6 FPSOs. 140+ producing wells. The largest single employer of petroleum engineers in Angola.

**Entry-level roles:**
- **Young Graduate Program** (Programme Jeunes Diplômés) — 18-month structured program, competitive
- **Internship / Estágio** — annual intake, often converts to full-time
- **Drilling Engineer** — for experienced engineers (2+ years)

**Why [TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md):** World-class training, best-in-class technical mentorship, exposure to the most complex deepwater operations in Africa. If you get into TotalEnergies Angola as a graduate, you are in the top 1% of Angolan petroleum engineering careers.

**How to apply:**
1. **careers.[totalenergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md).com** → Angola
2. Network with TotalEnergies Angola employees on LinkedIn
3. The Young Graduate Program opens annually — apply in Q3/Q4 of each year
4. Contact ISPTEC career office — TotalEnergies sometimes recruits directly from campus

**What they look for:** Strong GPA, English proficiency (French is a bonus), [BOSIET](../../docs/glossary.md)/HUET, technical interview performance, personal drive.

**Company profile:** [data/company-directory/totalenergies.md](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md)

### 2. Azule Energy (BP + ENI Joint Venture)

**What they do:** [Azule Energy](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md) is the largest independent oil company in Angola, formed from the merger of BP Angola and ENI's Angolan assets. They operate Block 18 (Greater Plutonio [FPSO](../../docs/glossary.md)), Block 15/06 (N'Goma [FPSO](../../docs/glossary.md)), and are developing the Agogo field (Blocks 20/11 and 21/09).

**Why [Azule](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md) for graduates:** They hire **~150 positions/year** (stated publicly). They have a **40% female hiring target**. They're newer and more growth-oriented than the established operators — which means more openings and faster progression.

**Entry-level roles:**
- **Professional Internship Program** — 12-month paid program, 150 positions
- **Graduate Engineer** — reservoir, drilling, production, subsea
- **HSE Officer**

**How to apply:**
1. **[azule](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md)-energy.com/careers**
2. LinkedIn: [Azule Energy](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md) Angola recruiters
3. The internship program is the primary entry point — apply 6 months before graduation

**Company profile:** [data/company-directory/azule-energy.md](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md)

### 3. Chevron (CABGOC)

**What they do:** [Chevron](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md)'s Angola operations (Cabinda Gulf Oil Company, [CABGOC](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md)) operate Block 0 and Block 14. Block 0 is Angola's oldest producing concession — onshore Cabinda and shallow water. Block 14 is deepwater.

**Angola history:** [CABGOC](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md) has operated in Angola since 1955. They have the deepest roots of any international operator in Angola. Their relationship with the Cabinda province and [Sonangol](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) is unmatched.

**Entry-level roles:**
- **Graduate Internship Program** — annual intake
- **Drilling Engineer** — for technically strong candidates
- **Production Technologist**

**How to apply:**
1. **[chevron](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md).com/careers** → Angola
2. Network in Cabinda (if you're from the region, the local connection is valuable)
3. Apply through [Sonangol](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md)-related channels — CABGOC has a local HR team in Luanda

**Company profile:** [data/company-directory/chevron.md](../../data/company-directory/chevron.md)

### 4. Sonangol E.P.

**What they do:** Sonangol is Angola's National Oil Company. It holds partner stakes in every Angolan oil block. Sonangol E.P. is the exploration and production arm.

**Why Sonangol:** The most stable employer in Angola's oil sector. Not the highest salary (USD salaries are often lower than international counterparts), but unmatched job security, benefits, and local career progression. Sonangol also invests in employee training (international scholarships, overseas assignments).

**Entry-level roles:**
- **Petroleum Engineer (Reservoir, Drilling, Production)**
- **Annual Graduate Intake** — largest single batch of graduates hired in Angola's oil sector
- **Sonangol Scholarship Program** — funded post-graduate education abroad

**How to apply:**
1. **sonangol.co.ao** → career-deep-dives/emprego
2. ISPTEC career office — Sonangol recruits directly from campus annually
3. Sonangol annual graduate day events at ISPTEC

**Company profile:** [data/company-directory/sonangol.md](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md)

## The Application Hierarchy (Priority Order)

If you have your [BOSIET](../../docs/glossary.md) + Offshore Medical + IWCF Level 1, apply in this order:

1. **[SLB](../../data/company-directory/slb.md) Field Engineer Trainee** — highest training quality, global reputation, accepts fresh graduates
2. **[Azule Energy](../../data/company-directory/azule-energy.md) Professional Internship** — direct Tier 1 entry, 150 positions, best odds of any operator
3. **[Halliburton](../../data/company-directory/halliburton.md) LEAD Program** — strong Angola presence, structured program
4. **[Baker Hughes](../../data/company-directory/bakerhughes.md) Emerging Talent** — completions and [artificial lift](../../docs/skills/09-artificial-lift.md) focus
5. **[TotalEnergies](../../data/company-directory/totalenergies.md) Young Graduate** — most competitive, but worth applying every year
6. **[Sonangol](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) Graduate Intake** — most stable, slowest progression, best for long-term Angola career
7. **[SONILS](../../data/company-directory/sonangol.md) / Tier 3** — fallback, but still valuable. Start here rather than waiting.

**Apply to multiple at once.** This is not a single-application process.
