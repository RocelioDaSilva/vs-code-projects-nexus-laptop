## 🔗 Cross-Arc Connections

### Themes Across Acts
- **Act I → Act II:** Mentorship becomes infiltration (knowledge becomes power)
- **Act II → Act III:** Networks become force (organization becomes action)
- **Act I → Act III:** Individual growth becomes institutional change

### Character Continuity by Arc
- **Aisen:** Protagonist all arcs; evolves from student → operative → sacrifice
- **Harald & Tarovin:** Mentors in Act I; referenced/sacrificed in Act III
- **Academy Allies:** Introduced Act II; active participants Act III
- **Antagonists:** Mysterious (Caspian, Seraphine) → revealed (Covenant) → confronted (final)

### System Progression by Arc
- **Act I:** Learning individual systems (combat, magic)
- **Act II:** Understanding institutional systems (Academy, Covenant)
- **Act III:** Fighting systemic corruption (institutional change)
