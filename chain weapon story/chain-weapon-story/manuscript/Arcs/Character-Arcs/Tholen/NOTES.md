# Master Tholen — Character Notes

Role: Archivist / quiet ally — custodian of erased records and pragmatic conspirator.

Arc summary:
- Act I: Keeps quiet records; provides Aisen with the first hint that history is curated.
- Act II: Supplies fragments and marginalia that become operational tools for the network.
- Act III: Works to disseminate truth cautiously; risk increases and he pays with exposure and loss of position (but his materials survive).

Motivations:
- Preserve truth and make it useful without needlessly endangering sources.

Key beats (what happens):
- Gives Aisen a folio of pre-Covenant records that reframes the Covenant's legitimacy.
- Becomes a node for safe dissemination; takes risks that endanger his post but seed the network with durable facts.

Relationships:
- Aisen, Tarovin: intellectual allies.

POV pairing suggestions:
- Pair with Aisen in discovery scenes and with Amara/archivist moments to show dissemination choices.

Voice/Notes:
- Scholarly, careful, morally weighty in small acts. Avoid melodrama; his heroism is archival and procedural.
