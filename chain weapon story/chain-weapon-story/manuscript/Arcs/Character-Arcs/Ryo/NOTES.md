- Act II: Encounters that test his two-selves problem; tactical value to the resistance grows.
- Act III: The Chasm Vision (full-sequence chapter) — crisis and synthesis; emerges more coherent and committed.

Motivations:

- Resolves identity tension enough to act as a focused combat leader during the final confrontations.

Relationships:
