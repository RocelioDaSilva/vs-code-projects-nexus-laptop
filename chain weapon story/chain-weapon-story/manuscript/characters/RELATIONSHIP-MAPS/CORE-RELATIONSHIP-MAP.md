## Relationship Evolution by Act

### Act I (Ch 1-13): Formation
- Aisen meets Harald (mentor #1) → learns physical combat
- Aisen meets Tarovin (mentor #2) → learns telekinesis
- Aisen meets Elara → childhood bond; royal identity concealed
- Aisen observes Caspian → first recognition of "something wrong"

### Act II (Ch 14-44): Network Building
- Aisen + Elara become co-leaders
- Rin recruited (spy network integrates)
- Amara recruited (merchant intelligence)
- Kaelen recruited (synthesis magic)
- Ccoya recruited (cultural bridge)
- Pieter recruited (finance/logistics)
- Caspian encounters intensify → mutual recognition grows
- Seraphine recognizes Aisen as genuine threat

### Act III (Ch 45-85): War, Death & Legacy
- Harald dies → teaching legacy validated
- Tarovin dies → sacrifice costs real
- Aisen dies (Ch 70) → network continues without him
- Caspian freed (Ch 81) → chooses own path
- Elara steps into political leadership
- Network reconfigures → distributed leadership proven

## Writing Checks

Before writing a scene with two+ characters:
1. Check this map for their relationship type
2. Note the current act's relationship state
3. Ensure dialogue reflects the specific dynamic (not generic alliance)
4. Reference [CHARACTER-VOICE-BANK.md](CHARACTER-VOICE-BANK.md) for how each speaks
