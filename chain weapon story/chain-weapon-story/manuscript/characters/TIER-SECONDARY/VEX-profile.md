# Vex

## Role
Systems Reader and structural analyst. Deciphers complex organizational patterns, dependencies, and vulnerabilities in institutional systems.

## Physical Description
Observant bearing, minimal adornment, clothing chosen for practicality and anonymity. Moves through crowds without being remembered.

## Key Attributes
- **Strength:** Can analyze any system (institutional, magical, military, economic) and identify critical failure points.
- **Limitation:** Struggles with human emotion. Relationships are mechanisms, not connections. Cannot lead directly, only advise.
- **Core Conflict:** Sees patterns that demand intervention, but lacks the charisma or authority to drive change independently.

## First Appearance
Chapter 17B: "The Survey" (analyzing Academy structure)

## Key Scenes
- Revealing the Covenant's organizational fragility
- Mapping the network's architecture and its replication of institutional hierarchy
- Guiding demolition of corrupted systems

## Character Arc
- **Beginning:** Neutral analyst; serves whoever pays for insight
- **Midpoint:** Realizes their analysis enables atrocities; becomes complicit
- **Climax:** Must choose between continuing analysis or becoming an architect of change
- **Resolution:** Becomes designer of post-war institutional frameworks; applies pattern-breaking principles

## Voice Example
"Every system contains the seeds of its own failure. It's never a question of whether it will collapse, only when, how quickly, and who suffers most during the transition."

## Relationships
- **Aisen:** Intellectual partnership; Vex's analysis supports Aisen's strategies
- **Seraphine:** Adversaries; Seraphine grasps pattern-reading but lacks Vex's objectivity
- **Post-War Governance:** Vex becomes indispensable to institutional redesign
