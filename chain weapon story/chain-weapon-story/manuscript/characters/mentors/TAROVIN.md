# Tarovin (Cleric-Scholar)

**Role:** Wandering cleric, telekinesis teacher

## Demographics
- Early 40s during Aisen's childhood; nondescript, warm presence

## Teaching Method & Drills
- Teaches telekinesis through barter (food/drink)
- Emphasizes precision and constraint over power
- Recommended drills: Candle Nudge, Feather Flick, Rotational Drill, Multi-Tether Warmup

## Impact
- First formal magic teacher for Aisen; establishes low-power/high-precision philosophy

## Story Arc
- Itinerant teacher: Trades practical lessons for food and shelter, shaping Aisen's discipline.
- Moral guide: Demonstrates that precision and constraint are virtues, not weakness.
- Living tradition: Moves on but leaves a pragmatic teaching lineage that resurfaces in Aisen's methods.
