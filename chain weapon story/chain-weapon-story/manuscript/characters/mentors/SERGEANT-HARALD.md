# Sergeant Harald (The First Mentor)

**Role:** Aisen's first mentor; guard-turned-teacher

## Demographics
- Early 60s when he meets Aisen; dies ~70
- Barrel-chested, missing two fingers, walks with a limp

## Teaching Method & Influence
- Emphasizes leverage over strength; reading opponents by stance and breathing
- Drills: sliding thrust, pivot step, retreating thrusts, planted-anchor drills
- Inspiration for chain-spear techniques; provides letter to Academy

## Impact
- Dies of old age before the war; his training and letter shape Aisen's path

## Story Arc
- Recognition: Sees raw potential in a young Aisen and chooses to train him.
- Mentor: Teaches practical spear drills and physics-minded approaches rather than glory.
- Legacy: Dies before the war, but his methods and letter send Aisen to the Academy and echo in later curriculum.
