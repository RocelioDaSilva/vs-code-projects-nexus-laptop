# Master Wei (The Celestial Scholar)

**Role:** Celestial Hall professor; theoretical grounding for Aisen

## Demographics
- Late 60s in story; thin, ink-stained hands

## Teaching & Impact
- Provides theoretical grounding for telekinesis through physics and geomancy
- Defends Aisen after Frostfang incident; writes treatise on his methods after Aisen's death
