## Magical Approach

### Philosophy: Precision Through Understanding

**Core principle**: "Telekinesis is physics embodied in will; understand the physics, limits become clear"

### Teaching Framework

**Levels of telekinesis instruction**:

| Level | Concept | Goal | Risk |
|-------|---------|------|------|
| **Level 1** | Direct push/pull | Understand force application | Over-exertion |
| **Level 2** | Torque calculations | Maximize efficiency | Precision errors |
| **Level 3** | Multi-point control** | Divided attention mastery | Mana exhaustion |
| **Level 4** | Sustained holding | Energy management | Magical corruption |
| **Level 5** | Breaking limits** | Transcending normal capacity | PERMANENT COST |

**Level 5 warning**: Each breaking of limits costs the mage permanently
- Telekinesis → nerve damage, tremor, reduced capacity
- Other magics → different costs (aging, emotional loss, reality distortion)
- Covenant exploits this: Forces heroes into limit-breaking situations
- Prevention is better than heroic sacrifice

### Magical Capabilities

| Ability | Description | Limitation |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| **Telekinesis mastery** | Can teach precise control; demonstrate complex techniques | Own tremor limits his current practice |
| **Mana sense** | Feel magical energy in others; diagnose magical injuries | Cannot cure what he damaged in himself |
| **Protective wards** | Can create temporary magical barriers (weakened by his condition) | Takes time; cannot sustain indefinitely |
| **Healing touch** | Accelerate body's natural recovery (physical only; not magical) | Cannot reverse limit-breaking costs |
| **Lecture hall magic** | Demonstrate techniques at scale for classroom instruction | Drains him quickly; recovery is slow |

- **Method**: Explains through principles, not mystique
- **Impact**: Aisen understands; decides he wants to learn properly
- **Callback**: Aisen refers to this scene in later teaching of others

### Warning About Limits (Chapter 8-9, Ages 12-13 for Aisen)
- **Setting**: Private conversation during training
- **Moment**: Tarovin shows left arm scar; explains the cost
- **Content**: "I broke limits. I gained power. I paid being diminished. The Covenant banks on you being willing to pay that cost. Don't be."
