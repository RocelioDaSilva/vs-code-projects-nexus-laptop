# Aisen (Protagonist)

**Source:** Extracted from CHARACTER-BIBLE.md

## Demographics & Physical Description

- Name: Aisen (no surname; bastard child of a tavern-keeper)
- Age Range: 8–23 in main story
- Height: Average (5'9"−5'11"), wiry, lean muscle
- Face: Plain, strong jaw, deep-set eyes; broken nose (never set); small scar on left cheek
- Hands: Calloused, crossed with fine scars between fingers; crooked fingers from an old break
- Hair: Dark, kept short; early gray streaks
- Posture: Slightly forward, always watching footing
- Distinctive Markers: Worn practical clothing; light armor with fail-points; chain link on cord around wrist (his design)

---

## Personality & Worldview

- Core Philosophy: "Small edges, accumulated, win fights." / "A person's worth is in their choices, not their birth."
- Strengths: Observant, adaptable, pragmatic, humble, curious about causality
- Flaws: Skeptical to cynicism, slow to bond, holds nothing tightly, carries guilt, avoids confrontation
- Habits: Taps a chain link when thinking; hums hunting tunes; keeps a journal; sleeps lightly; reads physics and magic theory

---

## Character Arc (Ghost, Lie, Want, Need, Truth)

- Ghost: Grew up watching the world reward birth, not effort. Father died while he was away.
- Lie: "I have to become someone important/legendary to matter."
- Want (External): Earn respect, help family, become skilled enough to never be helpless
- Need (Internal): Realize worth is in choices and who he protects
- Truth: "A commoner can matter by standing up for what's right, even if the world never sings songs about it."

## Skills & Training Path

**Spear Mastery (Harald)**
- 100+ techniques across offensive, defensive, mobility, construct, and trap categories
- Emphasis on torque, leverage, and momentum; positioning and pacing

**Telekinesis (Tarovin)**
- Low-power, high-precision approach
- Core techniques: direct push/pull, torque application, multi-point control, kinetic redirection, pressure concentration, sustained hold
- Costs and consequences: nerve damage, permanent mana capacity reduction, accelerated aging, permanent trait loss

**Hunting & Survival**
- Tracking, trapping, ecology, patience and timing

**Physics & Strategic Thinking**
- Thinks in systems and chains of causality
