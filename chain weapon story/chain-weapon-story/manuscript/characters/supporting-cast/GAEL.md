# Gael (Heartland Quartermaster)

**Role:** Quartermaster who documents corruption; dies before testimony

## Traits
- Weary, meticulous; keeps records that later expose Academy rot
- Dies before testifying but records become evidence

## Story Arc
- Record-keeper: Quietly compiles evidence of corruption despite personal risk.
- Martyr: His death elevates the importance of the records he kept.
- Catalyst: Posthumous evidence propels investigations and institutional accountability.
