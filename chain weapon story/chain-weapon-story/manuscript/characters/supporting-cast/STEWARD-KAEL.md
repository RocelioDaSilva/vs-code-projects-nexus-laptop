# Steward Kael

**Role:** Prince's steward; pragmatic and tired

## Function
- Represents cost of noble privilege; small moral choices that reveal palace rot

## Story Arc
- Administrator: Manages palace logistics and sees how corruption is institutionalized.
- Moral pivot: Faces a choice between career safety and exposing truth; may leak documents or testify.
- Consequence: His actions (or silence) shape how the palace is held accountable after the crisis.
