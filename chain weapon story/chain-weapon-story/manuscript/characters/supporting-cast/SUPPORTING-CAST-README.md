## Documentation To Add

When developing supporting character details:
- [ ] Complete profiles for 15+ named secondary characters
- [ ] Relationship matrix (who knows whom, how well)
- [ ] Voice/dialogue samples (3 lines per character minimum)
- [ ] Arc summary (start → change → end for each)
- [ ] Appearance consistency checklist (physical descriptions stable?)
- [ ] Knowledge state tracking (what do they know when?)
- [ ] Scene importance ranking (which supporting character moments matter most?)

## Supporting Character Impact

Supporting characters should:
- **Provide perspective** on Aisen's choices and growth
- **Create obstacles** or aid progress meaningfully
- **Show systemic effects** (how do ordinary people experience the story?)
- **Develop own arcs** (not just be passive to protagonist)
- **Honor relationships** (friendship/mentorship shown through action)
- **Enable growth** (challenges or support protagonist's development)

## Key Questions Per Character

When fleshing out supporting cast:
- [ ] Why does this character matter to the story?
- [ ] What do they want (separate from protagonist's wants)?
- [ ] How do they change by end of narrative?
- [ ] What sacrifice or choice defines them?
- [ ] Are they memorable? (What's one thing reader remembers about them?)
- [ ] Do they have dignity even if minor? (Not just tools)
