**Role:** Textbook mage; foil to Aisen

## Traits
- Perfect technique, becomes instructor; never a field mage; represents institutional success without lived experience

## Story Arc
- Prodigy: Excels in academic settings and becomes a respected instructor.
- Static: Her lack of field experience limits understanding of practical costs.
- Example: Serves as a foil to Aisen, showing the limits of textbook knowledge in wartime.
