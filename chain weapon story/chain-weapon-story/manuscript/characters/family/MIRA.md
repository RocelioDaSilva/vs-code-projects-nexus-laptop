# Mira (Aisen's Sister)

**Role:** Tavern keeper, emotional anchor

## Demographics
- Age: ~5 years older than Aisen
- Stocky, strong arms; gap-toothed smile; wears chain link on cord (Aisen's design)

## Background & Dynamics
- Stayed home running the tavern while Aisen left for Academy
- Resents his absence but loves him; places memorial plaque after his death
- The tavern becomes a pilgrimage site; she keeps his story alive

## Arc
- Tavern helper → resentful → grieving memorial carrier → keeper of legacy
