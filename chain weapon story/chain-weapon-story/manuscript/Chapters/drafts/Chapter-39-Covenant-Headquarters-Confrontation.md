Infiltration began at twilight. Teams moved in through sewer access and market blinds while Kaelen and others provided cover with ecological disruptions—false blooms and misdirected patrols. The headquarters rose like an ice cathedral, hostile and deliberate. 

Combat was layered: silent takedowns inside, pitched fights in courtyards, and Aisen pushing the chain-spear grammar into architecture—anchoring segments to chandeliers, redirecting guard formations, and creating temporary bridges out of tensioned links. Seraphine loomed like winter, and Caspian fought a private battle beneath her gaze. 

The scene emphasized the mechanics: state-shifting chains, telekinetic micro-impulses, and the choreography of ordinary people operating as system actors. The tide turned in small moments: a single anchor point released, a column collapsed enough to block reinforcements, a message delivered just in time. 

By the chapter's end, the battle had set the stage for the final confrontation—bodies lined, choices made, and a network preparing for sacrifice.
