In a windowless hall the Covenant's council convened. Faces in glass frowned at the leak reports. Their response: crush the network's nodes before information could become a movement. Purges would be surgical and public trials staged to reassert control. 

Orders rippled through institutions: arrests at the academy, simultaneous raids on suspected safe houses, and a propaganda push claiming false conspiracies. The council calculated the optics and accepted short-term outrage for longer-term authority. 

On the ground, officers moved with a new urgency. The resistance braced. The counter-strike would force decisions—hide, flee, or fight. The Covenant assumed control through fear, but their brutality also revealed their vulnerability. Violence answered discovery, and the world watched to see which truth would hold.
