\chapter{Aldric: The Mentor}

Aldric’s teaching was not formalized. There were no lessons, no progression, no structure.
Instead, Aldric brought Aisen into his life and showed him how to exist as a Flux-user in a
world that wanted to kill Flux-users.

\textbf{The First Rule:} \textit{Observe before acting.}

Aldric would take Aisen to the marketplace and point out small things: how the soldiers moved
in patterns, how the nobles touched their servants differently than their peers, how information
flowed from certain people and was suppressed from others.
