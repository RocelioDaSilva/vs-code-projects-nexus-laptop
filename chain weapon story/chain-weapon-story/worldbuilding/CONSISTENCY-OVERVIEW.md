## Consistency Verification Areas

### 1. Timeline & Age Consistency
- Aisen ages: 12 → 14 (Act I) → 24 (Act II) → 24-25 (Act III)
- Year markings per chapter and major events
- Seasonal/temporal markers for clarity

### 2. Character Consistency
- Voice patterns (dialogue, internal monologue)
- Knowledge progression (what do they know when?)
- Ability progression (power development logical?)
- Relationship continuity (allies stay allies unless plotted)

### 3. Magic System Consistency
- First introduction of each system clear?
- Limitations established before major use?
- Costs shown consistently?
- No power suddenly doing something new without explanation?

### 4. World Consistency
- Geography details stable across mentions?
- Institution structures consistent?
- Cultural practices uniform?
- Flora/fauna descriptions match?

### 5. Narrative Consistency
- Character motivations make sense throughout?
- Plot setup and payoff aligned?
- Foreshadowing legitimate in retrospect?
- Tone matches genre expectations?

## Quick Verification Checklist

**Before final submission:**
- [ ] No character ages impossibly fast/slow
- [ ] No character vanishes >15 chapters without reason
- [ ] Every magic system use follows established rules
- [ ] All promises made in Act I are paid off by act III
- [ ] Character knowledge states match information availability
- [ ] Dialogue sounds consistently like each character
- [ ] Timeline has no contradictory dates or ages
