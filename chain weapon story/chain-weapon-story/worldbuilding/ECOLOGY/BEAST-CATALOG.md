# Global Beast Catalog — Cross‑Cultural Index

Purpose

This catalog gathers notable folklore creatures by culture, with quick mechanical stats (Flux affinity, typical habitat, danger level) and story hooks. Use as a lookup when drafting encounters.

Format: Name — Short desc — Flux affinity (Low/Medium/High) — Habitat — Danger — Hooks

Island Fortress
- Yofitsu (Tide‑Foxes) — Illusory fox spirits — High — Coastal vents/rockpools — Medium — Illusion hooks, tide vents
- Kappalings (Water‑hollows) — Water imps that twist currents — Medium‑High — River mouths and tidal caves — Medium‑High — Tether snaps, flood traps
- Tengra (Wind‑Ravens) — Wind‑blade aerial predators — Medium — Mountain thermals — High — Wind blades, cliff ambushes
- Shore‑Wardens — Animated kelp constructs — Low‑Medium — Shipwrecks/ruins — Medium — LIP damage, supply chain threats

Heartland
- Canton Wardens (Ledger Golems) — Vault guardians gone hungry — Low‑Medium — Archive basins & vaults — High — Crystal consumption, void pockets
- Ledger Wyrms — Burrowing relic‑eaters — Medium — Drainage lines, archive subsurface — High — Legal/plot erasure, tunnel ambushes
- Archivist Moths — Animate script into phantasms — Low — Libraries, record vaults — Medium — Perjured testimony illusions

Celestial
- Cloud‑Longs — Thunder serpents that ride thermals — High — Mountain cloud terraces — Very High — Storm fields, formation disruption
- Mirror Naiads — Reflective water spirits — Medium — High lakes/ritual basins — Medium — Memory reveals (cost)
- Eye‑Cranes — Watchers that sense weave ripples — Low — Terrace edges, surveillance points — Low — Premonitory plot device

Sahelian
- Sand‑woven Djinns — Dust storm silhouettes — Medium — Deserts and temporary dunes — High — Mirage traps, flux sink
- Ancestral Lions — Communal guardian felids — Low‑Medium — Tribal territories, sacred grasses — High — Territory drama, sacrificial choice
- River‑Keepers — Amphibious protectors of deltas — High — River mouths, estuaries — High — Pollution mutation, tether sap

Andean
- Condor Kings — Crystalline‑feathered condors — High — Breath terraces and passes — Very High — Feather amplification, territorial wrath
- Stone‑Callers — Herding stone‑sheep that seed crystals — Medium — Mountain pastures, terraces — Medium — Route collapse, ritual seeding
- Pachamama Shards — Subterranean living shards — Low‑High — Deep terraces/subterranean hollows — Very High (if broken) — Long term Void Zone risk

Federation
- Clause Wyrms — Debt‑eating serpents — Medium — Contract nodes & vault outskirts — High — Legal erasure, retrieval quest
- Notary Sentinels — Contract enforcers (constructs) — Low — Vault thresholds, court halls — High — Binding traps
- Ledger Moths — Pale insects that animate marginalia — Low‑Medium — Archives, contract vaults — Medium — Testimony illusions

Using the catalog

- Each entry includes a short mechanical tag (Flux affinity) and habitat. For combat, map Flux affinity to Veil thickness adjustments (High = thin‑Veil encounter; costs are +30–80% in Flux Pockets).
- For glued narrative effect, combine cultural folklore with environmental mechanics: a Heartland vault raid can't just be about guards—the leakage attracts Ledger Wyrms over months.
