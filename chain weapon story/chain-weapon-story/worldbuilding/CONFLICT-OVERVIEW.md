## Conflict Types in Chain Weapon Story

### 1. Personal Conflict
- **Aisen vs. his own limitations** (ordinary protagonist in extraordinary world)
- **Character relationships** (trust earned/broken)
- **Individual ambitions** (career, love, meaning)

### 2. Institutional Conflict
- **Aisen vs. Covenant system** (structure of power)
- **Academy politics** (internal hierarchies and corruption)
- **Network building** (resistance infrastructure)

### 3. Systemic Conflict
- **Heresy of Information system** (institutional control mechanism)
- **Nation-state warfare** (Shukei vs. others)
- **Power structure rigidity** (why systems don't change easily)

### 4. Ideological Conflict
- **"Worth is choices" philosophy** vs. **"Destiny determines all"**
- **Individual agency** vs. **Institutional efficiency**
- **Transparency** vs. **Information control**
