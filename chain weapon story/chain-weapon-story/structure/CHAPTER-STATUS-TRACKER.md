## STATUS DEFINITIONS

| Status | Definition | % Range | When to Use |
|--------|-----------|---------|------------|
| **Draft** | Chapter written with prose; may need expansion or revision | 20-100% | First draft exists with real content |
| **Revision** | Chapter meets word target; prose refined; pacing checked | 90%+ | Draft complete; edits in progress |
| **Final** | Chapter polished; ready for publication | 100% | No further changes needed |

### Priority Definitions

| Priority | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **EXPAND** | Chapter is short or thin — needs additional scenes, dialogue, or description |
| **REVISION** | Chapter has sufficient content but needs prose polish, pacing fixes, or continuity checks |
