- Anchor character: Aisen (notes the oddity) / Amara (hears market rumor).

- Primary seed: **Memory‑metal jam** observed during training (sand/grit gets into a chain link; link seizes).
- Plant: Aisen's chain seizes, an instructor mutters about "imperfections." Aisen files the mechanical failure mentally.

Ch. 6 — "Tarovin Candle Demo / Academy Visit" (use `tarovin_candle_demo.md`)

Ch. 7 — "Rin & Aisen Crossing" (`010-rin-and-aisen-crossing.md`)

## Act II (Investigation & Reveals)

Ch. 14 — "Market Ledger" (scene in market)
- Action: Amara trades for a smuggled ledger; strange line items and redactions show unusual shipments.

## Planting Guide (practical)
- Plant one discreet clue per conspiracy in Act I (ledger stub, branded cord, missing-apprentice notice).
- Use Act II to interrogate clues: one market/ledger beat + one physical inspection beat per conspiracy.
- Make each reveal personal—link it to a named NPC or a character's moral choice for emotional payoff.
- Stagger reveals across middle chapters to avoid an info-dump.

### Act III Part A — Political Climaxes (Ch 45–52B)

Ch. 45 — "First Contact"
- Payoff: **Beast-Kin Alliance (Arc E ignites)**. Kaelen's nature magic enables communication with beast-kin civilization. Ecological crisis has non-human victims.
- Beat: Discovery → communication → the beast-kin ask: "Why should we trust you?"

Ch. 45B — "The Language of Roots" (Kaelen POV)
- Deepening: Kaelen serves as diplomat. Beast-kin "language" is ecological. He learns more in one conversation than years of research.
- Anchor: Beast-kin elder's question remains unanswered.

Ch. 47 — "The Merchant's War"
- Payoff: **Arc D climax**. Full economic warfare: Covenant blockade vs. shadow economy. Trade council votes; the losing side defects to the Covenant. Economic warfare becomes shooting warfare.
- Beat: Thrace makes case for resistance; Licensed Merchants argue compliance. The vote splits.

Ch. 48 — "The Moderate's Offer"
- Payoff: **Arc A climax**. Moderate Council offers managed magical access. Aisen convenes full network. Arguments split three ways (Amara: take it; Lysandra: reject; Kael: too late). Aisen rejects → three allies leave.
- Beat: The Lie fractures — understanding ISN'T producing consensus.

Ch. 48B — "The Crown's Gambit" (Elara POV)
- Payoff: **Elara midpoint / Arc C**. Royal compromise: Crown pardons for cessation of military operations. It WORKS temporarily. The Lie validated.
- Seed: Covenant accepted because it buys time (not genuine).

Ch. 50 — "The Ghost's Name"
- Payoff: **Mole revealed**. Aisen and Rin converge on the leak. Rin knew for months, said nothing. The confrontation is moral: information kills; withheld information also kills.
- Beat: Rin begins sharing — terrifying transition from hidden to known.

Ch. 51 — "The Trial of Synthesis"
- Payoff: **Arc A resolution**. Public demonstration of "forbidden" synthesis. Academy forced to respond. Master Wei's legal framework creates space. Heresy framework cracks.

### Act III Part B-1 — Network Crisis (Ch 53–60)

Ch. 53 — "Varro's Gambit"
- Reveal: Varro's timeline knowledge exposed — in his timeline, Aisen's synthesis revolution caused magical proliferation → uncontrolled Flux Tears. Varro's not wrong about the DANGER.
- Beat: Varro's faction within Covenant exposed as destabilizing.

Ch. 55 — "The Network Bleeds"
- Payoff: **Aisen's Lie collapses**. Three nodes fall in one week. People he recruited personally are dead. Analysis predicted this — couldn't prevent it.
- Beat: Understanding is not enough. The Lie dies.

Ch. 55B — "Kael's Collapse" (Kael POV)

Ch. 56 — "The Covenant Schism"

Ch. 56B — "The Blueprint of Destruction" (Vex POV)
- Payoff: **Vex crisis**. Both sides weaponize her knowledge. Must choose which buildings she helps DESTROY.
- Beat: Observation IS participation. She must choose explicitly.

Ch. 58 — "The Provincial Atrocity"

Ch. 58B — "The Document That Burns" (Amara POV)
- Payoff: **Amara's Lie shatters**. Her own reports were weaponized to identify resistance contacts. The institution didn't ignore her work — it used it as targeting documents.

Ch. 59 — "The Alliance Negotiation"
- Payoff: **Arc E resolution**. Formal treaty between resistance and beast-kin. Beast-kin demand: territory, end to deforestation. Lysandra objects. Kaelen advocates. Alliance forms — barely.
