%! TEX program = xelatex
% possibletemplate.tex — example using cwbook.cls and the Chain-Weapon roster
\documentclass{cwbook}

% Document configuration
\SetTotalChapters{52}
\SetAnchorPOV{Aisen}
% default environment for this chapter
\ENV{dungeon}{wasteland}

\begin{document}

\chapter{Ash and Iron}

% Print the roster for reference
\PrintRoster

% Anchor POV (Aisen)
\UsePOV{Aisen}
\TopLandscape

Aisen moved through the ruined corridor. Every step echoed like a warning. The stone smelled of iron and old vows.

He found Lyra waiting at a broken brazier, her hands steady as if she had never trembled.

\shift

% Caspian (interaction-driven shift)
\UsePOV{Caspian}
\TopLandscape

Caspian watched from the stairwell — a silhouette without a past that did not need one.

He slipped away before any clean answer could form.

\shift

% Ser Corvin
\UsePOV{Corvin}
\TopLandscape

Ser Corvin steadied his spear and kept his oath in the hollow of his chest.

\BottomLandscape

\end{document}
