# Chain-Weapon World Bestiary (Compiled)

This file collects formatted bestiary entries for creatures of the Flux‑Weave ecosystem. Each entry follows the standardized format: Etymology, Physical Description, Behavioral Profile, Flux Abilities, Combat Notes, Story/World Value, Lore Notes, and an art prompt for concept art.

### 1. Name: RESONANCE THRUSH

**Etymology Breakdown**: *Resonance* (vibrating in harmony) + *Thrush* (small songbird) → A creature that sings, and everything sings with it

**Size Category**: Small (25cm wingspan)
**Threat Level**: Novice
**Primary Flux Law**: Information & Sound (bends information‑carrying waves)

**Physical Description** (Entomology)
- Height/Length: 25cm wingspan, 8cm body length
- Body Structure: Crystalline feather‑structure; each feather resonates independently at different frequencies
- Distinctive Features: Feathers shimmer with visible sound waves; throat glows when singing
- Vulnerability Points: Head (vocal sacs cluster); resonance feedback loop if song repeated back

**Behavioral Profile** (Zoology)
- Primary Habitat: Forest edges, academy courtyards
- Diet & Feeding Pattern: Insects and sound vibrations; draws energy from ambient magical resonance
- Temperament: Curious; follows interesting sounds
- Social Structure: Pair‑bonding; hunts with mate
- Reproduction: Lays eggs in crystalline nests; 30‑day incubation

**Flux Abilities**
1. Resonant Scream — vibrates nearby objects
   - Cost: 5 mana/sec
   - Range: 20m radius
   - Interaction: Breaks concentration; disrupts Information spells
2. Echo Projection — creates bouncing echoes that strike multiple times
   - Cost: 10 mana per bounce
   - Range: 3–5 echoes

**Combat Notes**
- Optimal Counter: Gravity‑Masters or Chaos‑Weavers
- Ineffective: Stealth/silence fails; invisibility does not hide sound
- Weakness: Vulnerable to silence spells or chaotic noise

**Story/World/Economy Value**
- Dungeon Placement: Academy courtyard / lower floors
- Drops: Resonance Feathers (sound crafting)
- Environmental Impact: Marker of healthy magical resonance

**Lore Notes**
- Populations increased near thinned Veil; sometimes companion animals for mages

**Art Prompt:** small crystalline songbird perched on an academy balustrade, feathers refracting light as visible sound‑waves ripple outward; warm courtyard background; painterly, high detail, soft rim lighting.
