## POWER & ENERGY SYSTEMS

### I. MANA POOLS & GENERATION

**Master Reference:** [reference/magic-system/POWER-SYSTEMS-COMPREHENSIVE.md](reference/magic-system/POWER-SYSTEMS-COMPREHENSIVE.md)  

#### Standard Pool
- **Capacity:** 120 MU (typical mage at 17 years old)
- **Generation:** ~2 MU/hour meditation, ~0.5 MU/hour passive
- **Fatigue Cost:** Additional 10-20% pool consumption when exhausted
- **Reference:** [POWER-SYSTEMS-COMPREHENSIVE.md - Pool Management](reference/magic-system/POWER-SYSTEMS-COMPREHENSIVE.md#pool-management)

#### Regeneration Rates
- **Combat:** +1 MU/5 sec (concentration-based)
- **Rest:** +5-10 MU/minute
- **Deep Meditation:** +15-20 MU/minute
- **Chart:** [TELEKINESIS-NUMERIC.md - Regeneration](reference/magic-system/TELEKINESIS-NUMERIC.md#regeneration-rates)

#### Cost Factors
- **Environmental:** Flux zones cost 20-50% more
- **Emotions:** Fear/anger increases costs 10-25%
- **Distance:** Range penalties increase costs 5-30%
- **Precision:** Fine control > raw power (counterintuitive cost model)
