Summary:
During winter travels with Harald, Aisen and Harald shelter in a forest village where Aisen meets Kaelen, a local boy who tends a secret grove of winter flowers. Aisen helps gather the flowers and learns that magic is sometimes embedded in the land itself.

Key details:
- Kaelen is an orphaned child who cares for a secret grove of faintly glowing plants.
- He has dirt under his fingernails and smells of earth and flowers; he teaches Aisen to notice small signs of life.
- The flowers that Kaelen tends will be relevant later when they reappear in Aisen's journey and Kaelen eventually reaches the Academy.

Notes:
- Play up the sensory contrast—cold winter air against the unexpected warmth of the glowing flowers.
