# Technological Innovations & Artifacts

## Magical Items & Artifacts

### Tier 1: Common (Accessible; Lower-tier characters)
- **Light stones**: Glow steadily; portable illumination
- **Preservation vessels**: Keep food fresh longer (magical stasis)
- **Healing salves**: Accelerate natural healing (herbalism + magic)
- **Reinforced materials**: Tools that don't break easily (weapons, farming implements)

### Tier 2: Rare (Academy-level; Trained practitioners)
- **Scrying mirrors**: Distant viewing (limited range; unclear images)
- **Communication crystals**: Telepathic contact (paired sets; limited distance)
- **Protective amulets**: Ward against specific magics (personalized to user)
- **Weapons of power**: Enchanted to hold magical charge longer
- **Maps that update**: Magical charts showing real-time positions (navigation)

### Tier 3: Restricted (Military/Elite; Heavily guarded)
- **Portal devices**: Temporary access tunnels between distant points (rare; unpredictable)
- **Binding artifacts**: Magical restraint items (containing spirits/beings)
- **Sensing networks**: Detect activity across large areas (surveillance)
- **Amplification focus**: Multiply magical power output
- **State artifacts**: Nation-specific items representing authority (crowns, scepters with power)

## Transportation & Infrastructure

### Standard (All Nations)
- **By foot**: Primary; roads vary in quality
- **Horse/animal transport**: Expensive; not universal
- **Water routes**: Primary infrastructure; heavily taxed
- **Caravans**: Organized trade; relatively safe
- **Merchant ships**: Coastal + river commerce

### Experimental/Restricted
- **Magical flight**: Rare; theoretically possible; considered dangerous
- **Teleportation**: Portal devices exist but unpredictable
- **Rapid messaging**: Crystal networks or magical courier methods
- **Thought-speed travel**: Theorized; not implemented?

## Covenant Possible Advantages

If Covenant has technological edge (assumption):
- **Teleportation network**: Instant communication/coordination across nations
- **Portal system**: Rapid troop deployment (bypassing geography)
- **Magical amplification**: Allowing fewer mages to accomplish more
- **Information network**: Scrying coordination (perfect intelligence)
- **Binding/control devices**: Slavery or magical compulsion of followers

**Story consequence**: Resistance cannot win through military superiority; must find strategic alternatives (cutting communication, destroying hardware, recruiting defectors).
