# Celestial Kingdom — Geography & Territory

## Core Geography

### The High Plateau
The Kingdom occupies an elevated plateau enclosed by mountain ranges on three sides and a vast glacial lake system to the south. Altitude: 3,000–6,000m. The thin air and extreme conditions define every aspect of life.

| Zone | Altitude | Character | Primary Use |
|------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Lake Rim** | 3,000–3,500m | Relatively hospitable; warmer microclimate near lakes | Agriculture, largest settlements, trade stations |
| **Monastery Belt** | 3,500–4,500m | Moderate; optimal for meditation practice | Monastery-cities, governance centers, spiritual academies |
| **Summit Zone** | 4,500–6,000m | Extreme cold, thin air, Veil-thin zones | Hermitages, crystal mining, vision quests, military observation |

### Key Settlements

**Bodhi Dzong** (Capital-Monastery, ~4,200m)
- Fortress-monastery built into cliff face; 800+ years old
- Population: ~15,000 (monastic + lay community)
- Seat of the Triune Council; largest library of meditation texts in the world
- Architecture: white-washed walls, golden roofs, prayer corridors connecting 200+ meditation chambers

**Mirror Lake City** (Southern Trade Hub, ~3,100m)
- Built on stilts along the shore of the Great Mirror Lake
- Population: ~12,000; most cosmopolitan Celestial settlement
- Southern pass access; primary contact point with outside world
- Rin Celestara's likely point of departure when sent to the Academy

**Crystal Peak Hermitage** (~5,800m)
- Highest permanent habitation; ~200 residents
- Aerolith mining and thin-Veil vision practice
- Air so thin non-adapted humans cannot function beyond 24 hours
- Produces the finest aerolith crystals used in meditation and magical practice

### The Six Passes
Six mountain passes connect the plateau to surrounding territories. Natural military chokepoints.

| Pass | Direction | Status | Notes |
|------|-----------|--------|-------|
| Southern Gate | South to Mirror Lake | Open year-round | Primary trade route; most guarded |
| Western Crossing | West to Valdimere frontier | Open 8 months | Tension point; Valdimere military patrols nearby |
| Northern Thread | North to Andean Alliance | Open 7 months | Spiritual exchange route; lightly used |
| Eastern Descent | East to lowlands | Open 9 months | Rin's intelligence network communication route |
| Cloud Pass | Northeast | Open 5 months | Dangerous; avalanche-prone; emergency military route |
| Pilgrim's Way | Southeast | Open 6 months | Sacred route; no military traffic allowed by tradition |

### Flux Geology

**Aeroliths**: Crystalline Flux deposits unique to high-altitude thin-Veil zones. Properties:
- Amplify meditation effects; extend awareness duration
- Store impressions (magical/emotional recordings) for centuries
- Fragile; shatter under impact; fragments retain partial functionality
- Only form above 4,500m in specific geological conditions
- **Strategic resource**: The Covenant wants aerolith access; main leverage point

**Thin-Veil Zones**: Above 5,000m, the boundary between physical and magical reality becomes permeable. Effects:
- Enhanced magical sensitivity; reduced control
- Spontaneous vision events (disturbing for non-meditators)
- Weather manipulation becomes easy but unpredictable
- Creatures from the magical ecosystem appear more frequently
- **Military advantage**: Non-adapted invaders experience disorientation, fear, hallucinations
