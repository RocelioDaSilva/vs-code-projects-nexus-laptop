# Shukei — Economy, Politics, Religion, Conflicts & Notable Figures (Consolidated)

## Economics & Trade
**Agricultural**: Rice-based subsistence; taxes extracted as rice quota  
**Fishing**: Primary protein source; valued cultural commodity  
**Maritime Trade**: Controlled through licensed ports; wealth accumulates in trading cities  
**Taxation**: Percentage of production to daimyo; varies by region  
**Currency**: Symbol more than substance; barter common  
**Wealth Distribution**: Concentrated in merchant cities; rural poverty endemic  
**Crisis Approach**: Isolationist response (minimize external dependency)

---

## Politics & Power Structure
**Authority Hierarchy**: Shogun → Daimyo → Samurai → Commoners  
**Decision-Making**: Councils of senior samurai under daimyo authority  
**Inter-daimyo Relations**: Constant low-level tension; occasional major conflicts  
**Imperial Court**: Distant center; significant internal autonomy  
**Factions**:
- **Militarists** (aggressive expansion toward other daimyo territories)
- **Traditionalists** (absolute preservation of methods)
- **Reformers** (rare; quickly suppressed)
- **Isolationists** (resist any foreign engagement)

**Political Vulnerability**: Shogun assassination/replacement cascades through entire system; no succession mechanisms create power vacuums Covenant could exploit.

---

## Religion & Philosophy
**Core Religion**: Ancestor veneration + nature spirituality (Shinto parallel)  
**Sacred Concepts**: Ritual purity, harmony with natural forces, honor/shame  
**Institutional Religion**: State temples enforce loyalty; priests report to daimyo  
**Heretical Practices**: Forbidden shamanism, foreign religions suppressed  
**Philosophical Education**: Limited to samurai; emphasizes loyalty and warrior virtue  
**Religious Authority**: Priests hold power equal to military commanders in some regions  

**Key Value**: *Honor* - More important than life itself; dishonor = suicide obligation  
**Key Fear**: *Impurity* (spiritual contamination; magical corruption very threatening)

---

## Conflicts & Crises
1. **Ecological Crisis** (Flux corruption) — Islands particularly vulnerable; unknown waters may carry threats
2. **Inter-daimyo Tensions** — Increasing conflicts; military spending escalating
3. **Hidden Internal Rebellion** — Ronin, outcasts, merchants secretly organizing
4. **Isolationist Crisis** — External events forcing engagement whether accepted or not
5. **Religious/Philosophical Questions** — Young people questioning old answers

**Covenant Approach in Shukei**: Likely infiltrates through ronin networks or merchant connections. Uses insularity against them (information suppression). Exacerbates daimyo conflicts (warfare = destabilization).
