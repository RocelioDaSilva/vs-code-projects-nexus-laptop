# Andean Alliance — Notable Figures

## Current Leadership

### Qhapaq Rimaq (Alliance Speaker)
- **Name**: Inti Wayra
- **Age**: 67
- **Band**: Qucha (Mid Band)
- **Role**: Elected Speaker of the Alliance; 22-year tenure; longest-serving in three generations
- **Personality**: Patient to the point of paralysis; excellent mediator; chronically indecisive on existential matters
- **Crisis**: Health declining; succession question increasingly urgent; refuses to name preferred successor
- **Narrative relevance**: His death or incapacity during the story's events would trigger succession crisis at the worst possible moment

### Yachaq Mama Sisa
- **Age**: 81
- **Band**: Urqu (High Band)
- **Role**: Senior shamanic elder; spiritual authority recognized across all three bands
- **Specialty**: Condor Dreaming — most experienced vision-reader in living memory
- **Personality**: Cryptic, deliberate, occasionally terrifying; speaks in questions rather than statements
- **Position**: Opposes any accommodation with the Covenant; believes their standardized magic will damage the earth-vein network permanently
- **Narrative relevance**: Her visions may provide crucial intelligence about Covenant plans; her death would remove last barrier to modernization faction

### War Commander Tupaq Sinchi
- **Age**: 44
- **Band**: Urqu (High Band)
- **Role**: Commander of Alliance military forces; Warak'a master; trained 800+ sling warriors
- **Personality**: Calm, physical, deeply loyal to the mountain; fights with mathematical precision (resonance with Serath)
- **Position**: Defensive isolationist; will fight any invasion but opposes projecting Alliance power beyond the passes
- **Narrative relevance**: If the Alliance joins the resistance, Tupaq commands the military contribution; his defensive philosophy conflicts with offensive operations

### Administrator K'anchay Rimay
- **Age**: 38
- **Band**: Yunka (Low Band)
- **Role**: Chief Trade Administrator for Yunka Hatun; unofficial leader of the modernization faction
- **Personality**: Charismatic, pragmatic, bilingual (speaks Federation Trade Common); frustrated with Alliance isolationism
- **Position**: Advocates Mercantile Federation trade agreements and selective Covenant engagement
- **Narrative relevance**: Represents the internal force that could split the Alliance during the crisis; either an ally to the resistance (through trade networks) or a vulnerability (through Covenant co-optation)

## Historical Figures

### Apu Willka (The First Speaker)
- Founded the Three-Band Government 400 years ago
- Unified mountain communities against lowland aggression through reciprocal resource treaties
- Established the mit'a labor system and the condor signal network
- Revered as founding ancestor; invoked in political arguments by all factions

### Mama K'uichi (The Earthquake Shaman)
- Performed the Earthquake Step during the Western Invasion 180 years ago
- Killed 3,000 invaders; saved the Alliance; carried the moral weight until her death
- Said to have never spoken again after the event; communicated only through mineral singing
- Cautionary figure: proof that ultimate power requires ultimate cost
