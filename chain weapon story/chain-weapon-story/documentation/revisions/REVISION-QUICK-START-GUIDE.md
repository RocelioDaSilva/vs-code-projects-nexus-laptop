4. **The pacing heat map is gold** — Once you see it visually, pacing fixes become obvious

5. **Dialogue distinctiveness changes everything** — Readers recognize voices much faster than you'd expect

6. **You don't have to fix every issue** — Even improving the top 10-15 makes manuscript significantly stronger
