## 🎓 Key Concepts in These Documents

### Concept 1: Priority Framework
**Critical** (fix first) → **Important** (fix second) → **Polish** (fix last)
- Prevents overwhelm by sequencing
- Ensures high-impact fixes done first
- Allows stopping at any point with improvement

### Concept 2: Heat Map Visualization
**Pacing heat map** shows intensity across all chapters
- Identifies 3+ consecutive high-intensity sections (break them up)
- Identifies large gaps without recovery (add quiet moment)
- Makes pacing fixes data-driven

### Concept 3: Reference Building
Create reference documents while revising:
- Dialogue voice patterns (ensures distinctiveness)
- Sensory anchors (ensures consistency)
- Character arc completion (ensures satisfaction)
- Makes future revisions 2x faster

### Concept 4: Verification Before Polish
**Verify systems consistent** before polishing prose
- Fixing story architecture before prose prevents wasted work
- Can't polish what needs restructuring
- Once verified, polish is faster
