A scene that uses all six techniques:

Aisen knelt in the dead forest. (Jemisin – ecology)
His hands shook from mana exhaustion. (Sanderson – cost)
The Covenant soldier was just a boy, maybe fourteen. (Abercrombie – gray)
"Please," the boy whispered. "They have my mother."
Aisen remembered his own father's face. He could not remember his voice. (Hobb – trauma)
He had a tracking link in his pouch. He could tag the boy… (Rothfuss – planted mystery)
He gave the boy a map and a coin. "Run north."
Three days later, Amara's network reported the boy was hanged. His mother died anyway. (Martin – consequence)
Aisen added two names to the list in his head. He did not sleep. (Hobb – interiority)
