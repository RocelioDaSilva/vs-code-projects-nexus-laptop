Rothfuss uses all five senses (plus balance, temperature, time) to immerse the reader.

Example: Instead of "the tavern was cold," write:

"The Wandering Stag smelled of old ale and older secrets. The fire had died hours ago, but no one had relit it. Aisen's breath misted. He did not mind the cold. It reminded him he was still alive."

Exercise: Rewrite a paragraph of your story using at least three different senses.
