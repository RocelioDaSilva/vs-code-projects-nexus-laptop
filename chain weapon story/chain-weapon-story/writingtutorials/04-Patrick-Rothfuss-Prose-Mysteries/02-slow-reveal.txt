Plant a mystery early, pay it off hundreds of pages later. The reader feels smart for remembering.

Example from your story: The String and the Teleporter.

Early: Aisen sees a teleporter die because a piece of twine was embedded in his arm. He keeps the twine, not knowing why.

Late: Aisen rigs a doorway with fishing line. A teleporter appears and is killed by the line. The reader remembers.

Write a 500-word scene that plants a mystery to be resolved later.
