Welcome to the Author Study Folder.

This folder contains lessons, exercises, and examples drawn from six master fantasy authors. Each subfolder focuses on one author's primary strength.

How to use:
1. Read the "apply-to-your-story" file first.
2. Try the exercise in the same folder.
3. Move to the next author.

After studying all six, open the "Master Synthesis" folder for checklists and combined examples.

Your story already has a strong foundation. These lessons will help you polish it to a professional level.
