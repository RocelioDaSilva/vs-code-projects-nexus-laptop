%    • Orin: hollow core — emptiness, isolation

% ── Aisen: fractured ascent triangle ──
\newcommand{\GlyphAisen}{%
  \begin{tikzpicture}[x=1cm,y=1cm,line cap=round,line join=round]
    \draw[line width=0.7pt] (0,0) -- (1,2) -- (2,0) -- cycle;
    \draw[line width=0.5pt] (1,2) -- (1,0.75);
    \draw[line width=0.5pt] (0.5,0.85) -- (0.85,0.85);
  \end{tikzpicture}}

\lettrine[lines=3]{T}{he wind came down from the Grey Mountains carrying the smell of burnt cedar and something older—something that had no name in the common tongue.} Aelindra tightened the strap of her vambrace and said nothing.

Below, the valley curved like a wound in the earth, its floor dark with the remnants of three villages not a fortnight past. The smoke still rose in thin, patient threads, as though the ground itself had not yet decided whether to forgive.

\SceneBreak

She had spent seventeen years learning the difference between decisions made in rooms and decisions that lived in the soil afterward. She had yet to find a word for the distance between them. The boy behind her shifted weight. The boy said many true things. That was what made him dangerous to keep near and impossible to send away.

``We should not have come this way,'' he said.

``The path chooses itself,'' she replied.
