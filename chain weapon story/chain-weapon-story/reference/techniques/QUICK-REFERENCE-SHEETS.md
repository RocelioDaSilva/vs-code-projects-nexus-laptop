# Quick Reference Sheets

Purpose: ultra-compact author / scene cheat sheets for quick lookup during drafting.

--

**Aisen — Quick Stats**
- MU pool: 120 MU (baseline).  
- Regen: 2 MU/min idle; 4 MU/min after 10+ minutes focused meditation.  
- Burst reserve: ~30–40 MU for short, intense sequences.  
- Fatigue thresholds: mild ≈60% pool, severe 80–90%, collapse >100% draw.

**Telekinesis — Quick Cheat**
- Point Push: 1 MU per impulse.  
- Thread Probe / Boundary Sense: 0.5–1 MU probe; 0.2 MU continuous for active thread.  
- Toggle (5-link segment): ≈4 MU.  
- Tether: create 3 MU; maintain 0.4 MU/min.  
- Trajectory Curving: 3–6 MU per object.  
- Torque Twist: 2–6 MU depending on mass/leverage.  
- Sustained Float: 4 MU to hold ~5 kg (≈+0.8 MU per additional 5 kg).

Use tiers: Tier 1 (1–5 MU), Tier 2 (5–15 MU), Tier 3 (15–40 MU).

**Chain Spear — Quick Cheat**
- Typical configuration: core shaft + detachable segments grouped into 5-link modules.  
- Telekinetic toggle maps cleanly to module boundaries (fastest control).  
- Typical toggle cost: 4 MU per 5-link module (Aisen baseline).  
- Maintenance: clean pivots, realign collars, and check LIP (Link Integrity Points) after high-speed snaps or heavy impacts.  
- LIP note: high‑snap whip motions accelerate LIP wear; repeated abuse requires replacement parts from Forlorn Engineers.

**Combat MU Tiers (shorthand)**
- Low: ≤20 MU — quick tricks, single toggles, short tethers.  
- Medium: 20–60 MU — multi-toggle sequences, short sustained holds, area manipulations.  
- High: 60+ MU — long holds, heavy lifts, sacrificial sequences.

**Common Sequence Costs (cheat)**
- Quick alley reaction (3 toggles + tether + 3 pushes + 1 min maintain): ~18–22 MU.  
- Duel sequence (2 toggles + momentum nudge + torque + curve + tether assist): ~20–26 MU.  
- Area denial / sustained defense (multiple tethers + holds + repeated pushes): 35–50+ MU (sustained over minutes).

**Scene Anchors**
- Frostfang Incident: Age 18 — academy expedition ambushed (major trauma + acclaim).  
- Final Border‑Stand: Age 30 — signature sacrificial defense (canon end point; tune mortality outcome).  
- Exam Sabotage / Conclave: mid‑timeline political arc (see `ACADEMY-POLITICS.md`).

**Environmental Shortcuts**
- Flux Pocket: MU costs +30–80% (precision failure).  
- Memory‑Bloom Wetlands: short-term weave templates available; risk of mind‑fog.  
- Silt Rot: corrodes memory‑metal; increases LIP loss during exposure.

**Author Quick Rules**
- Keep MU bookkeeping narrative-simple: use Low/Medium/High tags in first draft, then add explicit MU values for technical scenes.  
- Use flux pockets and ecological hazards to force improvisation rather than to remove agency.  
- Show LIP wear visually (loose pivot, faint shimmer on link edges) before manufacturing failures.
