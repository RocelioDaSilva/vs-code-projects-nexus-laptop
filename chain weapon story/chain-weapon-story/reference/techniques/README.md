## Combat Sequences by Scenario

### Sequence 1: Alley Ambush
- **Context:** Close quarters, two attackers, reactive
- **Cost:** ~21.4 MU (18% of pool)
- **Duration:** Immediate + 1 min maintenance
- **Fatigue:** Mild
- **See:** [COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md](COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md#sequence-1--alley-ambush-close-quarters-reaction)

### Sequence 2: Open Field Duel
- **Context:** One-on-one, measured, tactical
- **Cost:** 23 MU immediate
- **Duration:** Tactical rhythm
- **Fatigue:** Low-moderate
- **See:** [COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md](COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md#sequence-2--open-field-duel-momentum-exchange--feint)

### Sequence 3: Sustained Defense
- **Context:** Last stand, area denial, high cost
- **Cost:** ~43.4 MU over 2 minutes (36% of pool)
- **Duration:** 2-3 minutes
- **Fatigue:** Severe (neural impact)
- **See:** [COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md](COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md#sequence-3--sustained-defense-area-denial-high-cost)

### Sequence 4: Urban Suppression
- **Context:** Civilian-heavy, non-lethal, crowd control
- **Cost:** ~34 MU immediate + 0.8 MU/min maintenance
- **Duration:** Extended
- **Fatigue:** Moderate
- **See:** [COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md](COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md#sequence-4--urban-suppression-non-lethal-area-control)

### Sequence 5: Cavalry Disruptor
- **Context:** Fast-moving targets, avoid lethal, specialized
- **Cost:** ~17 MU immediately
- **Duration:** Variable
- **Fatigue:** Low (brief action)
- **See:** [COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md](COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md#sequence-5--cavalry-disruptor-anti-mount-tactics)

### Sequence 6: Zipline Rescue
- **Context:** Casualty extraction, sustained effort
- **Cost:** ~12-16 MU per trip + maintenance
- **Duration:** Multiple trips (6-10 min typical)
- **Fatigue:** Accumulative (recovery between trips)
- **See:** [COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md](COMBAT-CHOREOGRAPHY-MASTER.md#sequence-6--zipline-rescue--casualty-extraction)

## Tuning & Authoring Notes

**For different difficulty levels:**
- Easy: lower MU costs, reduce fatigue
- Normal: use listed values
- Hard: increase MU costs by 20-30%, increase fatigue

**For different opponent types:**
- Single skilled opponent: use Sequence 2
- Multiple weak opponents: use Sequence 1 or 4
- Overwhelming force: use Sequence 3
- Mounted/mobile: use Sequence 5

**For emotional beats:**
- Underdog moment: Sequence 5 (limited resources)
- Desperate last stand: Sequence 3 (visual spectacle)
- Clever victory: Sequence 2 (misdirection)
- Sacrifice: Sequence 6 (sustained cost)
