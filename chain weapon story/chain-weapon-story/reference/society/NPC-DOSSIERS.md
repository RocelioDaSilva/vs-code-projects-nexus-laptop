# NPC DOSSIERS — Key Characters

This file contains expanded, usable dossiers for major characters in the Chain Spear world. Each profile includes physical description, personality, motivations, key relationships, fighting style, narrative beats, and hooks for scenes.

---

## Sergeant Harald
- **Role:** Mentor, city guard sergeant, spear trainer, father‑figure to Aisen.
- **Age:** Late 40s.
- **Physical:** Broad shoulders, left ear nicked, deep lines from smoke and cold; missing two toes on right foot (old wound) so his gait is slightly asymmetrical. Carries an old thrusting spear and a short ceremonial badge of service.
- **Personality:** Stoic, dry humor, blunt. Protective but not sentimental. Expects competence and pays back kindness in loyalty.
- **Philosophy:** “Discipline buys minutes.” He trains recruits to value routine and formation practice.
- **Fighting style:** Formation spearwork, bracing, simple but effective anti‑charge modules. Uses a short shield in close quarters; excels at reading formation flaws.
- **Connection to Aisen:** First mentor; taught him to read formations and to value small, repeatable practices. Wrote Aisen’s letter of recommendation to the Common Stave.
- **Arc & Beats:** Recruits Aisen; defends him politically at the academy; ages into a quieter role; present at Aisen’s bedside. Missing scene: Harald’s private confession to Aisen about why he pushed for academy entry.

## Master Wei
- **Role:** Celestial Hall scholar, physics/magic hybrid, Aisen's academic protector.
- **Age:** 60s.
- **Physical:** Thin, spectacles, long white hair in a tidy knot; robes ink‑stained. Small resonance meter always tucked in a sleeve.
- **Personality:** Curious, disciplined, quietly proud of the scientific method. Has patience for craft and distaste for political theater.
- **Philosophy:** “Weave and Flux are not enemies; they are data.” He treats magic like empirical study.
- **Fighting style:** Not a frontline warrior; uses precise geomantic wards and alchemical devices. Prefers traps and research to fight.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Recognized Aisen’s physics intuition; saved him during the Frostfang inquiry by producing a dated note corroborating Aisen’s warnings. Keeps records of Aisen’s chain techniques in the Celestial archive.
- **Arc & Beats:** Mentor figure who ensures Aisen’s legacy receives academic notation; later formalizes Aisen’s methods into a Hall syllabus. Missing scene: Master Wei discovering the memory‑metal resonance property and testing it with Aisen’s chain.

## Mira (Aisen's sister)
- **Role:** Sister, tavern manager, emotional anchor.
- **Age:** ~6 years younger than Aisen.
- **Physical:** Quick hands, warm smile, practical clothes with flour dust; hair braided for work.
- **Personality:** Socially gifted, practical, firm but affectionate. Runs the family business with competence.
- **Philosophy:** Community matters more than glory. Prefers small stability over risky heroics.
- **Fighting style:** Not a fighter; knows basic farm pole defense and how to handle the tavern staff in a crisis.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Motivates him; keeps family afloat; last person he sees before the final campaign.
- **Arc & Beats:** Grows into tavern owner and community leader; keeps Aisen's ledger and chain fragment at the bar. Missing scene: Mira’s decision to refuse the Heartland wreath and keep the broken chain as a memorial.

## Amara
- **Role:** Sahelian friend, information broker, griot‑trained oral historian.
- **Age:** Early 30s.
- **Physical:** Dark skin, braided hair with beads carrying regional tokens; traveling satchel full of notes and mnemonic cords.
- **Personality:** Wry, relentless listener, networker. She keeps secrets but trades them wisely.
- **Philosophy:** Information is currency and responsibility; memory is preservation.
- **Fighting style:** Lightweight, uses darts, smoke, and distraction; prefers nonlethal capture when possible.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Saved his reputation with oral testimony; recorded Aisen’s exploits in griot form; coordinated caravan routes and informants for Aisen.
- **Arc & Beats:** Grows into a diplomatic channel between the Academy and Sahelian trade nodes; preserves Aisen’s oral record. Missing scene: Amara confronting a merchant who trafficked fourth‑party relics.

## Pieter van der Berg
- **Role:** Federation merchant’s son, financier contact, pragmatic ally.
- **Age:** Mid‑20s.
- **Physical:** Slight, quick fingers; always smelling faintly of salt and ink; wears a narrow coat with hidden pockets.
- **Personality:** Cool, numbers‑minded, socially polished but not cruel.
- **Philosophy:** Commerce stabilizes war; contracts are trusts to uphold.
- **Fighting style:** Not a fighter; understands logistics and naval positioning, and calls in ship crews for river tasks.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Lends small funds, helps manage merchant loan repayment, helps Aisen with logistics and resources on river missions.
- **Arc & Beats:** Becomes a friend who adjusts his mercantile risk profile to support commoner causes; missing scene: Pieter negotiating an insurance payout for Aisen’s chain replacement.

## Tupac
- **Role:** Andean highlander ally, rope expert, spiritual pragmatist.
- **Age:** Late 30s.
- **Physical:** Stocky, weathered skin, rope scars on palms; carries a woven sling and a coil of sturdy rope.
- **Personality:** Quiet, philosophical, communal. Believes in ayni — reciprocal work.
- **Philosophy:** People survive by holding each other up; bridges are literal and social.
- **Fighting style:** Sling master, expert in vertical terrain and team maneuvers.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Teaches Aisen rope work and mountain tactics; later helps in zipline rescues.
- **Arc & Beats:** Lives long enough to be a tribal elder; missing scene: Tupac teaching a village how to quickly rig a rescue line.

## Ren (samurai's daughter / ronin’s kin)
- **Role:** Island Fortress affiliate, precision fighter, begrudging friend.
- **Age:** Early 20s.
- **Physical:** Athletic, small, scar along jaw; dresses simply when off duty.
- **Personality:** Honor‑minded but pragmatic; frustrated by rigid class expectations.
- **Philosophy:** Skill matters more than birth; honor is what you do, not who praises you.
- **Fighting style:** Fast draw techniques, short sword precision, footwork that controls maai.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Respectful of his craft; they swap lessons—her precision for his improvisation.
- **Arc & Beats:** Breaks with clan expectation to act on skill/merit; missing scene: a duel where she learns to work with Aisen’s chain rather than oppose it.

## Edrin Halvar
- **Role:** Heartland heir, youthful antagonist (Frostfang expedition casualty), symbolic driver of political fallout.
- **Age:** Late teens.
- **Physical:** Pampered, pale, ornate crest on cloak.
- **Personality:** Arrogant, entitled, impulsive.
- **Philosophy:** Glory and speed; tests limits by ignoring caution.
- **Fighting style:** Rudimentary cavalry and parade techniques; lacks real survival instincts.
- **Connection to Aisen:** His death on the Frostfang expedition precipitates the political scandal that almost ruins Aisen.
- **Arc & Beats:** Serves as a catalyst rather than a developed arc; missing scene: private moment revealing his insecurity before the expedition.

## Heartland General (Aldric Varre — tentative name)
- **Role:** Political antagonist; Heartland military authority, father of Edrin.
- **Age:** 50s.
- **Physical:** Tall, trimmed grey beard, scar across brow; carries a general’s baton.
- **Personality:** Calculating, proud, slow to admit mistakes.
- **Philosophy:** Stability through order; expansion is national destiny.
- **Fighting style:** Not a fighter personally; uses strategy and bureaucratic leverage.
- **Connection to Aisen:** His son’s death drives his demand for scapegoats and influences academy politics; key driver of Heartland ambition.
- **Arc & Beats:** Exploits Frostfang fallout to press for Academy militarization; missing scene: his private order to investigators to find a culprit quickly.

## Caspian Vane
- **Role:** Shadow‑scholar and occasional patron agent; rumored to traffic in experimental pulse memory techniques.
- **Age:** ~38.
- **Physical:** Pale, narrow‑faced, wears a dark travelling coat with hidden pockets for reagents; always a faint frost of condensation at his collar in cold weather.
- **Personality:** Charismatic, urbane, quietly ruthless; more scientist than soldier, but comfortable with field work when necessary.
- **Fighting style:** Prefers avoidant movement and surgical use of short blades; sometimes employs small pulse traps when cornered.
- **Connection to Aisen:** An early interlocutor of Master Wei; he funds obscure research chairs and shows interest in Aisen’s chain experiments for reasons that may be political.
- **Arc & Beats:** Moves between patronage and clandestine objectives; a reveal of his patronage network helps explain some sabotage patterns. Missing scene: Caspian meeting a Patron House representative in secret.

## Seraphine Moroz
- **Role:** Head of Weavecraft—rival to Mistress Rava; pragmatic experimentalist.
- **Age:** Mid‑40s.
- **Physical:** Heavy‑lidded, grease‑stained hands; tends to wear protective goggles pushed up on her brow.
- **Personality:** Blunt, efficient, sharply moral in her own way; she distrusts theatrical donors and prefers reproducible results.
- **Fighting style:** Not a frontline fighter; specializes in defensive weave nodes and emergency dampeners.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Tested several of Aisen’s early chain prototypes; later forced to testify during political hearings. Missing scene: Seraphine calibrating a prototype crystal with Aisen present.

## Ser Corvin Ashford
- **Role:** Knight‑investigator attached to Heartland interests; leads early inquiry after Frostfang.
- **Age:** Early 30s.
- **Physical:** Broad, scarred jaw, stiff armor with family crest; slow, deliberate bearing.
- **Personality:** Formal, blunt, sincere in his own honor code; hates uncertainty and seeks clear culpability.
- **Fighting style:** Heavy spear and shield; excels at ordered tactics and close defense.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Often placed at odds with Aisen; seeks a scapegoat after Frostfang to satisfy his patron's grief. Missing scene: Corvin confronting Aisen after the expedition.

## Princess Elara Voss
- **Role:** Patron and public face for a major house; politically adept and careful.
- **Age:** Early 30s.
- **Physical:** Ornate but practical dress, light scar at temple (old injury), voice practiced for public address.
- **Personality:** Diplomatic, publicly warm, privately calculating; understands theater and leverage.
- **Fighting style:** None; her influence is political and financial.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Funds a scholarship program that affects Academy admissions; her public statements can make or break a Council vote. Missing scene: Elara offering conditional funding at a public ceremony.

## Lysandra (Forlorn Captain)
- **Role:** Leader of the Forlorn Engineers (scavenger guild); master repairer of damaged linkwork.
- **Age:** Late 30s.
- **Physical:** Soot‑blackened forearms, braided hair stuffed into a kerchief, utility belt full of improvised tools.
- **Personality:** Practical, wary, fiercely protective of her crew; will trade favors but hates dishonor among craftsmen.
- **Fighting style:** Guerrilla traps, improvised explosives, and clever mechanical snares rather than direct combat.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Repairs his prototypes once and later is framed during a cache theft; their bargain shapes a rescue/raid scene. Missing scene: Lysandra negotiating a quiet repair for a prototype blade.

---

## Lucius Varro
- **Role:** Patron House steward—merchant interest with a hawkish bent on returns and influence.
- **Age:** Mid‑50s.
- **Physical:** Trim, impeccably dressed, with the pin of his house always visible; quick, watchful eyes.
- **Personality:** Calculating, outwardly affable, privately intolerant of uncertainty; believes in leverage and returns.
- **Fighting style:** Desk power, contracts, and patron networks. Not a fighter.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Pressures the Council and funds Practical Arms projects; interested in fieldable tech rather than theoretical purity. Missing scene: Lucius bargaining with the Provost for test field priority.

---

## Kaelen Thornwood
- **Role:** Ecologist and Hall of the Common Stave representative; voice for environmental and ethical mitigation.
- **Age:** Early 40s.
- **Physical:** Mud‑stained boots, weathered hands, small herb satchel; a strong, plain face.
- **Personality:** Thoughtful, steady, morally driven; insists that resource extraction has consequences.
- **Fighting style:** Uses terrain and traps; prefers avoidance and remediation to violence.
- **Connection to Aisen:** Allies on matters of village defense and pulse‑memory wetland ethics; argues against reckless extraction. Missing scene: Kaelen confronting researchers over a proposed memory‑bloom harvest.
