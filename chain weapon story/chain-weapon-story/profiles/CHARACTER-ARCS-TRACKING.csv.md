## KEY CONSEQUENCE CHAINS

### Chain 1: Mentorship Ripple
Harald teaches Aisen → Aisen teaches network → Network survives Aisen's death → Post-war society teaches synthesis
- Result: One mentor's commitment becomes generational standard

### Chain 2: Privilege Weaponized  
Elara uses royal access → Feeds intelligence to resistance → Enables kingdom's involvement → Post-war leadership positioned
- Result: Privilege becomes tool for democratization not control

### Chain 3: Documentation as Resistance
Amara records Aisen's work → Stories survive Aisen's death → Post-war society builds on documented principles
- Result: Information preservation = power preservation

### Chain 4: Synthesis as Revolutionary
Aisen + Kaelen synthesize magic → Multiple traditions validated → Threatens all four nations' authority → Post-war legitimacy through pluralism
- Result: Understanding = institutional change possibility

### Chain 5: Sacrifice as Teaching
Harald dies prophetically → Aisen accepts death → Network continues → Principle of "act despite uncertainty" becomes cultural value
- Result: Mentor's death validates student's final choice
