## Part 1: BACKGROUND (Origins & Formation)

### Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Perse
- **Aliases/Titles**: Corpse Saint
- **Birth**: Unknown
- **Family**: Unknown
- **Upbringing**: Originally trained strictly as a healer with little to no combat capabilities. Her early life was dedicated to preserving life rather than taking it.
- **Pre-story history**: Practiced traditional healing magic, focusing on mending wounds and restoring stamina.

### Physical Description
- **Age appearance**: Ranges from late teens to mid-twenties.
- **Build & distinctive features**: Initially soft and unobtrusive; later becomes scarred yet constantly regenerating, creating a highly erratic physical state. Her constant bodily destruction and recreation leave her with an unnatural aura.
- **Style/clothing**: Practical healer's robes that eventually become stained and adapted for high-mobility, bloody magical combat. 
- **Distinguishing marks**: Constantly shifting scars or fresh tissue from regenerating her own body parts.

### Psychological Foundation
- **Core Drive**: Survival and protecting her remaining allies, pushing her magic beyond conventional ethical limits.
- **Core Fear**: Being helpless again, like she was when she was just a traditional healer.
- **Core Value**: Endurance. If she can survive the damage, she can win.
- **Unresolved wound**: The trauma of losing friends (like Aisen) driving her to embrace ruthless methods.

## Part 2: ACT I — Childhood/Formation (Chapters 1-12ish)

### Character State at Act I Start
- **Position**: Support healer, non-combatant.
- **Beliefs**: Magic is meant to restore and heal; combat is left to warriors.
- **Capabilities**: Healing magic, stamina restoration.
- **Limitations**: Severe lack of combat knowledge or offensive capability.

### Act I Arc: Emotional Journey
**[Passive Healer] → [Exposure to Brutality] → [Realization of Inadequacy]**

1. **Innocence/Formation** 
   - Acts strictly as a field medic.
   
2. **Learning/Growth** 
   - Witnesses the horrors of the world and realizes traditional healing is not enough to stop the source of the injuries.
   
3. **Integration/Preparation** 
   - Begins experimenting with the limits of her healing magic on her own body.

## Part 3: ACT II — Academy/Conspiracy (Chapters 13-38ish)

### Character State at Act II Start
- **Position**: Developing combat mage.
- **Capabilities**: Enhanced stamina from constant low-level self-healing.

### Act II Arc: Emotional Journey
**[Experimentation] → [Breaking Limits] → [Becoming the Weapon]**

1. **Pushing boundaries**
   - Abuses her magical regeneration and enhanced stamina.
   
2. **Developing the "Corpse Saint" style**
   - Learns to break and regenerate her body, turning bones and blood into deadly projectiles and weapons.
   
3. **Malignant Magic**
   - Discovers how to weaponize cell growth—essentially giving targets aggressive cancer during battles to destroy them from the inside.

---

## Part 4: ACT III — War/Loss/Climax (Chapters 39-52+)

### Character State at Act III Start
- **Position**: Feared combatant known as the "Corpse Saint."
- **Capabilities**: Can use her body almost like a corpse, weaponizing regeneration, cell manipulation (cancer), and biological projectiles.

### Act III Arc: Emotional Journey
**[Grief] → [Vengeance] → [Climax]**

1. **Aisen's Funeral & The Ambush**
   - Following Aisen's death, she attends his funeral.
   - When Caspian (now revealed as a traitor/vampire) observes the funeral, Perse, alongside Ryo, launches a brutal, coordinated ambush against him. 
   - Her relentless combat style forces Caspian to use Seraphim's powers to dematerialize, costing him his human heart.
   
2. **War against the Covenant/Void**
   - Acts as a ruthless vanguard, an embodiment of the consequences of the war.
