## 📝 How to Add New Cross-References

When creating new content, add cross-references in these locations:

1. **In your new file**: Add "See also" sections with links to related content
2. **In hub files**: Update the relevant mapping table (character index, structure map, etc.)
3. **In related files**: Add reciprocal links back to your new content
4. **In this document**: Consider adding new rows/sections for interconnected content

**Format for new references:**
- Use markdown links: `[Display Text](path/to/file.md)` or `[Display Text](path/to/file.md#section)`
- Be specific about section references with anchors
- Group related links with brief descriptions
- Update date modified in the linked file's frontmatter
