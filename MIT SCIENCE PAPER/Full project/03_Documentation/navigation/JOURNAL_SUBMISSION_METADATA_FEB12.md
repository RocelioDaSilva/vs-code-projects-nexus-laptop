## 📧 COVER LETTER TEMPLATE (IF REQUIRED)

```
Subject: Manuscript Submission - GEESP-Angola

Dear Editor,

We submit our manuscript titled "GEESP-Angola: Geospatial Energy Site 
Selection for Community Solar Systems Using Multicriteria Analysis" for 
consideration in Energy Policy.

NOVELTY & IMPACT:
This work introduces an innovative geospatial-MCDA framework (GEESP) for 
optimizing solar mini-grid siting in sub-Saharan rural contexts, addressing 
a critical gap in evidence-based energy planning. We demonstrate application 
to Huíla Province, Angola, identifying 3 priority zones benefiting 95,000 
people with estimated LCOE USD 0.18-0.22/kWh.

RELEVANCE:
The methodology aligns with global energy access targets (SDG 7) and 
addresses rural electrification challenges in emerging economies, with 
direct replicability across African contexts.

ORIGINALITY:
This is the first integrated AHP-GIS framework published for Angola's 
renewable energy sector, supported by field validation protocols and 
comprehensive sensitivity analysis (42 scenarios tested).

URGENCY:
Findings directly support government planning (2023-2027 Energy Plan) with 
immediate application to USD 12M phase 1 implementation commencing Feb 2026.

The manuscript has not been previously published and is not under 
consideration elsewhere.
```
