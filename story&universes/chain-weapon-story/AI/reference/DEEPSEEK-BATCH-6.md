## Key Anomalies (summary)
- Prodigy: rapid mastery but fragile fundamentals and ego.
- Lucky Encounter Haver: repeatedly benefits from improbable coincidences.
- Noble Blood: inherited status, gear, and entitlement.
- Chosen One / Prophecy Bearer: narrative protection and factional attention.
- (Implicitly referenced earlier) Regressors, Transmigrators, Reincarnators.

## Core Principles (How to Fight Anomalies)
- Identification: watch for behavioral patterns (overreliance on rituals, predictable tactics, cultural blindspots).
- Deny the advantage: remove the environmental or informational factor that enables the anomaly.
- Use asymmetric tactics: bait expected behavior, then break the script; exploit isolation or dependency.
- Political solutions: sow doubt, manipulate reputations, expose reliance on support networks.

## Tactical & Political Methods (condensed)
- Against Regressors
  - Tactical: change predictable variables; feint then break script.
  - Political: disrupt symbolic events; feed false intelligence.
  - Example: Lucius Varro expects Frostfang to play out—protagonist creates new variables.

- Against Transmigrators
  - Tactical: exploit unfamiliar local physics; use non‑systemic attacks.
  - Political: feed false "modern" knowledge; socially isolate.

- Against Reincarnators
  - Tactical: modern guerrilla and tech tactics; bait with nostalgia.
  - Political: expose anachronistic claims; trap with artifacts.

- Against Prodigies
  - Tactical: extend the engagement; neutralize specialty; attack fundamentals.
  - Political: isolate mentors and patrons; public humiliation to fracture fragile ego.

- Against Lucky Encounter Havers
  - Tactical: controlled environments; force skill tests; set false opportunities.
  - Political: deny access to random encounters; surveillance of apparent luck sources.

- Against Noble Blood
  - Tactical: fight outside comfort zones; target gear and retinues.
  - Political: leverage rival families, debt, and scandal.

- Against Chosen Ones
  - Tactical: avoid direct confrontation; attack allies/logistics.
  - Political: manipulate prophecy; isolate followers; reinterpret destiny.
