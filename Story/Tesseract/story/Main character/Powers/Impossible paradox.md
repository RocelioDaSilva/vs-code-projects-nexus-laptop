In the cosmology of your story *Tesseract*, the **Impossible Paradox** is not merely a superpower. It is a foundational, reality-breaking protocol—a brute-force attack on causality itself. It is the most dangerous and psychologically devastating tool in C-43's arsenal.

Here is a comprehensive breakdown of its nature, mechanics, and narrative purpose.

### Core Definition: The God-Mode Gambit
The **Impossible Paradox** is a targeted temporal recursion field. When activated, it allows the user to explore near-infinite branching possibilities from a single starting point ("safe state") to achieve a predefined goal. It is not omniscience or omnipotence; it is **infinite trial and error**, experienced in real-time by the user.

### Key Mechanics & Rules

1.  **The Goal is the Law:**
    *   **Activation Requirement:** You must declare a goal *before* activation (e.g., "Defeat X without casualties," "Retrieve the artifact," "Escape this room").
    *   **The Vague is Vast:** The vaguer the goal ("Survive"), the more possibilities must be exhausted, leading to exponentially more attempts and psychological strain.
    *   **The Only Off-Switch:** The Paradox **only deactivates when the goal is met**. You cannot voluntarily exit it. You are trapped in the loop until a version of you satisfies the conditions.

2.  **The Loop of Safe States:**
    *   You can set one or multiple "safe states"—checkpoints in the timeline, which can be the moment of activation or any point you manually designate during a loop.
    *   If you die or fail, your consciousness snaps back to the last safe state with full memory of the failed attempt.
    *   The loop continues, attempting a new branching path each time.

3.  **The Cost & Limitations:**
    *   **Coin Burn:** It requires a massive, continuous expenditure of **Coins** (fragments of your Anchor) to maintain the recursive reality check and data backup for each loop.
    *   **Power Lockout:** While active, you cannot use your other powers (Multiplication, Telekinesis, etc.). Your entire being is dedicated to fueling the Paradox.
    *   **No New Tools:** It does not grant you new abilities or knowledge you don't already possess. It only gives you more *chances* to apply what you have in different combinations and sequences.

### The Hidden Catch: The Utilitarian Horror
This is the true core of the power's narrative weight. **The Impossible Paradox is a perfectly logical, amoral machine.**

*   It will find the ***most efficient*** path to your goal, not the *best* or *most moral* one.
*   **Example:** Your goal is "Save the city from the monster." The Paradox might determine, after 10,000 loops, that the most efficient solution is to collapse a building on the monster, killing 10 civilians inside. It **saves the city**. The goal is met. The loop ends. You are now the person who made that call, remembering all 9,999 failures and the one horrific success.
*   This forces the user into an unbearable position: to achieve their noble goal, they must willingly accept and execute the Paradox's coldest, most ruthless calculation. This is why it "breaks" people.

### The Visual Signature: The Green Hue
*   Upon successful deactivation, the "correct" timeline is marked by a faint, lingering **green hue**—a permanent data scar on reality signifying where the temporal recursion was resolved and collapsed into a single causal line.
*   This is a crucial story element: it's a visible record that an Impossible Paradox was used here, a tell-tale sign for other dimensional beings that something unnatural was forced into coherence.

### Narrative Purpose & Why It Fits C-43

1.  **The Metaphor Made Power:** C-43 is an experiment with a hazy goal. The Impossible Paradox is the literal manifestation of his existence: **trapped in a loop, trying endless variations of action to achieve an objective he didn't choose, with the cost being pieces of his very self (Coins).**
2.  **The Tesseract Connection:** A tesseract is a 4D shape showing all states of a 3D object. The Impossible Paradox forces C-43 to **live out a tesseract of his own potential actions** to find the single viable 3D timeline. He experiences his own fourth-dimensional possibility space.
3.  **The Ultimate Character Test:** This power is the crucible for C-43's morality and sanity. Will he use it and become a monster of cold logic? Or will he refuse, embracing failure to retain his humanity? His relationship with this power *defines* him.
4.  **Anchor Damage:** The immense Coin cost means each use brings him closer to **Anchor corruption** and the **Forgotten Shores**. It is the power of self-annihilation disguised as omnipotence.

### In-Universe Summary:
The **Impossible Paradox** is a Class-5 Reality Anomaly, often called "The God-Engine" or "The Torturer's Gift." It is a protocol that allows a dimensional entity to perform a localized, recursive brute-force attack on the probability matrix of a timeline to achieve a defined objective, at the cost of existential currency and profound psychological fragmentation. Its use is considered a hallmark of desperation or profound hubris among veteran travelers.

**In essence, it is the power to guarantee any outcome, at the price of your sanity, your morality, and the very data that constitutes your soul.** It is the perfect, tragic power for an experimental being trying to find the "correct" version of himself in an infinite omniverse.
