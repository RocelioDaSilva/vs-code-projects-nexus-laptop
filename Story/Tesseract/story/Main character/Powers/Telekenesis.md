In the context of your story and its unique sci-fi mechanics, **Telekinesis** is not just moving objects with the mind. It is a fundamental, low-level data manipulation power that carries both great potential and significant risk.

Here’s a breakdown of what Telekinesis is in the world of *Tesseract*:

### Core Definition: Manipulation of Information
In your universe, where everything is composed of **Information** (the data that defines reality), telekinesis is the ability to directly read and rewrite the spatial and relational data of objects within a localized field.

*   **It's not "psychic force."** It is more like **applying a physics engine command** to a target. You are altering the coordinates (`position_z: +2`) or the relationship (`is_touching: false`) in an object's data profile.
*   **The Power Source:** The energy to make this change doesn't come from you biologically. As you noted, "powers don't come out of nowhere." The energy is drawn from the local dimension's "rule set" or is paid for with your **Coins** (fragments of your Anchor) to temporarily overwrite local physics.

### Mechanics & Application: A Programmer's Power
This view of telekinesis makes it incredibly versatile but difficult to master.
*   **Basic Use (The "Lift"):** The most common application. You rewrite an object's "relationship with gravitational force" data, making it neutral or negative, causing it to rise.
*   **Advanced Use (Pressure Manipulation):** This is where the high skill ceiling lies. By precisely manipulating the data of air molecules—increasing their density, velocity, and collision force in a specific point—you can create **heat** and **fire**. By doing the opposite, slowing and spreading them out, you can create **cold** and **ice**. This is what you referred to as "telemobaric changes."
*   **Precision vs. Power:** It allows for surgical precision (e.g., stopping a single bullet, manipulating the hinges of a lock) but can be scaled to brute force (crushing a car, creating a telekinetic shield).

### The Risk: Data Corruption and Unintended Consequences
This is the critical drawback that makes it a "beginner's power" that many advanced travelers abandon.
*   **You Are Changing Reality's Code:** Every time you use it, you are editing the live data of the universe. The universe's **checking system** constantly runs to ensure its data is consistent. A clumsy or large-scale telekinetic edit can trigger an alert or cause a cascade error.
*   **The Domino Effect:** If you telekinetically "pull" a support beam to you, you are not just moving the beam. You are altering the stress data of the entire structure, the air pressure data around it, etc. If these changes don't "check out" with the rest of the universe's physics, it can lead to localized **data corruption**—reality glitching, objects phasing, or worse, the corruption feeding back into **your own Anchor**.
*   **Tied to Local Forces:** Your power's effectiveness and side-effects are dependent on the local dimension's physical laws. In a high-gravity universe, lifting a rock costs more. In a fluid-based universe, the "pressure manipulation" to create fire might be impossible. You have to "debug" your power use for every new reality.

### Why It Fits C-43 and Your Story
1.  **Thematic Resonance:** Telekinesis is the power of **direct, tangible manipulation**, mirroring how C-43 is himself being manipulated by external guidelines and how he must manipulate his own data (Anchor) to survive.
2.  **A Power of Potential and Peril:** Like C-43's entire existence, it is powerful but inherently unstable. It represents the risky, hands-on struggle to control one's environment, in contrast to the god-like but morally damning abstraction of the **Impossible Paradox**.
3.  **The "Code" Metaphor:** It is the most literal manifestation of your story's core premise: that reality is software. A telekinetic is a live-code editor, working without a safety net.

**In summary, in your story, Telekinesis is the direct, granular, and risk-laden manipulation of an object's spatial and energetic data within the universe's information matrix. It is a fundamental tool that reflects the dangerous, code-like nature of reality itself, perfect for a protagonist who is a piece of experimental data searching for his place in the system.**
