*   **Anchor & Data-Self:** C-43's core is his **Anchor**—a compact data construct containing all the information that defines him. This allows him to travel dimensions by becoming a fourth-dimensional data pattern and inserting himself into a three-dimensional reality.

He is not just a 3D character in a moment; his entire journey—all the paradox timelines, all the dimensional jumps, his unknown origin and his sought-after end—forms the complete 4D shape of his identity. The reader, like C-43, pieces together this shape slice by slice.
