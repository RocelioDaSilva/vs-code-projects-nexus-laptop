How to Capture:
1. Launch 2XKO Training Mode
2. Select Vi and opponent
3. Practice the move
4. Press PrintScreen at the moment it activates
5. Crop to show Vi and the move effect clearly
6. Save as PNG (recommended) or JPG

Tips:
- Capture from neutral position for consistency
- Show the move startup/active frames
- Include opponent for scale reference
- Good lighting (avoid dark/unclear screenshots)
- 1280×720px minimum resolution

See ../IMAGES_GUIDE.md for detailed specifications.
